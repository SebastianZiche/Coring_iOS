App Description
2U.Chat is more than a messaging and social media app – it is a lifestyle for one billion users across the world. Chat, make translate call, one-to-one chat, group chat, voice chat, video chat with friends, share your life to Memories, and much more.
Why do we use 2U.Chat?
• TRANSLATE CALLS: Supports real-time intelligent translation calls between 20+ languages.
• MULTIMEDIA MESSAGES: Supports sending video, images, text, and voice messages.
• GROUP CHATS AND CALLS: Create group chats with up to 1000 people and real-time video chats with up to 9 people.
• VOICE AND VIDEO CHATS: Provides high-quality voice and video calls to anywhere in the world.
• STICKER GALLERY: Browse thousands of fun, animated stickers to help express yourself in chats, including stickers with your favorite cartoon and movie characters.
• MEMORIES: Never forget your favorite memories. Post photos, videos, and more to share with others on your personal Memories stream.
• FIND NEW FRIENDS: Find and add new friends through "Shake" and sharing "Contact".
• REAL-TIME LOCATION: Not good at explaining directions? Share your real-time location with the tap of a button.
• LANGUAGE SUPPORT: Localized in 20 different languages and can translate friends' messages and Moments posts.
• MORE FUNCTIONS: Support Interpreter, Assets, News, etc.
• EASY MODE: Clearer and larger text and buttons for better readability.
