App Description
Diary Book is a lovely diary app that can record mood and keep a diary.

Accumulate your feelings day by day, record your daily life, and keep good memories in Mood Diary.

[Our Features]

Mood Calendar: super cute little expression to record your daily mood;

Hand Book Cover: create your own lovely hand book and collect your own life's hand book.

Graphic Stickers: rich and lovely stickers, graphic collage materials, make your hand account more fresh and beautiful anytime and anywhere.

Calendar: convenient for querying calendar dates.

Graphic Diary: write a diary for the next day and add pictures.

Dynamic theme: multiple home page dynamic theme styles to decorate your page.

[Our Expectations]

Writing morning records every morning, summarizing and reflecting every night can bring a sense of ritual to our daily life. When we open the hand account records in emotions, this whole record is "our own history".

I hope you can look back on the past and record everything beautiful through Emotion.
