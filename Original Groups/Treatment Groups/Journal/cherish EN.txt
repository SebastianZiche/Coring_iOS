App Promotional Text
An app for you who need time to look into your mind!
Take time to get to know yourself with Cherish :)
App Description
Cherish offers these features!
# One sentence you want to remember
Write down a message to yourself. Simply reading it every day will give you a lot of strength.

# Diary with music
Keep a diary while listening to the calm background music. It will help you record your thoughts.

# Write about yourself using 3 different diary formats
The diary presents a diary guide for strangers. Choose the format of the diary you want to write today and fill it out.
- Look at your emotions
- Keep a diary with any subject you want
- Take question recommendations
