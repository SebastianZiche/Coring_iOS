App Description
Notejoy is a fast and focused notes app for individuals and teams. It's the simplest way to capture your ideas and share your work with others.

AVAILABLE EVERYWHERE YOU NEED IT
Notejoy syncs in real-time and is also available on Web, Mac, Windows, and iOS. It even integrates with G Suite, Microsoft Office, Zoom, Slack, and Trello.

SHARE IDEAS QUICKLY
Sharing in Notejoy is as fast as sending an email. No more worrying about permissions, sending docs over email, or lost document links.

COLLABORATE IN REAL-TIME
From coworkers to external clients, Notejoy is built from the ground up for collaboration. See when people have viewed your notes, give and get light-weight reactions, @mention and discuss.

LEVERAGE YOUR TEAM'S KNOWLEDGE
Searching in Notejoy is a pleasure. Find what you need and easily stay in the know on key projects and people.

Join hundreds of thousands of individuals and teams using Notejoy for client management, project management, content writing, meeting notes, product roadmaps, goal setting, and more.
