App Description
MiroNote
Is a fast and stress-free note-taking application for recording thoughts and life,
You can use hierarchical tags to label and index the recorded content, eliminating the trouble of classification during recording.
You can store all data locally or in your icloud drive, miro note will support more types of storage space such as google drive, to ensure data privacy and no loss.
If you are a Notion user, you can integrate it into the Notion workflow by setting data synchronization with notion. The rapid recording capability of miro note is a supplement to Notion.
