App Description
Translator app is a language translator app that allows you to communicate effectively world wide. Translator app is a powerful voice translator and dictionary app which translates in over a 100+ languages

You can use the app to:

Translate Audio
Translate picture
Translate camara
Translate text

Furthermore, It’s available in offline Mode to save your few bucks while you are on roaming

Get instant translations between 100+ different languages for example

spanish to english translator
english to korean translator
arabic to english translator
chinese to english translator
german to english translator
french to english translator
english to polish translator

Features:

• Image translator, photo translator, voice translator

• Available in male or female voices.

• Includes synonyms and meanings.

• Phrasebook - With over 250 predefined phrases.

- Use your camera for visual translator or dictionary

- See and hear the pronunciation of the words

- Simply point and translate foreign text to English

• Apple Watch App

• Transliteration, Sharing, Favorites, History, and much more.

* The history of your translations will be saved on your device

* Voice settings: choose male or female voice and its speed

app provides the most up-to-date translations.
