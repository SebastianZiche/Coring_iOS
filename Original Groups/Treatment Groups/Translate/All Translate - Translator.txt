App Description
All Translate is a translation APP for your iPhone. You can get translations from 100+ languages to study, work, and live with ease, without any language barriers.
FEATURE：
Supports 100+ commonly used languages, all language apps you can think of are included.
Multiple translation modes, easy to deal with various scenarios, including text, voice, and OCR translation.
Clear and beautiful interface, let you get started easily.

Just enter the text you want to translate, select the corresponding language, click the translate button, and you can get accurate translation results right away.
If your hands are temporarily unavailable, try a simpler voice translation, directly wake up the microphone and say what you want to translate, All Translate can also voice output the answer you need.
Are you at a loss for documents full of foreign languages? Take a photo and upload it to All Translate, and the translated text will be presented immediately.
