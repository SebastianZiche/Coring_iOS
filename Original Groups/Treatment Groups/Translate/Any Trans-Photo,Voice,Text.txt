App Description
It's a powerful app for everyday life. The translation function can easily help you translate various languages ​​in your life.Are you afraid that you will not be able to understand other languages and communicate when traveling abroad?
Fear not, we have a great language translator for you: Any Trans

Any Trans is an app designed to improve your travel experience or daily language switching needs. Even if you know the language of some countries/regions, there may be situations where you don’t know how to express it in another language, don’t worry, Translate Now will help you: After choosing the target language, all you need to do is take a picture/use The language description, it will help you convert the language into the language content you need, and the waiting time is only a few seconds.

Discover more:
- Image translation:
Take a photo or select a photo from your camera roll, recognize the object and translate it into any language you want.
-Voice Mode:
Enter a voice, we will analyze the content and convert it into a familiar language and display the text.
- Text mode:
Enter text content and translate it into any language you want.

Wallpapers and widgets can make your phone look cool. Scan the QR code, let you deal with the ubiquitous QR code in your life.
