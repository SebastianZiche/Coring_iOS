App Description
All translator is an efficient, accurate and powerful multi language translation & dictionary application. It supports translation in more than 100 languages, easily real-time translation of text. Photo translation, voice translation and dialogue translation make your communication more convenient, it is an intimate "translation partner" for your life, work, study and travel.

【All Translator main functions covered】：
1、Photo translation（Latest features）
Mobile Photo translation, photo album translation, menu, books, road signs… you can take photos at will and get translation results in time.
2、Real-time dialogue translation
Translate dialogue content in real time, realize bilingual dialogue, and easily solve language barriers such as cross-border meetings, friends' appointments, travel questions, etc., which makes your communication convenient and unimpeded.
3、Multilingual text translation
Enter text content, you can translate for free to get high-quality translation.
4、Intelligent voice translation
Click to input the voice and translate the voice content immediately.


【Subscription(VIP) terms】：
All translator offers three different subscription cycle options. You can choose to start your subscription with a 3-day free trial or subscribe directly：
1、Weekly subscription: $1.99 per week , renew the subscription once a week
2、Weekly subscription: $1.99 per month , 3 day free trail, renew the subscription once a week
• The price in different regions will be adjusted according to the local actual situation and tax policy, please take the actual price as the standard.
• Your free trial subscription will automatically renew to a paid subscription.
• 24 hours before the subscription service expires, the system will automatically renew and deduct the corresponding fee according to the subscription type.
• You can cancel the free trial or subscription at any time by turning off automatic renewal through the iTunes account settings. This operation must be completed 24 hours before the end of the free trial or subscription period to avoid charges. Cancellation will take effect after the last day of the current subscription period, and you will be downgraded to a free service.
• Once you cancel your subscription, you will no longer be able to enjoy the content and functions of the subscription member.
• Non subscription members can still use the app normally.
