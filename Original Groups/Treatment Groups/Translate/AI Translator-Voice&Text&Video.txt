App Description
AI Translator - Your Bridge Across Languages

Introducing AI Translator - the all-in-one translation app that empowers you to:
Translate voice: Speak in one language and hear the translated response in another, in real-time. Perfect for conversations, interviews, and presentations.
Translate text: Type or paste any text and get instant, accurate translations in over 100 languages. Great for emails, messages, and documents.
Translate video: Record videos and watch them with subtitles in your chosen language. Unlock a world of global content.
AI Translator is more than just a translation app. It's your key to:

Connect with people from all over the world. Build relationships, collaborate on projects, and experience new cultures without language limitations.
Expand your knowledge and horizons. Access information and entertainment in any language, from news and educational resources to movies and music.
Break down barriers and promote understanding. AI Translator can help create a more inclusive and connected world.
With AI Translator, you're not just translating words, you're translating possibilities.

Of course, it also comes with a recording function to meet your daily study and work needs:

Recordings: Users can easily record voices and sounds.

Player: Play your recordings.

Recording Studio: This studio helps users edit their recordings. You can cut, merge, remove, insert, denoise your audio clips.

Download AI Translator today and start breaking down language barriers!
