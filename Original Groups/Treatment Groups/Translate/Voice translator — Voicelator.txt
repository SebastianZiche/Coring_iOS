App Description
We designed an amazing app interface for you to translate on the go. Everything you need is just at your fingertips. Voicelator is accessible any time you browse the internet or chat in messengers. Download now to enjoy all the cool features Voicelator has to offer.

Why Voicelator?

-Supports voice and text translations for 100+ languages to communicate freely
-Compatible with Apple Watch
-Translate texts offline
-Provides an instant translation in other apps
-Helps you discover the world around with its cool Object recognition & translation feature.
-Translates to and from all popular languages in any app that uses a keyboard (WhatsApp, Twitter)
-Detects language automatically
-Contains a phrasebook
-Translates texts in images by just pointing the camera or importing photos from your phone library
-Has voice settings: choose male or female voice and its speed
-High-quality translation
-Saves your translations in Favorites which you can view any time
-Share your voice and text translation by email, messages, SMS, social media
