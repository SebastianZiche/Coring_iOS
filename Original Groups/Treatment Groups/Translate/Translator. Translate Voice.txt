App Promotional Text

App Description
The translation is now quicker and easier than ever! The translator is the top-performing translator app due to its ability to quickly and accurately translate text, audio, and photographs! Whether you’re in a loud and crowded restaurant or need quick help navigating public transportation in a new place, Translator is ready to come to your rescue. By offering both real-time audio translation, photo translation, and quick audio conversation aid, you will never feel challenged by a language barrier again.

The Translator App features

• Over 100 languages with the ability to translate from voice to voice, text to voice, text to text, voice to text, or camera to text. This makes sure that you’re protected in all situations including email, conversation, all audio, and written text.
• Easily navigate and switch between different dialects as you travel
• Offline version of the app for instances without internet connection

Gone are the days of sifting through a translation dictionary to navigate a new language. While the app is perfect for real-time situations, it is also a great learning tool for those who are looking to develop both their written and verbal language skills. The Translator is your new pocket translator to help you easily navigate any and all new situations with ease.
