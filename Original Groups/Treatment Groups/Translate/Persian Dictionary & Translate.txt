App Description
Persians dictionary is an offline Persian to English and English to Persian dictionary and translator made for the people to learn English and Persian. The app is complete huge repository of thousands of words, where you can search your desired word and its meaning in English and in Persian from English.

The app is designed in a way where you can easily input your desired word through voice, and it will search the word and will show you the meaning. The same case in translation, user can search any word and can translate from English to Persian and Persian to English.

The app also have correct text pronunciations.

Follow are the feature of the app

English to Persian Dictionary - find a huge repository of Persian words, find English words meaning in Persian

Persian to English dictionary – Persian to English words meaning, find meaning of your desired Persian word in English

English to Persian translator – Translate English to Persian,
Persian to English translator – translate Persian to English
Multi-Language Translator – Users can translate text into 100+ languages that allow users to communicate effectively with people worldwide.
The app have huge repository – you can now find your words from a huge repository

Easy to search words and find meaning of the words – you can easily search you desired word to translate and find meaning

Daily notification – daily and weekly notification
Recent words – will save all your searched words

Install app, and start learning English
