App Description
Fast Translator was developed to be easy to use, quickly solve your quest and it supports many interface languages, that enables you to speak confidently a new language. It is an indispensable tool in your device!

Use your device as a translator between many languages, so you can break the ice in your conversations everywhere with anyone, no matter what language do they speak, you can use it while traveling, on holiday, at home... or when you are practicing a new language.

Some of the app’s features are:
- Free language translator.
- Text Translation over 50 languages.
- Intuitive, quick and easy to use.
- Instant translations.
- Copying and pasting text to the clipboard just by tapping a button.
