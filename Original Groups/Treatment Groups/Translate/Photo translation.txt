App Description
Photo Translator is a multi-mode translation software that can achieve photo translation, manual text input translation, and real-time voice translation.With a simple and elegant interface, fast and efficient operation, it is a must-have translation software for your work and study!

Application features:

Take a full-screen photo for translation, select a photo for translation, translate it instantly, and translate without manual input, which is efficient and accurate.

English to Mandarin, accurate voice recognition, fast translation into Chinese, English, and other foreign language text content, voice reading.

[Language Recognition] Supports multiple languages, covers multiple countries, and meets the needs of learning translation, work translation, overseas translation, travel translation, and more.

Manually enter words and sentences that you cannot read, and you can quickly translate them. It supports multiple languages and supports copying and translating text, making it convenient and fast.

Professional photo translation and voice translation software, supporting the translation of several international languages, is a powerful tool for English and foreign language learning and travel.
