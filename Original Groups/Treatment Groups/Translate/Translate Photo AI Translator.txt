App Description
Translate everywhere. Travel to any country and communicate with everyone around you. Voice translation is real-time and easy. Translator • Translate Voice will instantly translate and pronounce phrases from people you communicate with. Read and translate menus and signs with instant camera and photo translation.

Get Translator • Translate Voice now and stop spending time looking for phrases in the dictionary! Say any phrase and your digital voice translator will translate and speak translation back to you!

Translator • Translator Voice key features:
• Real-time speech recognition and translation
• Voice recognition with end of speech detection
• Translation keyboard extension for messengers and other apps
• Translation with share extension
• Male and female realistic voice generation
• Photo translation
• Variable voice speed of generated speech
• Translation in 114 languages
• Translate photo from your library
• Real-time voice translator

Translator • Translator Voice Pro features:
• Unlimited voice translation
• Instant camera and photo translation
• 100% Ads free

Premium access:
With upgrade to Translator • Translator Voice Premium you will get unlimited full access to all features.
