App Description
Download Magic Translator now and translate text, voice and pictures in real-time.
In today's information explosion, we are beginning to come into contact with more and more information in other regional languages. Whether it is in life or at work, we must know quickly and accurately what the information means. Importantly, Magic Translator will make language no longer a barrier, allowing you to have more moments of ease.
Key Features:
1. Support in more than 100+ languages.
2. Support voice-to-voice dialogue.
3. Support scan translation in photos.
4. Support copy and share.
Text translation: Just enter the text you want to translate, you can not only get the translation result but also share the translated text.
Voice translation: You need to turn on the microphone permission of the device, just speak to the device, and Magic Translator will automatically identify what you said.
OCR translation: Whether it is taking a real-time photo or selecting a photo from the gallery, advanced optical technology will automatically extract the text content of the image for translation.

You can switch between any of the following languages：
