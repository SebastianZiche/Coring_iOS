App Description
Translate, type & speak in 100+ languages.
Use a camera to translate unknown texts.
Get Translator Guru now!

Are you fond of communication with people from all over the world and tired because of misunderstandings? Translator Guru: Voice & Text covers it all. Transform your phone into a live translation device. Choose a pair of languages and communicate easily anywhere in the world. The language is no longer a barrier!

KEY FEATURES:

• Translations in 100+ languages.
• Translator keyboard for all messengers.
• Voice-to-Voice, voice-to-speech conversations.
• Camera for menus or signs and more translations.
• Dictionaries & useful phrasebook.
• Apple Watch support.
• Personalization: favorites and history.
• Translation reproduction and sharing.
• Translation of sounds of cats and dogs just for fun.

PRO FEATURES:

• Unlimited translations
• Instant camera translation
• 100% Ads Free

Once you download Translator Guru, you will forget about misunderstandings in communication!
