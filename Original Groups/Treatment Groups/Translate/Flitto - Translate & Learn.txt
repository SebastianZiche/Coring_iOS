Free
App Description
Fast and perfect translation based on crowdsourcing and AI.
Translation services with over 10 million users from 173 countries all over the world.
Whenever you feel language barriers during your language study, assignments, or even at work, Flitto is here for you!

◆ Translation services in 25 languages
Korean, English, Chinese (Simplified / Traditional), Spanish, French, Japanese, Arabic, German, Russian, Portuguese, Italian, Indonesian, Vietnamese, Hindi, Thai, Turkish, Tagalog, Dutch, Swedish, Malay, Polish, Czech, Swahili, Finnish

◆ Flitto features ◆

1. Translation
• AI Translation (texts / images / voices)
AI translation, built over the artificial neural networks, offers natural translation results compared to machine translation with a simple click.
• Crowdsourced Translation (texts / images / voices)
Crowdsourced translation service is available only from Flitto! This 'human translation' service, powered by over 10 million users in 173 countries all over the world, will give you translation results in just 3 minutes on average.

2. Proofreading
• Crowdsourced proofreading
Crowdsourced proofreading service in 25 languages between Flitto users.

3. Arcade
'Flitto Arcade', a playground for linguists! Learn foreign languages and earn points at the same time! Try various language quizzes such as multilingual translation, translation review, dictation, and reading.

◆ Are you a linguist? Take on a challenge to become a crowdsourced translator or a proofreader! Pass the level test to begin translating and proofreading and earn points.

▶Optional access authority (You may still use Flitto even if you do not consent to the access authority.)

• Image: You may translate images stored in your device.
• Camera: You may translate images.
• Notification: You will be notified of essential information for using services such as translation notifications.
• Location: You may be given more accurate translations based on user location data.

Version 5.0.2 (Oct 19, 2016)
    New Level System! Coming this October

    1. Each language pair will have its own level.
    For example, level of 'English to Chinese' will be separately managed from level of 'Chinese to English'.

    2. Crowdsourced Translators and 1:1 Pro Translators will be distinctly separated.
    1:1 Pro Translators, who are more able to provide quality translations, will be regarded a higher level to Crowdsourced Translators. Crowdsourced Translator of a given language pair can start from Level 1 and reach all the way up to Level 4. However, the only way to become a 1:1 Pro Translator of any language pair is to pass the Pro translation test. Please note that you do not have to be a Crowdsourced Translator to apply for 1:1 Pro translation test. In fact, if you pass the translation test of a language pair right from the beginning, you will become a 1:1 Pro Translator from that very moment.

    3. One must pass translation tests in order to become a translator in Flitto.
    To participate in Flitto Crowdsourced Translation as a translator, you need to pass the 'multiple-choice test'. To participate in Flitto 1:1 Pro Translation as a 1:1 Pro Translator, you need to pass the 'translation test'.

    4. Level up conditions have been changed.
    Previous conditions of number of translation participated and acceptance rate have been abolished. Instead, accumulated earned points as well as number of recommendation earned for each language pair will be the barometer for setting your level of that language pair.

    Please check out the FAQ for more details.
