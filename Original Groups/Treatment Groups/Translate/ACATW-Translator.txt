App Description
(Limited Free)"ACATW-Enjoy Translate", is a very small and strong translation software,support 72 languages translation & 42 languages HD aloud and also support picture and voice recognition input. The translation results can be saved to the local disk that makes you use them at anytime! No matter which country you travel in, you will not in trouble. This will make the oversea travel easy, colorful, exciting.
Translation support for 72 languages .
Pronunciation support for 42 languages .
Image recognition support for 19 languages .
