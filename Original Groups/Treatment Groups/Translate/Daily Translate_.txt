App Description
Daily Translate is an essential voice and text translator that enables you to efficiently communicate anywhere in the world.

Text Translator：
Enter the words and terms you want to get the translation in text format or listen to the accurate pronunciation.
Voice Translator:
Just speack and give the translator words or sentences, the app will translate it on your screen and read it out.
Camera Translator:
Take photos and scan text from images with your camera. Translate any paper document—menus, signage, anything—easily.
Conversation Translator:
Talk and translates conversations between a foreigner and yourself in real-time.

Pharse Book and Daily Quotes added for better translation experience.
Download this powerful tool to help you communicate well with foreign languages no matter where in the world.
