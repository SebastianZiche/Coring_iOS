App Description
120+ LANGUAGES!
REAL-TIME Voice、Text and Camera translation by AI support!

AI Translator: Voice&Text&Photo is focusing on Improving quality of rendering translated text,voice and Photos. Aim to break language barriers and connect people all over the world, users can effectively communicate with people who speak different languages. Express yourself as if you were a native speaker! Turn your smartphone into your personal multilingual translator!
Our app features advanced AI conversation and text to Photo/Photo to photo technology, ensuring accurate and reliable translations every time. You can use our app offline and enjoy auto language detection for convenience and flexibility.

Key Features:
- Voice Translation: Speak the content for accurate and smooth communication.
- AI Chat advanced functions, efficient photo translation
- Immediate translation of real-time conversations
- Photo Translation: Get menus or signs for quick reference during travels.
- Use your camera for instant text translation
- Translate texts from your photos and pics
- Translate as you type in any of your favorite messaging apps
- Over 120+ Languages Translation for speech-to-speech and text-to-text modes 
- You can either speak or type to translate your text
