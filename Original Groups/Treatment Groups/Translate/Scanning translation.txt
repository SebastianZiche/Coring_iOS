App Description
Fast scan translation of picture / document / card / card and other information, accurate identification of translation, simple operation, small size, one key export / read scan results. Professional OCR text recognition scan translation software!

"Scanning translation features"
[text extraction master] picture translated text, accurate recognition rate 99%+!
The assistant of foreign language learning can recognize 100+ languages, such as Chinese, English, Japanese and Korean.
(Intelligent collation and export) scanning results intelligent segmentation, one key read / export TXT/PDF
Rapid identification of translation pictures / documents /PDF/ ID card / bank card / business card and so on
[Edit] identification results support the fast scan results to make phone calls, send e-mail, access to web sites, see the calendar of events, check the map, check number, machine number Chabang goods etc.

"use of advice"
To make sure that you can get a clear scan, please take a photo:
* enough light to avoid jitter
