App Description
Introducing WhatSays! The ultimate summarizing, translating, and question-answering app for everyone. Got a long letter or document you need to understand? No problem! WhatSays will give you the gist of it all. And that's not all! Our app is packed with cool features designed to make your life easier. Here's what you can expect:

Summarize Anything: Just take a photo or upload a document, and WhatSays will quickly provide you with a short summary. No more struggling through lengthy texts!

Language Master: WhatSays can handle any language, making it perfect for traveling or communicating with friends from around the world. Just snap a pic of a text or document in any language and get a summary in your own language.

Key Points Highlight: Want to know the most important parts of a document? WhatSays will point out the key points for you, so you can focus on what truly matters.

Pay Attention: WhatSays will let you know what to pay special attention to in the content, ensuring you don't miss any crucial information.

Question Time: Got questions about the content? Just ask WhatSays, and it'll answer them for you. It's like having your very own personal assistant!

Give WhatSays a try and make understanding letters, papers, and documents a breeze! Download now and join our amazing community of users. Say goodbye to long reads and language barriers, and say hello to the future of content comprehension with WhatSays!
