App Description
OkTalk Translator is a handy translator for travels, business and study. You will get instant translation to 110 languages by Voice, Text, Camera or Keyboard. This powerful combination will give you an opportunity to feel confident in every corner of the world.

* Voice Translation
Translate conversations and separate phrases by Voice recognition function. Use text translations for words, sentences and long texts.

* Camera Translation
Bring the camera on the object and find its translation to any of language. Camera recognizes as objects as well as text on the objects: menus, signboards, magazines etc.

* AR Translation
Translate the world around you with augmented reality.

* Translator Keyboard
Now you can translate right in your Messenger or email! No need for additional jumping over tabs and apps. Write in your native language and send in any language just clicking one single button on the keyboard!

* Offline Mode
No Internet connection? No problem! Text translations are available in the offline mode.

* Translation Widget
Set up widget on your iPhone. Easy and fast access to the translations without even opening the app.

* Apple Watch
Install Widget in your Apple Watch and get translations even faster and in more comfortable way!

* Phrasebook
Over 2000 most used phrases are included.

* Siri Shortcuts
Add shortcuts to Siri to get your translations even faster.

How can you use OkTalk Translator?
Apply a smart assistance in café, restaurants, shop, pharmacy, taxi and hotel booking during your trips. Use OkTalk as personal facilitator having small talks and asking for direction. Translate boards and signs through camera, get help from locals using simultaneous voice translation.

Here is a list of available languages for translate: Afrikaans, Albanian, Arabic, Armenian, Azerbaijani, Basque, Belarusian, Bengali, Bosnian, Bulgarian, Catalan, Cebuano, Chichewa, Chinese (Simplified), Chinese (Traditional), Croatian, Czech, Danish, Dutch, English, Esperanto, Estonian, Filipino, Finnish, French, Galician, Georgian, German, Greek, Gujarati, Haitian Creole, Hausa, Hebrew, Hindi, Hmong, Hungarian, Icelandic, Igbo, Indonesian, Irish, Italian, Japanese, Javanese, Kannada, Kazakh, Khmer, Korean, Lao, Latin, Latvian, Lithuanian, Macedonian, Malagasy, Malay, Malayalam, Maltese, Maori, Marathi, Mongolian, Myanmar (Burmese), Nepali, Norwegian, Persian, Polish, Portuguese, Punjabi, Romanian, Russian, Serbian, Sesotho, Sinhala, Slovak, Slovenian, Somali, Spanish, Sundanese, Swahili, Swedish, Tajik, Tamil, Telugu, Thai, Turkish, Ukrainian, Urdu, Uzbek, Vietnamese, Welsh, Yiddish, Yoruba, Zulu
