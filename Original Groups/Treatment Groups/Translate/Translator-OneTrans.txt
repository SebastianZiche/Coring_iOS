App Description
Do you have difficulties in communicating with foreigners or reading foreign books?
Use OneTrans Now, solving all your translation problems.

With Multi-Modle translation ways, OneTrans will become your best companion for travel, learning, and communication.
Text Translation：Text translation function can help you easily translate any text into multiple languages.Simply enter or paste the text that needs to be translated, and we will immediately provide you with the translation results.In addition, if you use OneTrans's keyboard, you can directly enter text on the keyboard and instantly obtain translation results without switching to other applications or browsers.

Voice Translation：OneTrans can instantly convert what you say into other languages and play it out in voice format, making cross language communication more convenient.With the voice translation function, you don't need to manually input text. You can quickly achieve translation through voice input, saving time and effort.

Photo Translation：By using the Photo translation function, you can take an image containing the text to be translated, and OneTrans will automatically recognize and convert it into text in the selected language for you to understand its meaning.

Real-Time Translation：The real-time translation function is a technology that can instantly translate languages into other languages, saving you time of waiting.
