App Description
Addressing challenges in communication, travel, or studying? Buddy Translate has been designed to resolve language-related issues, transforming your iPhone into a convenient language translator.



Key functionalities include:



Accurate translation of words, phrases, and sentences.

Voice recognition for seamless speech translation.

Translation of text from photos or scanned image files.

Face-to-face conversations with foreigners, allowing you to speak and check translations alternately.

Download stylish wallpapers featuring quotes and words.

Say goodbye to language barriers when interacting with individuals from diverse backgrounds. Simply install this app and embark on a journey of exploration by engaging in conversations with foreign people.
