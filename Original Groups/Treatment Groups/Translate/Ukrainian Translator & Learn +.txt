App Description
Hi ! UkraineGot is the leading ukrainian Translator and Learning App with a lot of outstanding features such as accurate voice translator, translate any text in ukrainian to english or ukrainian to english and extremely useful photo translating feature or practice reading, listening & reviewing ukrainian words.

Support for learning ukrainian more easily & Translate work-related documents, voice when traveling or working abroad, get the meaning of different words in photo, signal .

FEATURES
• Get text translation to english from ukrainian and many languages over the world.
• Listen to voice translator in male or female voices.
• Switch between different dialects from english to ukrainian or ukrainian to english.
• ukrainian Keyboard Extension
• Transliteration, Sharing, Favorites, History, Learn and much more.
• Learn ukrainian language like speaking, translating, reading, listening
• Use your camera to instantly translate menus or signs and more from ukrainian or any language to english.
• Speech translator, learn speaking ukrainian or any language easily
• Practice listening & reading ukrainian short stories, touch words for meaning
• Save new words easily from reading stories
• Improve ukrainian Vocabulary by reading ukrainian short stories from basic to advanced with english translation

1) Translate text
Real-time text translation for phrases and words, voice from any language to English.

2) Photo Translator
Automatically recognize and translate text in photo by using camera or scan photo and pressing a button, can touch a word to know its meaning or see all translated. Learn every where, any time.

3) Voice translator
Real-time speech translator to both text and audio, tap-to-play again and learn speaking languages.

4) Learn ukrainian Language
Learn speaking ukrainian to assess of pronunciation with the feature of Voice Translator

5) Improve ukrainian Vocabulary
Improve ukrainian Vocabulary by reading short stories from basic to advanced with english translation, reviewing new word flash cards.
