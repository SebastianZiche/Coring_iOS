App Description
Whether you're traveling to new places or trying to get started learning a new language, thanks to “Translate Mate”, the language is no longer a barrier to communication.
• Voice Translation
Speak to translate. Dictate what you want to translate and let the app read out the translation, or read it on your screen.
• Camera Translation
Translate any pic or text using your camera. Easily translate menus, signs, or any paper document.

•Text translation
Translate text into many popular languages. Enter the phrase you want to translate to receive the results in text format or to hear the correct pronunciation.
Our app makes it easy to communicate with people from around the world with its intuitive multilingual interface.Break down language barriers with our communication app, designed to help you connect with anyone, anywhere.Travel with confidence knowing you have our app, which can translate languages on the go.
