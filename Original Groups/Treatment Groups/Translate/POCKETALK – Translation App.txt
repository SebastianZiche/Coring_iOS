App Description
Pocketalk App brings industry-leading translation solutions to mobile devices for a connection anywhere you go. Honed through over half a billion translations, you’ll have access to the most reliable and trusted conversational translations whenever you have your phone (so always).

Pocketalk app hand selects the best translation engines for each language pairing ensuring fast, accurate and accessible translations that bridge the language divide. It stands in a class of its own; you won’t find another app that can translate with the same speed, level of accuracy, or in as many languages.

Why Pocketalk?
Pocketalk App creates a world of new possibilities for casual, spontaneous, and on-the-go conversations. The cloud-based app solution is the perfect companion to the handheld device; where the device is better for more formal settings with HIPAA and GDPR compliance, the app is found at your fingertips for those times conversation just happens.
● Ideal translation solution in casual settings like over coffee with an international colleague or during an interview with an international reporter.
● Never miss a conversation again with your grandparents that only speak their native language. Using the app will support building connections that may have been missed relying on other family members to translate conversations.
● Informal exchanges with your international colleagues that you’re meeting for the first time in person, or hundredth time for happy hour.
● Spark up a genuine conversation with your neighbor from Mexico, grocer from Greece, and beyond.

Features

● 56 languages: Take a picture and have the text translate with Pocketalk’s language autorecognition technology.
