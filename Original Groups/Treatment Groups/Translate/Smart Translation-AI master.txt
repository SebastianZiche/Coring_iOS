App Promotional Text

App Description
Language communication barriers when traveling abroad? You can receive corresponding service satisfaction in our application, whether you are at the airport, hotel, or in daily life, you only need to take a photo to translate with one click, or for business office, cross-border communication. With our dialogue translation, you can have barrier free communication!
We support multiple translation capabilities in order to provide users with better service standards
1. AI dialogue translation: You can directly ask AI about the content you want to translate, and AI also supports other abilities, so you can ask AI questions
2. Text translation: If you are accustomed to translating by entering text, you can use our text translation function for translation
3. Voice translation: We support voice translation function, and you can input the content you want to translate through voice input
4. Real time translation of conversations: You can use voice/text input for real-time translation, apply your multiple translation scenarios, and translate bilingual conversations in real-time
5. Document translation: You can upload the documents that need to be translated, and we support translating the content of your documents for your daily office scenarios
