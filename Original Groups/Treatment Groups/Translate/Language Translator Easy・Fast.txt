App Description
Voice Translator app is an easy-to-use app that helps communicate around the world. Immediate interpretation into 50 and translation into more than 100 languages.

Perfect translation process:
- 50 languages for instant voice translation
- More than 100 languages for text translation
- Possibility to conduct a dialog
- Support for Apple voice recognition

How it works?
- Dictate what you want to translate or type it
- Let the app read out the translation, or read it on your screen

Advanced features:
- Selection of an appropriate pronunciation rate
- Comfortable ways for sharing translations

Travelling around the world, carrying on international business or studying abroad will be much easier and let you put away language barriers only with the help of just one app.

Supported languages & dialects for translation:

Arabic (International), Catalan, Czech , Danish, Dutch English (Australia),English (GB), English (US), Finnish, French (Canada), French (France), German, Greek, Hebrew, Hindi, Hungarian, Italian, Japanese, Korean, Mandarin (China / Simplified), Mandarin (Taiwan / Traditional), Norwegian, Polish, Portuguese (Brazil), Portuguese (Portugal), Romanian, Russian, Slovak, Spanish (Spain), Spanish (LatAm), Swedish, Thai, Turkish and more
