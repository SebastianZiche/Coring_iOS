App Description
Voicelator: Translate Voice is an indispensable voice and text translator that allows you to communicate effectively in any corner of the globe. When you are at work, traveling abroad, meeting with foreign friends, solve your language barriers overseas business travel, and support easily translate over 100+ kinds of languages.



FEATURES



• Get translations in over 100 languages.



• Listen to translations in male or female voices.



• Switch between different dialects.



• Dictionaries with synonyms and different meanings.



• Phrasebook - With over 250 predefined phrases.



• Transliteration, Sharing, Favorites, History, and much more.



CORE FUNCTION



• Text translation: input text, you can get high quality translation



• Camera translation: translate text in images instantly, support camera taken and album selection. Map. Menus, books, street signs and etc, take photos and get translation results in time



• Real-time speech translation: real time two-way speech translation, making communication easier and more convenient



• Offline translation mode.



• Voice-to-Voice conversations.
