App Description
PRIMARY FEATURES

1. TRANSLATE TO and FROM 50 LANGUAGES. Translate any word, any phrase such as I want to exchange dollars, Take me to the airport, Can you help me etc and translate them to English, Spanish, French, Russian, Italian, Chinese, Hindi and 50 other major languages.

2. Bookmark your FAVORITE phrases.

3. Copy and paste from the app

4. EMAIL TRANSLATED PHRASES

5. Source can include many different languages like English, French, Japanese, German, Dutch etc.
