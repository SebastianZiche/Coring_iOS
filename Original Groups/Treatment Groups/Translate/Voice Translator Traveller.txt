App Description
Easily translate your voice into different languages.

With this app you can translate your voice into different languages.

Just speak to have your voice translated into the language you want.



Voice translator app features:

* Recognize your voice and convert to text

* Select your voice translation language

* Text-to-speech

* Ability to translate text

* Various spokespersons

* Text translator


* Multi Language Voice Translate



This app is for everyone and should always be with you to communicate with people from other countries and languages.
