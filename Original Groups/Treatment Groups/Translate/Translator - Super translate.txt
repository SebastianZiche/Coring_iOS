App Description
"Translator" integrates three major translation platforms, with photo translation, intelligent speech recognition, and voice into text translation black technology! Relying on the latest artificial intelligence technology, high-precision image and speech recognition model, easy and intelligent translation!

"The world outside is very exciting, and the world outside is very helpless." No foreign language, no matter where you go. As long as you have this translation tool, no matter where you are in the world, you don't have to worry about language problems. Multi-language translation function can let you go.


--【Core functions】--
1, text translation: integrated Baidu, Google, adao translation, accurate and authentic text translation!

2, photo translation: take photo recognition translation, album selection map translation optional; photo one-click translation, high-efficiency translation tool.

3, voice translation: the latest artificial intelligence technology, high-resolution vocal recognition turned into text translation!

4, dialogue translation: say a translation directly read, instant communication, accurate translation, travel and communication must travel abroad.

5, translation collection: word new words, unfamiliar common words, classic famous sentences, travel special language, business daily language, all-in-one collection, look and read anytime, anywhere.

6, practical settings: 1, support for setting the translation display font size; 2, support for setting the pronunciation speed, tone, volume.
