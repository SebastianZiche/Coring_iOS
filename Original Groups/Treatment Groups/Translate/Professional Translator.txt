App Description
Professional Translator is the must-have app if you need to switch between languages frequently or have a huge interest in new languages. We also provide VPN Servers for users, users can surf and entertain.
Basic Features:
Support real-time translation in more than 100 languages.

Various translation modes, including text, voice, and OCR.

Simple and beautiful translation interface.

VPN Server: Proxy Servers provided.

Text to Text: Easily enter text to translate into several languages.
Voice-to-Voice: Speak in your language and get the corresponding translation.
Camera to Text: Advanced scanning technology easily extracts text from pictures and translates them.
Text-to-Voice: You can choose to have your translated text output by voice.
