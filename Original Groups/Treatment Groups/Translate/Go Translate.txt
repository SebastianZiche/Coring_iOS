App Description
Whether you're traveling to new places or trying to get started learning a new language, thanks to “Go Translate”, the language is no longer a barrier to communication.

• Voice Translation

Speak to translate. Dictate what you want to translate and let the app read out the translation, or read it on your screen.

• Camera Translation

Translate any pic or text using your camera. Easily translate menus, signs, or any paper document.

• Text Translation

Enter the phrase you want to translate to receive the results in text format or listen to the correct pronunciation.
