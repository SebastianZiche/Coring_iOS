App Promotional Text

App Description
E-transmate Director is a professional translation application that supports text and voice translation, and you can also choose to take a photo to translate; it can help you communicate effectively in both work and life scenarios.
In text translation you need to manually enter the text you need to translate, when you choose voice translation, click on record to get the translated translation; when you take a photo, E-transmate Director can also quickly extract your text content and translate it into your target language.
