App Description
Global pioneering technology - RRT (Real-time ReWrite Translate), strikes a perfect balance between the demands for translation speed and accuracy in simultaneous interpretation. It makes simultaneous interpretation possible on a mobile app!

【Key Features】

• Instant translation in over 15 languages: Japanese, Chinese, English, Spanish, German, French, Italian, Russian, Korean, Thai, Czech, Indonesian, Cantonese, Indonesian, Vietnamese;

• Automatically detects the languages spoken by both parties;

• Automatically archives conversations and translation content;

• Female or male voice for simultaneous interpretation.

【Outstanding Advantages of RRT Function】
Felo's RRT function is a world-first, utilizing the latest AI technology to achieve a dual breakthrough in fast translation and high accuracy. By ingeniously combining two translation processes and considering text styles and contexts, it provides you with more precise and expressive translation results.

【Versatile Applications】

Whether you are engaging in face-to-face communication during international travel, participating in international conferences, seminars, cross-border trade exhibitions, or multinational company meetings, Felo's RRT function will make your communication smoother and more efficient. Furthermore, Felo's real-time translation is a powerful tool for learning foreign languages and practicing speaking. Let Felo be your intelligent translation assistant, ready to meet your language needs and eliminate language barriers in communication.

【Considerate Service】

Our application is filled with intelligence and warmth, just like a caring private translator by your side. Felo Real-time Translation unlocks your global vision instantly!
