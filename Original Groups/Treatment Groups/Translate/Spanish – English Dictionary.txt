App Description
Learn the translation and pronunciation of words in Spanish with the Spanish-English Dictionary. It's perfect for students!

Includes the following features:
· Audio PRONUNCIATION of each word through Text to Speech technology
· Spanish-English and English-Spanish dictionary
· Lots of examples!

Internet connection required. If you have any problems with the application please contact us at support@mobappeng.com
