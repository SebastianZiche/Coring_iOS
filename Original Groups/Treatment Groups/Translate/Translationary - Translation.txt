The fast, simple and convenient all-in-one app that helps you translate and define your everyday needs. Learning a new language can be this easy! Using the Today Notification Centre, words and phrases are at your finger tips. Quickly convert foreign hashtags and tweet in any language! Communicate with the world!


====================================
Features:

► Translate and define in over 40 languages used in 120 countries
► 100 most common nouns: English to spanish, French, Korean, Russian, Arabic, Portuguese Dictionary
► Unlimited Translation and definitions. No limits!
► Interact through the Today Notification Centre Widget! Easy access to translation and definitions of words and phrases without leaving any application
► Multiple language selection for Today Notification
► select which words to define through the Today Notification
► Option to copy translation and definition in Today Notification
► History of searched words for quick offline availability
