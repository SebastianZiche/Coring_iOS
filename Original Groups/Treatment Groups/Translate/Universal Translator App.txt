App Description
Do you have experienced language problems while traveling to another country? Then this app is the solution to your problems. This app supports 90+ languages. It included text to speech and speech to text converter.

Features:
Speak In the first Language and Listen in the Second Language
Text to speech & Speech to text
Reverse Language Selection
90+ Language Support
Font Customization
Select text from camera and image
Copy translated text to clipboard to paste anywhere.
