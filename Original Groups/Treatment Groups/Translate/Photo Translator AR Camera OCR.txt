App Description
Instantly recognize and translate any photo with text into over 100+ languages: For example, restaurant menus, documents, business cards, road signs, street signs and much more.

Main Functionality:
* Photo translation
Recognize text on photos and pictures of any format

* Object definition
Recognize any object and translate its name into the selected language

* AR Translation Camera
Point the camera at the text and get instant translation in real time

* Voice translator
Instant translation of a conversation in real time

√ Ability to share text through any applications

√ Auto-detect language

√ Support for voice over text

* Some features are not available in all languages.

** Free version may have limitations (for example, the number of transfers per day), which can be changed
*** We'd appreciate it if you take a minute to rate our app in the App Store!

Translations between the following languages are supported.

Afrikaans, albanian, arabic, azerbaijani, basque, belarusian, bengali, bulgarian, catalan, chinese (simplified), chinese (traditional), croatian, czech, danish, dutch, english, esperanto, estonian, filipino, finnish, french, galician, georgian, german, greek, gujarati, haitian creole, hebrew, hindi, hungarian, icelandic, italian, indonesian, irish, japanese, kannada, korean, latin, latvian, lithuanian, macedonian, malay, maltese, norwegian, persian, polish, portoghese, romanian, russian, serbian, slovak, slovenian, spanish, swahili, swedish, tamil, telugu, thai, turkish, ukranian, urdu, vietnamese, welsh.
