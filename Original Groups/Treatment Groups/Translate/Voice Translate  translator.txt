Amazing Voice Translation App with BUILT IN voice input & output SMART TECHNOLOGY and professional MULTI LANGUAGE TRANSLATIONS

With Translate Voice BE SURE YOU CAN !!!
JUST TALK AND LISTEN THE TRANSLATIONS !
Perfect if you want to:
- learn a new language.
- travel to a foreign country.
- chat with people who don't speak your language.

With Voice Translator you can:
SMART VOICE INPUT TECHNOLOGY!

NATURAL SOUNDING VOICE OUTPUT!
- If you're old school you can always bring up the keyboard and hear the translations. 36 languages and dialects are supported.
Share translations...
- Simply click on a send button to mail or SMS the translation.
Edit translations...

- you can always use your keyboard to edit translations.
