App Description
Leroy Translate is an advanced translation tool designed to provide users with an efficient and convenient language translation experience. Whether it's text translation or photo translation, Leroy Translate can meet your various needs in cross-language communication. With the help of advanced technology, this product can not only translate text quickly and accurately, but also achieve instant translation by taking photos, providing users with a more comprehensive language solution.

Core functions:
Text translation: Leroy Translate supports text translation in multiple languages, providing users with accurate and fast translation services. Just enter or paste text to get the translation results in the target language. This feature makes cross-language communication easy and provides convenience for users to break language barriers.
Photo translation: The innovative photo translation function frees you from the constraints of translating text. Upload an image of your text and Leroy Translate will quickly recognize and translate it into the language of your choice. Get instant translation results without typing, allowing you to understand and convey information more efficiently.
