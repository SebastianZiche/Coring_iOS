App Description
Have you ever wished for an always-on language teacher? With "Translator - AI Translate", you can turn your smartphone into your personal multilingual translator. Now powered by ChatGPT, you can ask questions in all languages, as if you had a language master at your side.

Whether you need to translate documents, photos, words, or voice, or want to converse in another language, our app is here for you. With the ability to translate between over 60 languages, "Translator - AI Translate" is your global companion for communication.

Key Features:

Text Translation: Translate over 60 languages online, with enhanced support for grammar and context.

Camera Translator: Capture or import photos for high-quality translations.

Voice Translator: Speak and let the app translate your voice in real time.

Conversations: Conduct bilingual conversations with smooth, accurate translation.

Access to ChatGPT: Ask questions in any language as if you had a language teacher at your side.

Save your translations: Record and save your translations for later use.

Whether you're learning a new language, traveling to a foreign country, or simply wanting to enhance your language skills, "Translator - AI Translate" is the perfect app for you. Download today and break through language barriers with a touch!

Afrikaans, Albanian, Arabic (العربية), Belarusian, Bengali, Bulgarian (Български), Cantonese (粵語繁體中文), Catalan (Català), Chinese Simplified (简体中文), Chinese Traditional (繁體中文), Croatian, Czech (Čeština), Danish (Dansk), Dutch (Belgium), Dutch (Nederlands), English (Australia), English (Ireland), English (South Africa), English (United Kingdom), English (united States), Esperanto, Estonian, Finnish (Suomi), French (Français), French (Canada), Galician, Georgian, German (Deutsch), Greek (Ελληνικά), Gujarati, Haitian, Hebrew (עברית), Hindi (हिंदी), Icelandic (Íslenska), Indonesian (Indonesia), Irish, Italian (Italiano), Japanese (日本語), Kannada, Korean (한국어), Latvian (Latviešu), Lithuanian (Lietuvių), Macedonian, Malay (Melayu), Maltese (Il-Malti), Marathi, Norwegian (Norsk), Persian (فارسی), Polish (Polski), Portuguese (Português), Portuguese (Brazilian), Romanian (Română), Russian (русский), Slovak, Slovenian (Slovenščina), Spanish (Español), Spanish (Mexican) Swahili (Kiswahili), Swedish (Svenska), Tagalog, Tamil, Telugu, Thai (ไทย), Turkish (Türkçe), Ukrainian (Українська), Urdu (اردو), Vietnamese (Tiếng Viê), Welsh.
