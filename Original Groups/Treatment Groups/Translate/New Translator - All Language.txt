App Description
Download the best translation app and get real-time translation in over 100 languages.
Features:
Support multiple translation modes, text, voice and OCR translation

Real-time translation of more than 100 languages.

Edit text anytime and paste it to the clipboard.

Advanced optical character recognition technology can help you quickly translate pictures.

Everything is effortless, just input content through the keyboard or microphone. In addition, any paper documents, restaurant menus, pictures on the Internet, manuals and manuals, as long as you take a picture and save it on your mobile phone, select it and perform OCR translation, you can quickly identify the characters in it and translate it into what you want.
Translate everything you want instantly! Come and download it!
