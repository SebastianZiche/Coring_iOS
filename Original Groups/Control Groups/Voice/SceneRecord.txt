App Description
A learning and sharing platform for recording production.

A MP3 Recorder, and is also a Voice-Changer.

●Support iOS 17●
●Support iMeesage●
●Support watchOS 5,watchOS 4,watchOS 3,watchOS 2 (Apple Watch can leave iPhone independent recording and playback)●
● Support 3D Touch ●
● Support iPad Pro ●
● Support Split View ●
● Support Widget ●

The operation is simple, easy to use.

●Taking pictures while recording, the best recording tool for meetings and classrooms●
●MP3 format direct recording, you can achieve full cross-platform sharing●
●Recording + photo = video, e-courseware a click to achieve●
●Cut, soundtrack, stitching, you can edit whatever you want●
●Text and handwritten notes taken pictures, convenient and quick to modify photos●
●Nine kinds of export methods, so that sharing becomes simple and easy●
●Data storage security technology, It can ensure that the recording data is well preserved●
●Real-time change the sound playback technology, opened a new chapter in the recording playback●
●Setting the volume level to start recording, monitor your sleep, and detect health problems in a timely manner●
●Apple Watch and iPhone control each other recording, complement each other●
●iMessage has no size limit to share●
●Support recording when Apple Watch lock screen, great savings in the valuable power to extend the use of time●


Special Features:
√ ●Data storage security technology, It can ensure that the recording data is well preserved●
√ ●Support to change sound when the sound is playing●
√ ●Added a key split function(Crop page can set the average number of segments, a key to automatically complete the recording average split)●
√ ●Support for handwritten notes on photos●
√ ●Support the photo to mark the text●
√ ●Support iMeesage●
√ ●Supports Apple Watch Complication●
√ ●Support for setting labels when recording●

√ Support set the volume level to start recording.

√ When other App play music, you can also record.

√ Support Touch ID.

√ Auto continue to record after being disturbed by call.

√ Support to change voice speed and pitch.

√ Support for MP3 format direct recording.

√ Support mix audio.

√ Support for camera(unlimited number of photos).

√ Support the recording and playback in the background or lock screen.

√ Query history recording notes and file names by keyword.

√ You can set a password to enter the software and ensure data security.

√ Supports loop or random continuous playback.

√ Support for export to album sharing.

√ Support for Wi-Fi sharing.

√ Support for export to other App to share.

Other features:

√ Support for other audio formats to MP3.

√ Support e-mail sharing.

√ Support iTunes sharing.

√ Support photo cropping.

√ Support recording crop (cropped recording format supported ringtone formats, facilitate the production of cell phone ringtones).

√ Support text notes and rename, can note in recording and playback.

√ Support for both compressed and uncompressed format.

√ Provide hourly 20M (MP3), 39M (WAV), 59M (AAC), 78M (WAV), 168M (IMA4), 320M (PCM),40M (MP3),10M (MP3) eight kinds of recording format, where 320M is not compressed recording format, the best sound quality.

√ Set the background music sounds volume(while mix audio).

Running very stable and reliable,with ads.

Very suitable for police,lawyers,journalists,voice acquisition personnel, meeting recording,training record,classroom recordings, music teaching, music production,recording and personal needs.

Voice Changer feature is ideal for funny and entertaining.
