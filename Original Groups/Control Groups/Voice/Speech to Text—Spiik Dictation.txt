App Description
Spiik transcribes audio, videos, and voice memos into text, with higher accuracy that beats our competitors hands down.

Featuring support for over 100 languages, the magic of voice-to-text translation is now at your fingertips!

FEATURES:

- Transcribe audio and video (100+ languages supported).
- Translate to 100+ languages.
- Transcriptions export as transcripts, SRT, or VTT.
- Organize your transcripts into projects.
- ad-free experience.

The best way to contact us: support+spiik@fadel.io

Our EULA can be found here: https://www.fadel.io/apps/terms.html
