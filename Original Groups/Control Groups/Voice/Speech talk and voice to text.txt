App Description
Odrey is the easiest way to convert audio and video to text. The application offers a number of options to work with files: direct recording, audio & video download and several sharing options.

Functionality

1. Direct record and import
Odrey offers several file management options: you can record audio directly in the application or download previously recorded audio and video.

2. The variety of languages
Odrey supports a huge number of languages, so it can be useful to everyone.

3. Smart cut
When you pause, Odrey will break the text into paragraphs, which makes it easier to navigate and edit the text.

4. No restrictions
Odrey allows you to decrypt files of any size, saving your time and effort.
