App Description
Hakara - Free sing karaoke & record songs app, enjoy and practice your singing

You can find karaoke songs with over 2 million songs

You can record your voice and share with your friends

FEATURES
- Sing karaoke free to millions of songs.
- Sing with the singer
- Karaoke anytime, anywhere on your phone
- Voice recording with high quality
- Sound effects like karaoke room
- Real time audio effect like echo, reverb if you like
- Save your song recorded
- Share your recorded to friend

Let join this app to experience a fantastic Karaoke world with your friends
