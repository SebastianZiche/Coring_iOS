App Description
SPEAK - SEND - DONE

The Philips voice recorder app offers first-class recording quality, professional functions such as inserting or cutting sequences, and an intuitive operation. For maximum security, browser-based file management and additional services such as speech recognition, we recommend the use of our convenient Philips SpeechLive service.

PHILIPS SPEECHLIVE - USE THE FULL POTENTIAL OF PHILIPS VOICE RECORDER APP
In combination with Philips SpeechLive you can benefit from many other advantages:

- SPEECH RECOGNITION

- TRANSCRIPTION SERVICE

- INTEGRATION OF YOUR PERSONAL ASSISTANT

- BROWSER-BASED FILE MANAGEMENT AND WORKFLOW

- INDIVIDUAL USER MANAGEMENT


TAILORED SOLUTION
Do you have special requirements for your dictation workflow? The Philips dictation app can be seamlessly integrated into your existing workflow.
