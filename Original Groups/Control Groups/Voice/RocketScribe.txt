App Description
100% free speech to text AI. Transcribe any file or dictate in real-time. Our AI is trained on hundreds of thousands of hours of speech data for world-class accuracy. Inference is made on the Apple Neural Engine for the best speed and performance possible. Your data is never shared with servers. RocketScribe speech-to-text AI runs entirely offline.
