App Description
MOREC is a high quality voice recorder.

You can listen to the sound while recording using your microphone and headphones, and you can add audio effects in realtime so that it's really useful for vocal practice and ASMR recordings.

MOREC is constituted of 8 sections below.

MICROPHONE GAIN
PITCH SHIFTER
HIGH PASS FILTER
LOW PASS FILTER
ECHO
REVERB
DISTORTION
STEREO DOUBLER

Each section has a button and a slider, which can make itself on/off and manipulate the parameter.

It's really useful to practice of singing, speech training, and so on.

Supported outputs: Headphones, Bluetooth, Airplay, AirPods, AirPods Pro, AirPods Max
Background listening/record supported.
WAV file export supported.
