Recorder Plus - Audio Editor
voice record, edit, transcribe
Utilities (Applications)
Free
iPhone Only
App Description
After nearly ten years of improvement, Recorder Plus has evolved into a simple, easy-to-use and powerful recorder - download now for FREE!

Record：
There is no limitation on recording time length and times.
You can record as long as you want and as many times as you want.
It's only up to the storage and battery power of your iPhone/iPad.
And you can add markers when recording.

Edit: (In-App purchase)
Cut, Copy, Paste, even between different recordings.
Undo, Redo, and you can get back original file easily.
Add markers and notes for the markers.
Import audio file from other apps or from Cloud drive.

Security:
Backup recordings automatically to your iCloud drive or Google Drive.
So your recordings will be safe even if your iPhone/iPad get lost.
And you can set iCloud as preferred storage in the app, so you can access all recordings on other devices(iPhone/iPad).

Share:
Share recordings by Email, Messages, other apps, cloud drive(iCloud drive, Dropbox, GoogleDrive, OneDrive, Box...).
Also you can download recordings by USB cable or WiFi sync.

Effect:(Subscription needed)
Tempo, Pitch, Gain, Noise reduction, Background music
Yes, this app is not only a recorder, but also a powerful audio editor.

Audio synthesis:
Text to speech support for 38 countries with 54 voices.

Speech to text:
Transcribe English speech to text.

More information about subscription($5/year):
1.Payment will be charged to iTunes Account at confirmation of purchase
2.Subscription automatically renews unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24-hours before the end of the current period.
3.Subscriptions may be managed by the user and auto-renewal may be turned off by going to the user's Account Settings after purchase
Terms of use: http://recorderplus.com/termsofuse.html
