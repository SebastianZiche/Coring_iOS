App Description
Effortlessly transcribe audio and video recordings, saving you time and effort.

◆⌘ ENHANCED TRANSCRIPTION WITH AI SUMMARIZATION ⌘◆
- GPT AI-powered summaries for quick comprehension of long recordings.

◆⌘ RECORD & TRANSCRIBE ⌘◆
- Record voice and conversations with user-friendly voice recorder.
- Transcribe audio & video from saved recordings and files.
- Record using built-in mic or Bluetooth headset's mic.

◆⌘ CAPTION RECORDINGS & VIDEOS ⌘◆
- Enhance recordings with noise reduction and sound amplification.
- Auto-remove pauses in speech and silence.
- Live remix recordings.
- Automatically generate and edit video subtitles.

◆⌘ NAVIGATE TEXT WITH AUDIO ⌘◆
- Convert voice recordings into text.
- Voice aloud reader for seamless listening while reading transcripts.

◆⌘ GET REMINDED ⌘◆
- Schedule reminders for meetings and lectures.
- Location reminders for recording tasks.

A perfect companion for students, professionals, journalists, and anyone seeking accurate and convenient transcription solutions. Convert voice recordings to text effortlessly and transcribe mp3 to text. Try voice dictation and speech texter features today for the future of transcription!
