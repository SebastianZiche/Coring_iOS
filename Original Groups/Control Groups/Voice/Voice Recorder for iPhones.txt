App Description
This is a free voice recorder that help users to record sound. It's useful for lectures, meetings, practice of foreign languages,... This tool is easy to use, it also records audio in background when your phone screen is off.
This app is absolutely free and does not limit the recording time, thus users can record any amount of audio depending on the remaining space of their phone internal memory. This voice recorder app stores your recordings and allow users to share them with their friends through social networking channels.
With simple & slick design, this app is very user-friendly & easy to use, which makes it a perfect portable audio recorder. Just tap on the record button to have your audio files ready to listen later.
