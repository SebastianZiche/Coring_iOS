App Description
Voice to text application brings you a very unique and distinctive feature of speech to text. This application has created ease for you to get text of whatever you speak, now you just need to press a button and speak whatever you want to and just in fractions of second get text of your speech. Voice to text application uses highly optimized voice recognition, which converts audio into text. Voice to text is not limited to single language but it supports multiple languages such, English, Spanish, Arabic, French, Italian, Urdu, Hindi, German, Chinese and many more.
Voice to text has made voice typing a piece of cake, now just dictate and get the text, this audio to text converter is easy to use and provides high usability for it users. Voice to text has various enriched features, along with getting speech to text, voice to text provides copy & share feature so that you can use it in various applications, and one more prominent feature of text to voice, you can also listen to the text, this feature enables you to play aloud the text.
Voice to text application also provides you, your recent activities so that you can get back to your previous data, so this application is a sum of various enriched features from copying & sharing to keeping track of your history.

Features:
• Supports Multiple Languages
• Speech to text
• Text to speech
• Voice & Text Translator
• Audio to text converter
• Keep track of your history
• Easy mic modes
• Copy, Share & Listen
• Edit text

The Voice & Text translator is brought to you in a new perspective, it as simple as a conversation, the whole theme is based on the conversation between to native speakers. Simply for using text translator type the phrase you want to translate, select the native language and the language into which you want to do the translation. For using voice translator just press mic button and speak whatever you want to translate and you will get instant and accurate translation in a matter of seconds.

Now speak in any language you want to and get a text in your desired language, this is not just a voice to text app but it acts as a voice typing tool, it assists you and make it easy for you to get text of what are you speaking.
Download this multi-function application now and save your time by voice typing, you just have to speak and this application will type it automatically..
