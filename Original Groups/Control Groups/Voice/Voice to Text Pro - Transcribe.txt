App Description
Redesigned from the ground up, Voice to Text Pro 5.0 is the best tool for converting any audio into text.

With Voice to Text Pro you won't need to type anything anymore, you just speak and your speech is instantly converted into text. It's also possible to transcribe audio from other sources files.

FEATURES WE LOVE

• Convert your speech to text
• Convert external files to text
• Share results to any app installed on your device or copy it to your clipboard
• Create notes based on your transcriptions or append text to existing notes
• Sync your notes across all your devices
• Optimized support for iOS 14, iPhone 12, iPhone 12 Pro and iPads
• And much more…
