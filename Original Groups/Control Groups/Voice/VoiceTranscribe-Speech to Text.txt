App Description
Effortlessly transcribe your voice memos and videos using VoiceTranscribe, leveraging advanced AI (Artificial Intelligence) and speech recognition technology. You only need to tap a button to provide easily readable and editable transcriptions.



Key Features:

-Transcribe in real-time while recording, with a preview.

-Import files from other applications and Dropbox for transcription, with no audio format limitations.

-Support for over 100 languages and dialects.

-Editable transcribed text, exportable in formats such as txt and Word.

-Intelligent voice recorder with multiple native recording modes.

-Audio editing capabilities: audio splitting, merging, compression, format conversion, speed adjustment, volume adjustment, and stereo synthesis.

-Text-to-speech conversion, featuring an intelligent voiceover tool with various selectable voices.

-Ad-free experience.



Designed with privacy in mind

Voice Recorderr Converter is 100% safe for your audio and documents. Audio processing is done locally on your device. Unlike other applications, Sound Recorder does not process files on remote servers. Unlike other applications, Sound Recorder does not process files on remote servers.
