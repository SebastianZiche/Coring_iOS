App Description
Transform your audio and video into text with AudioTranscriptor. Our advanced technology supports over 30 languages, allowing for easy transcription and sharing of your audio and video files as text, messages, and more. Upload your recorded audio files and store your transcripts locally for quick access. Experience the future of audio transcription today!

Features:

– Over 60 different languages

– Video transcription

– Easily share as plain text, messages, and more

– Transcribe recorded audio files – any audio file supported by your system

– Store files locally
