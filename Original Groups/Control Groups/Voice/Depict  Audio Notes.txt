App Description
Depict makes it easy to boost your productivity, and extend your memory with audio notes. Create notes and reminders quickly, by using your voice.

Depict transcribes speech on-device as you speak. Record audio notes, and receive playable transcripts.

INCREASE PRODUCTIVITY:
Voice reminders: create reminders with scheduled notifications using your voice

AUDIO TRANSCRIPTION:
On-device audio transcription
Record speech, and playback transcripts

NOTE-TAKING FEATURES:
- Tagging
- Pinning
- Archiving
- Searchable transcripts
- Append images
- Append text notes
- Save locations
- Sort by location

SEARCH, & FIND:
- Quickly search through past transcribed notes
- Filter recorded notes by tags

TRANSCRIBE, AND SHARE:
Export transcript text from audio notes

Depict includes an optional in-app purchase.
Subscriptions can be canceled at any time.
