App Description
Voice Recorder is your ultimate solution for voice recording with high-quality sound.

A new, modern design makes it even easier to capture and share personal notes, family moments, classroom lectures, work meetings, speeches, interviews and more. Also, It can help you learn languages easily.

Features

「Record」
- Use a Siri Shortcut to start a new recording.
- One tap to start, stop, pause and resume recording.
- Speech to Text, support for over 30 languages.
- Scheduled recording - Recording in the background at the agreed time.
- Noise Reduction - Make speech clearer.
- Record with background music. Tip: Please bring headphones when recording to avoid secondary recording of background music.
- Choose to record from the built-in mic, AirPods or external microphones.
- Record independently on Apple Watch and sync later.
- Markers - Use markers to select a specific audio position quickly.
- Multiple theme styles.

「Edit」
- Trim your recordings to save just the portion you want.
- Rename and tag recordings.
- Pinch-to-zoom for more precision when editing.
- Replace and resume to fine-tune your recordings.
- Add background music.
- Text - make corrections and add new text to your transcriptions.
- Collaborate with “Audio Editor” apps, using multi-track editing, music effects and more.
- Translate the text into the language you want.

「Storage」
- Quickly find recordings with the search feature.
- Choose to store recordings in iCloud Drive or locally on-device.
- Recordings stored in iCloud Drive automatically sync across all your devices.
- Recordings stored locally on-device are not accessible outside the app.

「Share and Export」
- Wi-Fi file sharing let you save recordings to your PC without any cable connections.
- With iCloud support, sync recordings between iPhone, iPad and MacBook.
- Share recordings via AirDrop, Mail, Messages, and more.

「APPLE WATCH」
Voice Recorder includes an Apple Watch app that gives you the freedom to record anywhere, even when your iPhone is not around.

- Start recording with a single tap on the Complication.
- Record discretely in the background.
- Unlimited recording time.
- Recordings automatically transfer to iPhone for transcription and iCloud syncing.
- Listen to recent recordings through the built-in speaker or AirPods.
