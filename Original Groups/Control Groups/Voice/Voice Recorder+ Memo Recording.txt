App Description
Voice Recorder by AppGeneration is the easiest, most flexible, and modern voice recorder ever! Download it for FREE!

It turns your iPhone or iPad into a portable audio recorder for any kind of professional and personal recordings and voice memos: working meetings, classrooms, lectures, speeches, personal notes, and more.

With Voice Recorder you can create powerful professional audio recordings in multiple formats, edit them with several customized options and manage / share with others.

Features:
• Unlimited recordings;
• Multiple supported formats: m4a, mp3, WAV, CAF, and AIFF;
• Transcribe recordings - Using advanced Speech-to-Text in several languages;
• Text notes - Add text notes to your recordings;
• Cloud support - Save your recording on your device or save them into iCloud;
• Share recordings - Send your audio recordings to friends;
• Edit your audio immediately - Trim, Merge, etc.;
• Add several audio effects - pitch, equalizer, delay, etc.;
• Add background audio - beach, city, rain, etc.;
• Manage your recordings in a calendar view;
• Search and favorite functions.

Voice Recorder is the only application that you will need on your iPhone or iPad to take personal and professional recordings and audio memos!
