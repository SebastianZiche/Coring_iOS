App Description
Turn your device into a karaoke machine! Singa karaoke app gives you free access to a huge library of high-quality karaoke songs. You can sing on your own, make it a party with friends or find the nearest Singa-powered karaoke venue and hit the stage.

- Sing karaoke anywhere, anytime – iPhone, iPad, Apple TV, computer, or Singa-powered karaoke venues

- All the best songs – Constantly updated library of high-quality karaoke songs from timeless classics to latest hits

- Hundreds of themed singlists – Find the right songs for every moment

- High-definition background videos – Looks crisp and clear on screens of any size

- Transposition – Adjust the song’s pitch to suits your voice

- Guide vocals – Helps you sing your way through an unfamiliar song

- Big screen karaoke – Use the Singa Apple TV app to upgrade your karaoke game at home

- Singa-powered karaoke venues – Find the nearest karaoke venue using the Singa mobile app, request a song, and hit the stage

Singa karaoke app takes you from boredom to karaoke stardom. Whether you’re a pro or just starting a new hobby, you’ll feel at home with Singa.

SINGA PREMIUM SUBSCRIPTION
Singa Premium is an auto-renewable monthly or yearly subscription that lets you sing karaoke as much as your heart desires. Any unused portion of a free premium trial period, if offered, will be forfeited when the user purchases a subscription during the free trial.

Payment will be charged to your iTunes Account. You can cancel a free premium trial or subscription anytime by turning off the auto-renewal through your Apple ID account settings. This must be done at least 24 hours before the end of a free trial or subscription period to avoid being charged.
