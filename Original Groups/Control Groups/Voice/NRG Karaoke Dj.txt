App Description
Ditch those old chunky music binders and get in tune with our new NRG karaoke app! From all the classic hits to today’s number 1 hit songs, including 10 different international languages!

You no longer need the old books to choose your favorite song from. At a click of a button you can pick from dozens of genres and save it to your favorites tab on the app. You can also stay informed with our live updates on the home page events and karaoke schedule all across Egypt! Karaoke has never been easier!

All you need is your device to rock out and sing like a star!
