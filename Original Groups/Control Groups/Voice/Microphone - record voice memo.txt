App Description
Microphone, a special effects recording software, in the recording can add a variety of music effects (applause, screaming, color, drums, etc.), podcast recording must app.

The following features are available:
1, (background) recording;
2, around the channel, stereo channel;
3, scene simulation;
4, music effects (applause, cheering, screaming, etc.);
5, echo function;
6, a variety of recording file format (wav, m4a, caf, aiff);
7, recording file sharing function;
