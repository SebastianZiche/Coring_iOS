App Description
Transcribe audio from media files or live capture offline using your device!

Transcriber uses the latest advancements in machine learning to leverage the power of your iPhone or iPad and provide transcriptions for media files or live recordings!

The whole transcription process is done using computational power from your device, so your data is never shared with any third party or external server, in respect to your privacy.

Keep your transcriptions organized on the app for later reading or share them easily to other apps with a single tap!

Transcriber is now available for all iOS devices!
