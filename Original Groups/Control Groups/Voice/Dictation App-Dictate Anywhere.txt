App Description
Presenting Dictation App - your pocket transcription powerhouse!
A professional-grade service that lets you dictate, edit, and share documents from anywhere.

WHY CHOOSE DICTATION APP?

Whether you're mobile or stationary, your documentation needs never pause. Dictation App eliminates restrictions on dictation length or time, letting you voice out anything from a quick note to a full-fledged report.

SPECIAL FEATURES:

• Immediate, 99% accurate dictation with zero training needed.
• Create and navigate form-based templates with voice commands - a lifesaver on-the-go!
• Add industry-specific words and make custom voice commands for repetitive tasks.
• Select, edit, and format text using voice commands, or navigate through report templates.
• Easily import/export documents to popular cloud services like Dropbox®.
• Synchronize with compatible desktop apps for a seamless workflow.

Remember, we need internet connection for our cloud-based speech recognition. For assistance, contact our support:
meta.innovation@outlook.com

We prioritize your privacy:
• All communication is secured with top-tier encryption.
• We only access your device’s microphone and storage.
