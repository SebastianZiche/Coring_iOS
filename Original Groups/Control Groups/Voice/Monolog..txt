Monolog.
Audio Recorder & Editor
Utilities (Applications)
Free
App Description
With Monolog you can:
• Record and listen by holding the device to your ear, like talking on the phone (iPhone only)
• Cut, copy and paste to edit a recording like text
• Transcribe speech while you record or afterwards
• Backup and sync your recordings with iCloud
• Share via message, email and more
• Keep recording or listening while the screen is locked
