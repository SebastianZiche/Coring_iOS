App Description
The BEST Voice Recorder for iPhone and iPad!
Voice Recorder allow you to record voice memos at unlimited length with high-quality. Voice Recorder can record directly in MP4 (AAC), and share with email and other ways.

Features:
- Unlimited recording time
- Auto-save for never losing any voice memo
- Useful file & voice list management system
- Loop and shuffle playing mode
- Share Audio via email and other ways
- Skip backwards/forwards 15 seconds
- Stunning user interface designed and easy to use

Voice Recorder must be your best choice for recording anything in your life!
