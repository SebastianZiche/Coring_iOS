App Description
Masingo - Karaoke Singing App



Discover the ultimate karaoke party in your pocket with the Masingo singing app – perfect for parties, family gatherings, and impromptu singing sessions! Our all-in-one karaoke app allows you to watch your favorite music videos all while singing along with the lyrics on screen.





-Highlights-





SING ALONG MUSIC VIDEOS

No more forgetting words or having to look them up online – just immerse yourself in the music with both the lyrics and music video on screen.





KARAOKE YOUR WAY

The Masingo karaoke app includes adjustable features such as a live Karaoke toggle (vocals on and off). Sing along with your favorite artists or switch their vocals off and become the star of the show!





CUSTOMIZE YOUR EXPERIENCE

Our user friendly app allows you to effortlessly change the lyric size and color to suit your preferences. You can even test your song knowledge and toggle the lyrics on and off, the ideal singing app to use if you’re learning a song to perform.





100K+ SONGS AT YOUR FINGERTIPS

Access our extensive library of top hits and popular songs across multiple genres, no matter what kind of music you love, we’ve got something for you. Filter, search and add your favorite songs to the playlist ready for you to sing again and again.

Can’t find a song? We strive to give our users a first class experience. Fill in our request a song form and we’ll do our best to add it to our app.





KARAOKE SHOP

Want to take karaoke to the next level? Visit our in-app store and find Masingo’s professional karaoke machines and equipment, so you can rock those tunes like never before!





Whether it's a fun night with friends or a solo jam session before bed, Masingo is the perfect karaoke app to let loose and get grooving!





Download FREE and start your karaoke performance!
