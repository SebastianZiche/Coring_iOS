App Description
AcousticLab is a professional stereo recorder APP developed by ZKXY (a member of BSWA Group). This APP will turn your iOS device into a professional noise measurement device with precise sound acquisition and analysis. The measurement system will fulfill the requirement of IEC and ASTM standards when using an external stereo sound card and precision microphones. The input channels (microphones) can be calibrated with a standard calibrator to get the actual sound level of test signals, and the output channels also can be corrected according to the surrounding environment.

- Support mono and stereo recording.
- The input channels can be calibrated with standard sound level calibrators or manually input the calibration factor.
- Support two kinds of setting to automatically trigger the recording.
- Prerecord function ensures every important signal will be capture.
- The key position of the signal can be easily marked and playback.
- The volume of the two playback channels can be adjusted respectively.


Monthly description of audio data package:
-FFT analysis of audio data. File format modification (caf/wav).
