App Description
Start a karaoke party from your phone! With over 20,000* licensed songs to choose from, it’s easy to start singing.

Features
- Songs in the style of Billie Eilish, Lil Nas X, Shawn Mendes, Queen, Bon Jovi, and many more
- All genres: pop, rock, country, Disney, show tunes, and more
- A wide range of eras: from yesterday’s classics to today’s hits
- A variety of languages
- Pre-made playlists (Holiday, What’s Hot, Just In, and more)
- Personalized song queues of up to 100 songs
- Compatibility with Airplay to mirror the karaoke app on Apple TV
- The option of casting lyrics to TV with Google Chromecast
- Possibility of singing along to lead lyrics or without them
- Low-bandwidth option, by switching the karaoke player to display lyrics on black, rather than video

Karaoke plans
FREE: 5 full-length songs, updated monthly
$9.99: Weekly access for unlimited access to all songs
$14.99: Monthly access for unlimited access to all songs
Payment will be charged to your iTunes Account at the time of purchase (or auto-renewal) in your local currency equivalent to the USD value as determined by the Apple App Store.
You may manage your subscription and turn off the auto-renewal by going to your iTunes Account Settings and selecting Subscriptions > Sing with Stingray Karaoke
