App Description
Finally you can use the new industry leading AI to transcribe your voice memos into text.

This app is able to process large audio recordings into accurate transcriptions with ease.

Generate transcriptions offline and all on your device.

Free Features!
- Easily record and transcribe audio files
- Transcribe without internet
- Transcribe all on your device, no audio leaves your device
- Get transcriptions immediately
- Select a language or have it auto detect it
- 5 transcription generations
- Easy to use interface
- Record audio in the background
- Share your transcriptions to your email and apps

Pro Features!
- Unlimited transcription generations
