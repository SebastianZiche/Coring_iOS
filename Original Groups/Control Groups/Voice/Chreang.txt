App Description
"Chreang" is the first Khmer Karaoke app for iPhone/iPod/iPad.

Now there is no need to go to karaoke room, you can enjoy singing from your smartphone at anywhere and anytime. With our app, you can sing and record your favourite songs and share it to Facebook.

"Chreang" is a realtime karaoke app, mean user can listen to their voice mix with music during singing. Moreover, user can choose which room type effect that fit with user voice and current environment such as Normal room, KTV room, Hall or Concert.

To support our work, please register to be our member and start test our app, we're guarantee that you will enjoy with this app.

This app is optimised for iPhoneX.

Let's share your voice to the world with us.
