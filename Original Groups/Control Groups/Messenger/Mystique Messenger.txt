App Description
Welcome to Mystique! The secure messaging app brought to you by 3rd Eye Technologies. Mystique allows you to communicate via private clouds with end-to-end encryption, that safeguards your messages. Mystique uses your device's internet connectivity (LTE/5G) or WIFI to create individual or group messages.
