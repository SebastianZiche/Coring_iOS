App Description
The DoTalk app is your all-in-one communication companion, making it fast, easy, and convenient to translate and communicate in over 90 languages. DoTalk multilingual platforms will help connect you with friends and business partners from all over the world and is a must have app for on all your amazing global travel adventures ahead.

Simply log in, or join for free to begin enjoying your DoTalk free features.

Why else should I use the app?
- You can send private messages in over 90 languages
- Send media and files
- Send voice messages
- Create group chats
- Make Calls & Video Calls
- Search People Near & Far
- DoTalk offers disappearing chats, messages can only be read by its intended recipient
- Writing a Post in the News Feed that is instantly translated to your friends and followers
- Access to the DoTalk web version

What are the Premium features?

- Image to text translation
- On The Go Translation
- Create group chats with in using 10 different languages
- Video Calls with subtitle translation
- Access to the DoTalk web version

DoTalk takes your privacy seriously and will never give any third parties access to your data. You can delete any message you ever sent or received for both sides, at any time and without a trace. DoTalk will never use or sell your data.

Download the DoTalk app now to have everything you need to communicate with anyone, anywhere, anytime!

Our aim is to make DoTalk the “Ultimate Multilingual Messenger” with many more unique features planned in future updates!
