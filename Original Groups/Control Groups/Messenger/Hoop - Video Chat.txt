App Description
Hoop is a Video Chat app that connects you with hundred with millions of nearby and around the word.

Wanna meet new people?

Want to make some new friends?

Do you want to meet that special somebody, or you just want to have some fun?

Whatever your heart desires, you are at the right place – welcome to Hoop - Video Chat!

Don’t be shy and just dive in – it’s friendly, it’s safe, it’s free, and it’s FUN! Have one-on-one video chats with millions of people and find new friends around the world with a single tap. Like people, stay in touch (if you want).

You can really use Hoop FREE OF CHARGE.

There are no limitations - talk to people as long as you wish. Some optional features do require in-app purchases.

Your user data

Your user data will always be protected on our secure and encrypted servers. Please just always be mindful of the private information you may reveal while chatting on Hoop.
