App Description
Kafu is a wonderful Group Voice Chat app.In Kafu,you can enjoy voice chat and endless fun with people who are from different regions and countries.Join an online party in Kafu anytime anywhere.Let's start the voice chatting party and find friends at Kafu voice chat room!TOTALLY FREE!

*Features*

1.Voice Match
-You can have voice chat with the best match.
-In an anonymous 4-minute voice conversation, everyone can find another "self" in Kafu.

2.Group Voice Chat
-Join a group chat room and start a voice chat party, have fun with real sweet people.
-Create your own room without any level limit and any fees.

3.A Diversity of Gifts
-Kafu provides stunning and animated gifts for you to show your adoration.
-Cool gifts effects make everyone pay attention to you,just like a super star.

4.Featured Rooms
-You can enjoy yourself in various romms such as Music room and Koran room.
-Choose the room according to the topic, then you will find friends with the same interests.

5.Match by Tag
-Kafu can help find the one who fits your soul best for you.

6.Safe Private Chat
- Say Hi！Have fun coversations here.
- You can send voice,messages and photos with your friends from all over the world.
