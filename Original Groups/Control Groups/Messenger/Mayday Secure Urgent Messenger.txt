App Description
An application to send and receive ONLY urgent messages. Rest assured: if it's not a Mayday, it's not important.
We want you to have time for yourself, to concentrate, to forget about so much multimedia content and be master of your time.


• ONE MESSAGE, ONE RESPONSE.
Limited messages: Mayday is only for communicating urgent situations. You can continue talking in other apps.

• MESSAGES ARE DELETED AFTER 24 HOURS.
We don't want your data: Mayday messages are encrypted and we don't save them: they are deleted 24 hours after being responded to automatically.

• STAY FOCUSED.
Take a break from notifications, knowing that if there's something truly important, you'll receive it.

This is the first version and we are still polishing things, so stay tuned for future updates!!!
