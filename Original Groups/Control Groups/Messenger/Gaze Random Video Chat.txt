App Description
Hey, there new Gazer are you ready to be part of the video clique? Gaze opens new doors to meeting new friends and video chat with people around the world. Private live video chats with thousands of users. Gaze is a safe place for you to embrace your individuality and share your quirks with others via video chat.

You do not need to swipe and wait for a match or a text. Start live video chatting instantly and have, fun video chats with people from all over the world. The greatest chat alternative app, Gaze is here for you.

Here is what we got for you:

- Send text messages and chat with the people
- Video Chat filters to find exactly who you are looking for
- Keep in touch with your friends and call them anytime
- Want to be totally surprised? We also have random video chat
- Video chats are translated in real-time
- Private and secure video chat
- Fast and simple
