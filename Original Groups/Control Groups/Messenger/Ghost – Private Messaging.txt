App Description
Finally, it is possible for You to communicate privately via Your mobile phone.
Ghost is an encrypted communication platform that allows voice, video, and instant messaging.

* Absolute Anonymity - we never collect or store your information

* No User Data - even we don't know who you are

* Ad-Free - focus on private messaging

* No Local Data Storage - no data is stored on your device

* Multi-Profiles - ability to manage discussions under multiple aliases

There is no connection between your account and your phone - Ghost can be used from any phone, as it is not connected to your mobile number.
