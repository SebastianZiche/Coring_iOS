App Description
Welcome to Botim - making your life easier and simpler.

Botim, the most loved and trusted communication platform has transformed into an ultra platform. The new intuitive interface and advanced features aim at performing multiple tasks for you, offering you ease and convenience every day along with complete security. No matter where you stay in the world, you can interact, and share all you like with your loved ones.

Download Botim and make the most of the following services:

BOTIM VOIP: Enjoy secure and private group video and voice calls; digital KYC; easy money transfers; built-in emoji dashboards; mobile recharges; bill payments; online games and much more! Get encrypted calling and messaging on 2G, 3G, 4G, 5G, and WiFi connections without using a VPN.
Conversations on Botim are secured with AES-256 encryption, allowing you to connect in a secure and reliable way.

MAKE ENCRYPTED CALLS ACROSS BORDERS
We're not just a Dubai video calling app! Whether it's a free call to a country or a free call from another country, Botim lets you make secure and encrypted connections with people across the globe!

CONNECT IN GROUP CHATS & CALLS
Botim lets you join secure and private group chats with up to 500 contacts and make group video calls with up to 21 people at the same time!

MAKE PHONE PAYMENTS & RECHARGES
Need to pay an Etisalat bill? Have to make a mobile top-up? We've got you! Make secure bill payments and mobile recharges for every major network provider across the world, including:
UAE: Etisalat, DU
India: Airtel, Vodafone, BSL, Jio, MTL, Vi India, Pakistan: Telenor, Ufone, Warid, Zong, Jazz Philippines: Globe, Cherry Mobile, Smart (SunCellular)
Bangladesh: Teletalk, Robi, Banglalink, Airtel, Grameenphone

BECOME A BOTIM VIP MEMBER
Subscribe and upgrade to an ad-free experience with Botim’s VIP Membership program! Get early access to upcoming features and enjoy higher network quality, HD calling, background blur, and an exclusive VIP badge on your Botim profile!

BOTIM MONEY: Sending and Receiving money has never been this easy. With Botim’s fast and secure International Money Transfer services, send money anytime, anywhere.

International and Local Money Transfer:
If you're using Botim in the UAE, you can quickly and easily send money to your loved ones. Experience the power of seamless, borderless money transfer across 170+ countries with Botim!

BOTIM SMART: Introducing Botim Smart, the all-in-one solution for government services, bill payments, and home services. With everything you need in one place, and easy access from your phone, life is simple and easy.

Emirates ID Issue and Renewal:
With just a few taps on your smartphone, and without visiting a government office, you can submit your application and receive your new Emirates ID within a few days.

BOTIM STORES: Experience the best of intuitive conversational commerce. From groceries to fashion, and electronics to home decor, you can have everything delivered at your doorstep in the most convenient of ways.

BOTIM HOME: Your one-stop-solution for all kinds of important services you could have asked for. Be it home services, pharmacy, or cleaning services, we've got you covered.

CONNECT AND COMPETE IN ONLINE GAMES
Stay entertained with games on Botim! Connect with players in live voice chats and climb the ranks in popular games such as YouDraw, Youkout, and YouLudo!

STEP INTO A HEALTHIER LIFE
Use the in-app step counter which integrates with the Health app and uses Apple Health Kit to keep track of your daily movements.

QURAN KAREEM AT YOUR FINGERTIPS
Discover the Holy Quran with Botim! Use the explore section to access verses from the Holy Quran in HD quality.
*Operator data charges may apply.
