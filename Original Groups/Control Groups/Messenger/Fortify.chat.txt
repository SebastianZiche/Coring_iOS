App Description
Fortify is a comprehensive messaging system which incorporates many useful features such as:

1 . Direct one-to-one and group messaging.
2 . Integration with other collaboration/messaging Apps
3 . Ability to upload commonly used file types (images, documents, audio and video).
4 . Notifications.
5 . User defined badges.
