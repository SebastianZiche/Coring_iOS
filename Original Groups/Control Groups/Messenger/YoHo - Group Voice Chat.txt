App Description
Anonymous chat is easier and voice chat is more fun!

If you want to chat with someone, try our app-YoHo. It only takes 1 second to create a voice group room or join other chat rooms which can chat, sing.

We are #YoHo! Meet strangers with your voice.
We are #YoHo! Chat with you with music.
We are #YoHo! Get more smiles with a group of funny people.
We are #YoHo! group voice chat app.

Join a seat to chat & private chat room
In YoHo, you can enjoy a fluent, high-quality voice chat, where you can speak your mind.

FEATURES:
#group voice chat
Create a group voice chat room, invite friends or wait for strangers to join in the chat room, use text or voice to chat with friends, enjoy the fun of a great voice chat party.

#Voice chat tag
You can add topic tags to your voice chat rooms. There are different chat tags for music, chatting, Karaoke, Truth or Dare game, and tons more. You can choose the chat room which you are interested in according to the topic. The tag will help you find friends with the same interests more accurately so that you guys will have more topics to talk about.

#Awesome virtual gifts
YoHo offers lots of amazing virtual gifts, strengthen the friendship between you and your friends by sending cute virtual gifts. Everyone likes receiving gifts from others. Every gift from a friend is a wish for your happiness.

#10 seconds to register
Use a message and Facebook to sign up for a YoHo account. It is easier and saves you more time. Enjoy your voice chat experience quickly and easily.
