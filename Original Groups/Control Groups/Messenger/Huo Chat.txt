App Description
Huoxin is an IM platform that fully embraces Web3.0 and metaverse ecological construction. Support large groups of 100,000 people, massive community online communication, community as the core, provide rich community management and operation tools and the latest and most complete industry hot trends, for blockchain enthusiasts, digital collections, meta-universe enthusiasts and Web3.0 enthusiasts to provide a convenient interactive platform.

Product features:

a. Communication, instant messaging. Supports multiple communication modes: text, voice, exclusive expression, picture, video, and call.

b. Ten thousand people group, private conversation. Join valuable project community, make friends in the circle, in-depth understanding of the project progress, get first-hand information, grasp the opportunity. More content, knowledge and wealth double harvest.

c. Digital collection verification into the group, verify personal digital collection, automatically join the corresponding community, establish collection consensus.

d. Digital collection avatar display, different from Web2.0 image display, has a unique identity authentication.
