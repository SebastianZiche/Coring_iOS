App Description
UChat is simple, reliable, and private, so you can easily keep in touch with your friends and family.

Your personal messages and calls to friends and family are end-to-end encrypted. No one outside of your chats, not even UChat Team, can read or listen to them.

Simple and secure connections, right away

All you need is your phone number, no user names or logins. You can quickly view your contacts who are on UChat and start messaging.
