App Description
Want a simple and quick way to send messages to your friends on the Discord app? Download MiniCord now, we support notifications and support for sending new messages.

We also support reading messages from all your favourite Discord servers and even your private direct messages!

Discord Server: https://discord.gg/R5vFY65

--UPCOMING FEATURES--
-Voice Chat Support
-Change profile details too in settings menu
-Emoji Support
-Send voice messages in chats
-Change your profile status
-Change your status(online/do not disturb/offline)
-See other people's profile status
-See other people's status(online/do not disturb/offline)

Current Features:
--2FA support(only when using the QR Code Login/Phone Login)
--QR Login Support
--Reply to messages
--Reactions
--Send messages
--View messages
--Access your servers, dms
--Access group chats in dms
--Access channels in Server
--Change some settings in the settings menu
