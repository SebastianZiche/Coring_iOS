App Description
Tokee is a messaging and video calling app. It's fast, fun, simple, and reliable. Tokee strives to connect everyone in the world no matter the distance. Share stories with your friends on your profile and even edit the videos / photos you took through the app. Enjoy Tokee anytime and anywhere in real-time!

PRIVATE MESSAGING
• Simple, secure, and reliable messaging
• Make group chats with all your friends
• Send fun and cool GIFs to your friends and loved ones
• Backup your messages & media data

VOICE & VIDEO CALLS
• Enjoy 1:1 voice and video calls
• Multi-task while on voice & video calls

PROFILE & STORIES
• Change and customize your Tokee Profile
• Share daily moments through Status

STICKERS
• A variety of stickers to choose from while chatting
• Popular stickers and GIFs from GIPHY collection, send as many as you like!

GIPHY
• Enjoy the ease of accessing and sending STICKERS, EMOJIs, and GIFs that GIPHY has to offer

Status allows you to share text, photos and videos that disappear after 24 hours.
