App Description
The first truly secure messenger. Tukan uses modern cryptography algorithms to ensure your privacy and anonymity while giving you the best messaging experience.

ANONYMOUS: We don't ask for your phone number, e-mail address or any other personal information.

SECURE: We use end-to-end encryption technology in both private and group chats. That means the messages can only be accessed by the end users' devices and are hidden from Tukan servers or any third parties.

SIMPLE: While having strong encryption under the hood, Tukan still provides a clean interface with all the features you are familiar with: group chats, stickers, photos, documents, voice messages and more.
