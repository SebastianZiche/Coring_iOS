App Description
YallaChat is an instant messaging app. Simple and fast, it can be downloaded and used for free. YallaChat allows you to safely communicate with your friends or family by sharing important moments, sending and receiving messages, photos, and superior voice & video messages and calls.

【Secure Messaging】
Communicate with your friends and family at any time by sending various types of messages such as stickers, photos, text, and voice and video messages. All messages are well-protected with end-to-end encryption on YallaChat.

【Voice & Video Calls】
High-quality audio and instant video calls with friends and family, no matter where you are; 1v1 or group calls are all available.

【Voice Chat Room】
24-hour online voice chat room and group chats on multiple topics to meet your abundant interests. You can create your own room to meet more new friends with shared hobbies.

【Interactive Games】
Play Ludo, plant your date-palm tree to get mobile credits or challenge the super difficult game Alpaca....more interesting games are waiting for you.

【Athan Reminders】
The Athan times are generated according to arithmetic methods adopted in most of the Islamic world. By automatically determining your location, this application can remind you of accurate prayer times as well as determine the direction of the Qibla.

【Share Moments】
Interact with your friends by sharing your important moments. Post photos, videos, and more to your Moments.
