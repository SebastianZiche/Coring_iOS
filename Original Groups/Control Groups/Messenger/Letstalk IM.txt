App Description
Letstalk provides free, no advertisement communication services with multiple encryptions for individuals or businesses around the world. Others cannot get your profile, messages, or calls under multiple security encryptions. Letstalk supports cross-device, multi-account management, and the variety of chat settings.

• Send a post about your life: Share files or latest news to your friends or family in the chatroom.

• No more losing important messages: Save the message to the Safe Box and set a password for the security.

• Account management: Log in to all your accounts on the same device and switch at any time.

• Let messages disappear automatically: Send the ""Burn after read"" message or set the message preserve time, deleting the message when expired.

• Send the greetings or remind someone on time: Preset the text and sending time, the message will be sent automatically when expires.

• Want to express your feeling: Send a sticker or adjust the display size of text, make your messages vividly.
