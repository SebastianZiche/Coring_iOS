App Description
Gala - a daily fun journey with strangers around the world

Bringing you new social adventures every day. We match you with strangers from all over the world so you can chat, play, and have a great time all day long.

At Gala, you'll have online entertainment with strangers from every corner of the world.Every day is a new social experience, like meeting new friends in a sea of people. Whether you want to video call or text with them, our powerful technology ensures smooth calls.

User privacy is our primary concern

We attach great importance to your privacy and security. You can share and communicate with confidence, because we have adopted the most advanced security technology to ensure that your personal information is fully protected.

Download Gala to start your global social journey and build meaningful connections with strangers around the world, all in one day!
