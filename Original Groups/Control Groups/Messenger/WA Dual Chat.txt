App Description
You can use WhatsApp on your iPad with this app and you can also have 2 numbers of WhatsApp in your iPhone or one account on two devices, if you like.

Just like the web version of your your favorite social media app, the WA Dual Chat is an extension of your phone, which simply mirror your conversations and messages as they are in your phone device, that means, all your messages remain originally saved on your phone.

To connect, open the "WA Dual Chat" app and you will see a QR code, simply 'scan' this code using WhatsApp on your phone and you will be logged in. You will find this option within WhatsApp settings on your phone, at "WhatsApp Web" menu.

To have an access to the functionality of this app user have to have active auto-renewable subscription.

Your phone must be connected to the internet for correct usage of the "DualChat for WhatsApp", and in addition, make sure you have the latest version of WhatsApp app is available for your phone.

Features :

—> Password protection. Face ID & Touch ID friendly.
