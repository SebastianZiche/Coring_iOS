App Description
Fachat is an online chat app where you can have tons of fun! Come join us to discover new people and perhaps find a friend who shares all your interests!



With Fachat, you can discover new people happy and carefree. Tap to light up your half of the heart if they’re your cup of tea, or simply tap again and chat with the next person. Open up to your new friends, confide your troubles to them, and tell them about your life through live video chat at any time, any place!



Fantastic Features of Fachat



- Simple & Fast Video Chat

Don’t be shy! Showcase the best you! Discover new people in the simplest way. Tap on the screen to video chat with friends from different parts of the world.



- Stress-free Text Chat

When you find yourself short of words or terribly shy, you can text to show your feelings or send a goofy sticker to ease the embarrassment. We also provide a real-time translation feature, so say bye to the language barrier!



- Meaningful Social Connection

On Fachat, you’re no longer just passersby to one another. You may even develop a profound friendship here. No worries, you can keep in touch with your new buddies on Fachat for as long as you want.



Let’s slow down for a second from our daily bustles, and enjoy the fun Fachat brings. Try Fachat and get to know awesome people from all over the world now!





Privacy Policy:

- In Fachat video chat app, all personal information is securely stored and information other than what you write directly on the profile cannot be seen by other users.

- Please take caution when delivering sensitive information, because you are responsible for the information you provide to the other party once you connect through Fachat chat video.

- Sexual, pornographic, or nude behaviors are not allowed and users generating them will be immediately banned.
