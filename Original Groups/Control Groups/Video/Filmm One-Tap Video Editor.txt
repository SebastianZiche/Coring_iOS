App Description
Filmm makes it simple to edit your videos! Mix and stack professional filters with authentic film textures, light effects, and subtle distortions. Add music and text with the press of a button, choosing from tons of royalty-free options.

Cut and clip your video with ease using Filmm’s easy video editing interface. Use crop detection and make sure your video is optimized for Tik Tok and other social media platforms. Check out the film-inspired frames and effects available in the app and control watermark size and placement with just a tap.

Edit your videos with one tap using Filmm’s Looks! With 100+ filters and effects, there’s sure to be a combination that captures your aesthetic. Mix, match and stack all the filters and effects to find the look you want.

Add captions or text overlays with over 30 fonts and text animations to choose from. Filmm has tons of royalty free music options for you to mix and match, letting you create the perfect soundtrack to back your Day in the Life or Get Ready With Me videos. Add audio effects and clips with the tap of a button, all from Filmm’s library.

Start editing like a pro today with Filmm! Download and subscribe today!

MULTIPLE EFFECTS AVAILABLE
• One-tap full featured Looks let you edit your videos quickly and easily
• Filmm packs over 100 filters and effects for presets you’ll love
• Realistic film-inspired frames and complete control over watermarks
• 30+ modern font and text animations let you customize captions
• Crop detection, pre-ready for social media dimensions

PROFESSIONAL EDITING FEATURES
• Multi-clip editing with professional transitions let you make videos like the pros
• Advanced adjustment tools including curves give you complete control over tone and color
• Choose from tons of audio and music options, including custom royalty-free music
• Story splitting save options let you save Instagram and Snapchat stories in timed increments
• Clip trim, cut, reverse and duplicate your frames, clips or videos to fit perfectly

Unlock all Filmm packs, frames, transitions, text, music, watermark control and all upcoming features with Filmm+!

SUBSCRIPTION PRICING, TERMS, & CONDITIONS

The Filmm+ subscription is $29.99 USD per year or $5.99 USD per month. Pricing in other countries may vary, and charges may be converted to your local currency depending on the country you live in.

FILMM+ YEARLY
Filmm+ Yearly Subscription starts with a free 3-day trial period. Once the trial is over, a recurring billing period of $29.99 per year automatically begins upon subscription. Payment will be charged to iTunes Account at confirmation of purchase. Subscription automatically renews unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24-hours before the end of the current period. Account will be charged for renewal within 24-hours prior to the end of the current period. Subscriptions may be managed by the user and auto-renewal may be turned off by going to the user's Account Settings after purchase.

Filmm+ Monthly Subscription enables access to all app features. A recurring billing period of $5.99 per month automatically begins upon subscription.
