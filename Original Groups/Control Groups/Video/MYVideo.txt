App Description
-Simple and easy to use
Track editing, support multiple video ratios, switch at will, easy to learn.
The whole process of clip splitting, variable speed, reverse playback, stickers, title subtitles, soundtracks and dubbing is complete, rich and powerful; leave a wonderful moment.
Editing and splitting Quickly and freely split the video, cut and adjust the video length at will.
Supports multiple format video and picture mixing, accurate to frame, efficient trimming,
Variable speed, 0.2 times to 4 times, free control of rhythm.
Play backwards, back in time, feel different videos, add some fun to your videos
When editing, arbitrarily modify the production ratio of the video to allow more freedom of production and inspiration at the touch of a finger

-Pretty good looking, can't stop-
One-click skin and beauty, professional style filters, rich special effects, dazzling special effects transitions, and videos instantly become big.
Picture color adjustment You can choose a variety of picture adjustments to highlight the personalized color of the video
Filter A variety of advanced professional style filters to make the video no longer monotonous
Beauty In the video editing, intelligently recognize the face shape, skin and beauty, adjust the exclusive beauty program
Transitions Support various types of transition effects such as basic, 3D, special effects, etc.


-Massive template creation classic-
Free template material, bursting with wonderful ideas. Add videos or pictures and generate interesting short videos with one click.
Summer party, eclipse diary, weekend time, enjoy travel, a large number of themes for you to choose, easily become a video dad.

-Video picture-in-picture powerful multi-track mixed editing-
Use the picture-in-picture track to superimpose multiple layers of video clips, freely use your imagination, so that your creativity is not constrained
The dull video material will be mixed and created to become an eye-catching masterpiece.
