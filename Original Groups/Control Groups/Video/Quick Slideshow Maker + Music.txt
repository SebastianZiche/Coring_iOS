App Promotional Text

App Description
It's so quick and easy to use! Do not waste your time making a slideshow when you can do it in just a few seconds.

Step 1. Add Photos
Step 2. Add Music
Step 3. Export Video To Gallery

It's that simple! Now you can share your slideshow with your friends and family on Facebook, Instagram, Youtube and more. Perfect for your special days like birthdays, weddings, anniversaries, graduations, etc.

Features:
► Add as many photos as you want
► Apply your favourite photo effects
► Add specialized texts and drawings
► Add multiple songs from our own library or yours
► Animated transitions between photos
► Auto-sync music with your slideshow
► Customizable photo and transition durations
