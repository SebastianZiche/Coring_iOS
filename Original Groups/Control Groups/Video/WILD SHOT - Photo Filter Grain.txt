App Description
Edit your photos with 15+ retro effects and professional color looks.

− More than 200 filters are available for editing in this app.

− Apply Film Burn, Film Grain, ...

− Add a subtitle to your image as you know it from old movies. This subtitle can be placed at the bottom of the image.

All Free Features:
− Professional filters
− Film grain
− Filmburn/ Light leaks
− Retro dust & scratches effects
− Date stamp
− Chromatic aberration
− Vintage subtitle text
− Vintage frames
− Brightness, Contrast, Shadows or Tint settings
− Motion blur effect
− Draw glitter or sparkle texture

Most functionality is free to use. If you upgrade to a premium account, you unlock:
− Use more than 200 presets in 20+ filter packs
− The filters are constantly updated and improved

These features are available through an In-App purchase:
Start filtering today and use all pro features for one week completely free of charge.
