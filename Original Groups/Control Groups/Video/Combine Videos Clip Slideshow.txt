App Description
Combine multiple clips together into one movie with Combine Videos!

First add videos from your library.

Then take advantage of the simple and easy to use editing features.

Crop your video projects just how you want them.

Quickly trim out unwanted parts from your video.

Add background music from your own music library.

Share with your friends on Instagram and YouTube.

Subscription pricing and terms

Combine Videos offers three auto-renewing subscription options:

$3.99 per week
$12.99 per month
$49.99 per year

These prices are for United States customers. Pricing in other countries may vary and actual charges may be converted to your local currency depending on the country of residence.

Your Combine Videos subscription will automatically renew at the end of each term and your credit card will be charged through your iTunes account. You can turn off auto-renew at any time from your iTunes account settings but refunds will not be provided for any unused portion of the term.
