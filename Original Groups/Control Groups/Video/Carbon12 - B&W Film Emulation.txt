App Description
Give your movies and photos a timeless black-and-white look with Monotone – our new app that's designed to make your images and videos stand out!

With over 300 B&W filters to choose from, including duotones and vintage presets, you can easily transform any image or video into a stunning black-and-white masterpiece. And with our advanced adjustment options, including curves, vignettes, and overlays, you can fine-tune your results to perfection.

Monotone is packed with features to help you get the most out of your black-and-white creations, including HSL sliders for B&W conversion and Photoshop's Calculations. You can also download presets with ease by scanning the QR codes provided.

But that's not all – our app also adds film grain for both images and videos, giving your creations an authentic, film-like feel. Plus, our advanced vignetting options, including Classic and Natural, let you add a unique touch to your images and videos.

Monotone comes with a variety of included analog film emulations, such as Agfa APX 25, Ilford Delta 400, and Kodak TRI-X 400, to name a few. With so many options to choose from, you're sure to find the perfect look for your black-and-white creations.

Download Monotone today and start creating timeless, unforgettable images and videos!
