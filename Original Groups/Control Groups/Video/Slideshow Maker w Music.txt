App Description
Create beautiful musical slideshow from photos & share on social media with just few taps. There are lots of music and theme to choose from.

A brief about the Tools are given below

Theme
- select a theme of your choice

Media
- hold and drag to re-order photos
- add caption to your slides
- crop and adjust photo frame
- delete a photo or add more photos

Music
- add your own music
- add music from our premium sound tracks
- use record to add voice over
- hold and drag the wave to adjust the starting position of your music

Speed
- adjust speed of your slideshow from four presets
- total duration of your slideshow will be adjusted with the speed preset

Ratio
- select a ratio for your slideshow output
