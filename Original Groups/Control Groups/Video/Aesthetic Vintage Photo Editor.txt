App Description
Make aesthetic edits to your photos with Aesthetic Photo Editor. Every filter and effect in Aesthetic is hand-crafted for you to create beautiful content.

FILTERS & PRESETS
Take your photo editing to the next level with Aesthetic presets. Easily add the perfect filter from presets made by our team, there is always the perfect filter for your picture. You can easily import and edit your photos from your gallery. Don’t forget to check our disposable filters, VHS filters, polaroid templates and retro effects to make your picture look like it’s taken in 90s.

PHOTO EDITOR
Enhance your photos with powerful photo editing tools. Use editing tools like Saturation and Contrast to make your photos look amazing or use Grain and Fade for an effortless film look. Recreate vintage film looks with VHS effects. Don’t forget to save and recreate your favorite edits. Aesthetic Photo Editor will help you to create outstanding posts with most useful photo editing tools.

VINTAGE CAMERA & FILTER
Aesthetic vintage camera is inspired by the retro film camera of the 90s. Aesthetic vintage camera has a disposable camera with a date stamp that turns your photos and videos into analog film with retro look. Create beautiful 90s retro-looking or grainy images with vintage touch.

Tag us in your photos with @aestheticfiltersapp and #aestheticfilters for a chance to be featured. We can’t wait to see what you create.

Aesthetic will launch new filters and effects from time to time, let’s look forward to it. We are working on new 3D animations which will turn your photos into 3D art by adding depth. Every week we are adding new VHS, polaroid, grain, 90s, disposable and retro effects to Aesthetic app to keep our content fresh.

For any questions, please send an email to xlabsapps@gmail.com
