App Description
MagicFX is a video editing app including lots of cool magic visual effects. Taking a magic video was never be so easy. We tuned every step visually and operationally for you.

HOW TOU USE
- Select your favorite video effect from exclusive library
- Shoot video by following our guide
- Save and share with your friends

MAGIC EFFECTS
- A huge variety of magic video effects are ready to use such as Energy Ball, Magic Shield, Fire, Thunder, Helicopters, Flying Cards…
- We add new filters and effects to MagicFX every week to keep our content fresh.

CARTOON CHARACTER
- Create comics, cartoon characters, sketches, artful oil paintings or pencil drawings from your photos just in a few seconds.
- Post them to social media platforms and set as your special head portrait to gain lots of comments!

AGING EFFECTS
- Check out the magic aging effect of your face
- Natural face aging effect

FINE-TUNE YOUR PICTURES
- After applying magic effects, enhance the results using image enhancement tools.
- Try out photo tools like exposure, sharpness, contrast, brightness etc.
