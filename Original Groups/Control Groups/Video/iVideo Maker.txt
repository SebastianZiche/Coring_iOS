App Description
“iVideo Maker” Application creates creative movie with your memories and tell your story. You don’t need to be an expert photo or video editor, It’s to easy to user for sweet memories creation! Just Create videos using photos from your device's Gallery.

You can also make funny videos with any sequential photos from your phone. Choose from your emotional, funny, sleek, romantic photos, add a song, and you’re done! and can Share via email, Twitter, or Facebook with your friends and family.

Multiple Video Effects VIDEO EFFECTS

Select your movie editor style like Rotate images and Fade In/Out Style with your beautiful images and can create video clips from your photos.

Music choose from gallery

Select any photos and Audio from your Mobile and can create lovely and amazing videos through “iVideo Maker” application.

SHARE VIDEOS

Create a special moment video and Share on Facebook, Instagram, Google+, Twitter, Email and Whatsapp.
