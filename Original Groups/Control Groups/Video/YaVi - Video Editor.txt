App Description
CREATE PROFESSIONAL VIDEOS IN MINUTES
- Apply effects, filters and transitions
- Use text and emoji
- Add music, sounds and voiceovers

With YaVi you can easily transform your videos into professional-looking movies. Simply choose videos from your media library, pick your favorite moments and make them perfect by adding filters, music and more. Whether you want to save your memories, become popular on Instagram or just have fun editing videos, you’ll love YaVi for its intuitive but powerful editing tools.

EDIT VIDEOS LIKE A PRO
- Add any number of layers
- Adjust brightness, saturation, contrast, temperature and more
- Trim, split, duplicate, and rotate objects
- Add beautiful audio tracks from our library to your video projects

CREATE THE RIGHT MOOD
- Apply your favorite filters
- Add music according to the style of your video
- Add text or emoji

CHOOSE THE BEST MUSIC OR SOUND EFFECT
- Filter our sound and music library
- Start your video with fade in and use fade out at the end
