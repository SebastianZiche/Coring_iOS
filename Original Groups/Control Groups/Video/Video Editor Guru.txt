App Description
Video Editor Guru is an easy & useful photo video editor app. It has powerful Photo Editor and Video Editor features. It helps you create awesome videos with ease and edit all types of video.

It is also a photo editor. Edit pictures and selfies, change aspect ratio and background, add text and stickers.

Features:
- Add music, sound effects to videos and adjust volume.
- Reverse or play video back to forth by one click.
- Apply customised video filters and effects to videos.
- Control video speed. Make Fast/Slow motion videos.
- Add Text on video with many fonts & styles and text editing tools.
- Apply animated stickers to videos.
- Easily share video to social media.
-There's no watermark and Banner Ads on Editing Page.

Download and enjoy.
