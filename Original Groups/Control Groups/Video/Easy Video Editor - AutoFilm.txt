App Description
Easy to use, simple and powerful way to create great videos. Turn your best photos and videos into a professional composition in minutes!

This video editing app comes with a wide range of editing tools to mix photos and videos to create professional video clips that you can share with family and friends.

• Combine photos and videos with ease
• Add stickers, text, GIFs and filters
• Built-in music and audio effects
• Record in-app live audio
• Create transitions between photos and videos
• Set a background color
• Choose aspect ratio
• Share with friends and family or save movies to your iPhone gallery
