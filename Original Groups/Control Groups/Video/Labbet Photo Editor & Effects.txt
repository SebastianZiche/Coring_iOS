App Description
"Filters and effects made for aesthetic storytellers around the world. Inspired by timeless style and the most popular trendsetters."

LABS - ONE CLICK. DONE.
We have carefully created a wide range of presets that you will love! Minimal, moody, vintage & BW - influenced by popular trendsetters around the world.

EXCLUSIVE FILTERS - WITH BEAUTIFUL AESTHETICS
We have worked hard to create photo filters you won’t find anywhere else. It will transform your photos with just one click and give them a classic or trendy look. Just like how the best creators do their edits. Labbet includes all the filters you need for an aesthetic look - minimal, moody, timeless, vintage, film, black & white.

TOOLS - ADJUSTMENTS WITH EASE
With our adjustment tools, you can easily correct your photos to your liking. Fine-tune the brightness, contrast, temperature, highlights, shadows, sharpening, skin tones, cropping, alignment, saturation, vibrance, tint & HSL

EFFECTS - FOR A REALISTIC LOOK
Go beyond the ordinary look of your photos and get that trendy look with the market's best Film grain, our unique Film blur, multi-option Motion Blur, the super trendy Vintage Glow effect, Un-Focus for an imperfect look, Low Pixels and Chromatic that will give your images a next-level look. It’s all in your hands to create the look you like and show your identity to your followers.

TEXT TOOL - WITH PREMIUM FONT
The Labbet Text tool feature is unique. We have created advanced layers that integrate the text into the photo to give it that sophisticated premium look. At the same time, the text gives your photos a minimalistic modern vibe. The text is editable so be creative and write what you want.

LABBET FILTERS

NO EDIT EDIT FILTER COLLECTION
Subtle filters that give your photos an instant trendy look with fine adjustments of the colors in your photo. Super all round and works on pretty much any photo in your camera roll.

BRIGHT FILTER COLLECTION
Created to give your photos a brighter look. With the muted colors and a slightly blue-ish tone, this filter brings you a sophisticated artistic look.

CHOCO FILTER COLLECTION
This is all about earthy neutral tones. You just must try 'em!

MOODY FILTER COLLECTION
Low-key moody looks are very trendy and we have nailed them for you!

WARM FILTER COLLECTION
Who likes warm and bright tones. Everyone! Right?

VINTAGE FILTER COLLECTION
The vintage look will always be trendy and our collection won’t disappoint you. We bring you a carefully designed collection of matte edits, airy edits, contrast-rich edits and the highly popular ”disposable film” look.

FILM FILTER COLLECTION
We can't get enough of film looks! Inspired by classic film stock we have created a brand new collection of filters suitable for all your photos. Combine with our super authentic Film Grain effect and your digital photos will get an analog classic look.

BLACK & WHITE FILTER COLLECTION
We love the classic look that B&W filters offer. We offer a collection of contrast-rich, matte, warm and all-round B&W filters. Combined with our Grain tool you can create some really amazing photo edits.

LabbetPlus
With LabbetPlus membership you get access to all filters, tools & effects. LabbetPlus is offered as a Monthly Plan or Yearly Plan.
