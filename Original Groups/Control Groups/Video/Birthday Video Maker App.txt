App Description
Just select several pictures, Birthday Video Maker will recreate a cool video in seconds for you. You can DIY the video in your own style, by picking animation mode you like best, editing your video with frames, sticker, text etc.

Birthday Video Maker is the most popular app for video with many amazing filters that make your birthday memorable!
It's can easily create videos with your own photos, add your favorite music track, write text or name, apply various frames and add stickers.

Birthday events are very important in our life which comes only once in a year. It is very important to celebrate with our family and friends. That’s why we have introduce you a birthday video maker app. Using Birthday wishes app you can decorate your video with more birthday stickers, songs and frames.

Share creative memories with Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, Whatsapp, Lion, Wechat, Weibo...

This app is the best Birthday video maker and video editor app. If you have any problems or suggestions, feel free to let us know. Email: info@devkrushna.com.
