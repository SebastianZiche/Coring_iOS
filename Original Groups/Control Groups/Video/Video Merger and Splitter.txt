App Description
Split and Merge your videos.

This app allows you to merge your clips into one video.
Also this app can split your video to multiple small clips with any duration.
You can add background music, crop, trim, add texts and video effects.
Save your competed video/videos or share with friends
Easy, fast and user friendly UI.
