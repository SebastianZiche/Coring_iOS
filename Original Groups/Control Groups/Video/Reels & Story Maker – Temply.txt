App Description
Temply is a video editing app, tailored for creating short-form content, particularly designed for social media platforms. It provides easy and straightforward solutions for creators seeking the trendiest aesthetics.

Create your dream video with wide selection of 600+ templates, 600+ royalty free music soundtracks, 100+ stunning fonts, 50+ eye catching filters and 800+ trendy stickers, whether you're a content creator, business owner, marketing professional and even a personal user - Temply is your one stop shop to your inspiration and efficient content creation.

Unlock your creativity with Temply – the ultimate template driven app that brings your ideas to life effortlessly! where cutting-edge AI meets creative expression! Unleash your imagination whether you're designing social media Reels, Stories, TikToks or more. Our pre-made templates make it a breeze to create stunning content in minutes, designing captivating visuals for any occasion has never been so intelligent and intuitive.

Inspiring templates:
Temply offers a diverse content genres and styles for creating captivating content across social platforms like Reels, TikTok and more.

Uplift your content:
Temply adds that final aesthetic touch, making your edits much more engaging effortlessly with fonts, transitions, animations and high-quality results.

AI-powered 'Created For You' feature:
Quickly generates engaging, trendy videos using your personal media in a few seconds.

User-friendly interface:
Ideal for newcomers in the world of TikTok and creating Reels, Temply boasts a quick learning curve, making it simple to get started.

Brand-focused content:
It allows personalization with logos, fonts, and colors, along with a diverse library of royalty-free music for impactful content aligned with your brand's essence.

All video editing features needed and beyond:
• A vast selection of pre-made templates: Beat-synced templates, Reels and other social media pre-made templates.
• Created for you – Personalized videos created by AI.
• Transform live photos to videos.
• Stock footage library with unlimited stock footage.
• Trim and mix music and videos with ease.
• Slow down or speed up capabilities for media.
• AI background remover.
• Unique 50+ filters and adjustments.
• Royalty free music soundtracks for social media and commercial usage.
• Import from your own iTunes collection.
• Text edit with 100+ fonts, shadows, strokes, color picking and more.
• Multi language automatic Auto Captions.
• Trendy 800+ sticker collection.
• Variety of advanced transitions and dynamic effects.
• High quality 4K export.
• Share right to your social media, friend and more.

Business Features:
• Business commercial and promotion templates.
• Import your brand logo, font, colors and more.
