App Promotional Text
Cut/Delete unwanted parts of a video. Trim your video Clips with accurate frames. Split movie clips into two and save as smaller parts. Import via Wifi and other Clouds.
App Description
Cut, Trim, Split Video Editor was created to help you cut, trim and split your videos easily.


Features:

- Trim and cut your videos

- Splitting large video into two small clips

- Import video from iCloud or other file sharing service

- Import video via Wifi

- Save video to your camera roll

- Multiple Video type Support

- Easy to use UI

- Fast Sharing

Supported File Type :

- MOV
- MP4
- MPEG
- MPEG-2
- AVI
- MPEG-4
- 3GPP
