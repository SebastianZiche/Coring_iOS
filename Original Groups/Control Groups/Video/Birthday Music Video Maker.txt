App Description
Key Features:-

- Photo Editing
- Video Editing
- Photo Collage
- Photo Slideshow
- Add music to videos
- Add music to slideshow


This app helps you to create beautiful birthday portrait, birthday slideshow with music and birthday video with advance editing features.

This app also provide different type of photo and video overlay collection like video filters, fx light and frames and effects like beautify, brightness, contrast, saturation, hue and blur.

Features:-
• Trending photo transition effects
• 150+ grid layouts to choose from for photo collage
• 100+ background images, patterns and color for slideshow
• Export video with different layouts like square, portrait and landscape
• Remove unwanted part from video with video trim
• Adjust video layout ration with video crop
• Add music over slideshow and video from birthday music and music gallery
• Add text, caption and sticker over photo and video
• Sort photos with drag and drop effects for slideshow
• Easy to use and share photos, videos and stories

Now give life to your photos and create awesome photos, videos and stories from photos with Birthday Music Video Maker!!
