App Description
Want your video to be creative and unique to amaze your friends?
Wanna make your Instagram page memorable and charming?
But it’s not always easy to capture the exact look you want while shooting your footage. Just download REEL to easily make your effect video anytime anywhere. Change video to art with simple video editing app that uses next-level real-time technology!

You will find making masterpieces has never been easier than now. No matter what you are, celebrity on media space or casual user, various video effects are awaiting your discovery. Motion, clone, cartoon, sketch, glitch and many other effects is very simple to use, just real-time apply and watch the result. REEL makes it easy to edit your video as you wish!

Features:

- Add the creative effect on your video, to make your video worth shareable among your friends
- Get each time a new unique result of your creativity with a unique technology for adding effects in real time
- Making a video trends can never be so easy. Quickly and easily make high-quality effects videos right during playback
- Animated huge library of effects to make your video vivid
- Share the your own aesthetic video masterpiece for Instagram Facebook YouTube WhatsApp Twitter and impress everyone!
