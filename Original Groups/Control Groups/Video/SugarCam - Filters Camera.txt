App Description
SugarCam is a practical camera and image editor. Super many filters and stickers, easy to create the perfect picture.

[Beautiful filter]
Versatile style texture filter improves the quality of photos, whether it's self portrait, food or scenery, perfectly presents every photo in your life.

[Beautification tool]
Simple and practical operation, cutting, stickers, filters, text functions, creative play to explore their own, so that your photos are no longer monotonous!

[Practical Jigsaw]
Life is more than a moment, photos are more than a grid. According to the selected photos, various templates are automatically generated to help you quickly organize complex pictures.
