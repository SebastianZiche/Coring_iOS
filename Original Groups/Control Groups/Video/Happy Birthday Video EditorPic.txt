Happy Birthday Video EditorPic
You can easily Create Videos with your own photos, add birthday music track, add various birthday and cake frames, apply filter effects.
Save video to library or share on social media.

Its easy to Create Birthday Videos:
- Select Birthday or cake fames.
- Select photos from gallery.
- Add birthday songs from selected Birthday collection.
- Save and share on social media like Facebook, Twitter, WhatsApp, Instagram and many more social media apps.
