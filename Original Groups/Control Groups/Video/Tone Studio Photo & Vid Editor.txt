App Description
The Tone Studio photo and video editing app is designed to bring you all the tools you need to create beautiful content, all in one easy-to-use app.

Designed by creators, for creators, transform your photo and video content in seconds using our variety of editing tools, including:

Presets
Edit in one click using our wide selection of 80 unique filters which include various styles such as film-inspired, moody, vintage, bright and colourful, minimal, black and white, and more.

Overlays
Create artistic effects with a choice of over 15 unique overlays.

Frames
Over 30 film-inspired frames.

Light Leaks
Get creative with our choice of 12 realistic light leaks.

Adjustments
Perfect your edit using our 13 professional adjustment tools, including HSL, shadows, grain, and more.

Fonts and text templates
10 text templates and 37 unique fonts to add artistic titles to your content.


Subscription pricing and terms

Tone Studio offers two auto-renewing subscription options:

$4.99 USD per month or
$29.99 USD per year silver subscription that unlocks all fonts, presets and overlays

$6.99 USD per month or
$41.99 USD per year gold subscription that unlocks all fonts, presets and overlays and Instagram feed planner

Your Tone subscription will automatically renew 24 hours before the end of the current period. You will be charged through your iTunes account. You can turn off your subscription at any time from your iTunes account settings. You will not receive refunds for the unused portion of your term.
