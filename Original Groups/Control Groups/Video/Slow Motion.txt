App Description
Play your videos in slow motion!
Slow Motion Video is the quickest and easiest way to edit the speed of your videos.
Choose from your library or record a new one with the camera.
Slide the dial to adjust the speed.


Pick which moments you want slowed down with Slow Motion Video Pro.
Unlock fast mode to make your videos play faster.
Make professional looking videos and clips.
Share your Slow Motion Video creation on Instagram.

Slow Motion Video Editor offers three auto-renewing subscription options:

$3.99 per week
$8.99 per month
$69.99 per year

These prices are for United States customers. Pricing in other countries may vary and actual charges may be converted to your local currency depending on the country of residence.

Your Slow Motion Video subscription will automatically renew at the end of each term and your credit card will be charged through your iTunes account. You can turn off auto-renew at any time from your iTunes account settings but refunds will not be provided for any unused portion of the term.
