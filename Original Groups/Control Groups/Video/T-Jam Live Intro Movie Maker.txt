App Description
Jam now to create awesome Movie Intros.
Thats right! Jam them!

Everything is prepared. Now its up to you. Tickle the screen! Loads of gestures, actions, swipes, wooshes, twists & shakes are just a tip away to give them all what they want. A time! Creating professional intros has never been more fun. Explore the many ready-to-go-actions and the powerful Layouter, which gives you a well selected stack of design-tools.

Besides the enjoyable making, the final output movie is superior and truly professional. Export your intros in HD-quality and use them in iMovie, other video editing apps, or upload them to get feedback from your friends.

DOWNLOAD now, import your favourite music and go for a creative ride!
