App Description
Create impressive intros, inserts, announcements and film credits for any movie project, right from your iPhone!
Intro includes a stunning a huge library of customizable templates to make you a movie director in minutes!

-Preloaded with thousands gorgeous and highly customizable HD templates
-1000+ video template
-700+ amazing motion stickers
-Add creative transitions to each text titles and set the start time and duration
-Add unlimited titles
-Adjust each title individually to your liking
-Edit the size, stroke even shadow of each title
-Add sound tracks or create your own voice overs
-Save your intro to the Camera Roll in just a few taps!
