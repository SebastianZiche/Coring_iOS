App Description
Your fandom. Your Passion. It’s on.

Cineverse is a free ad-supported streaming service that offers limitless access to the best movies and TV shows from around the world. To satisfy any content craving. Mood. Budget. Fandom. Or Lifestyle.

With more ways to watch, you can explore on demand or tune in live to explore a world of entertainment. And discover the stories that matter – no matter your passion.
