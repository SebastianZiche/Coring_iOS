App Description
Lightweight and easy way to find movies and TV shows , tv time and nearby theatres for watching movies and TV shows tonight.

-MOVIE / TV SHOW
Find and browse today's movies and TV shows in seconds.
A big list of movies of various genres.

-SEARCH
Save your time with beautiful search Ul design.

-SAVE/ FAVORIE LISTE
Organize your favorite movies and TV shows in one place.

-TV TIME
Keep track of your favorite shows by knowing when and where it will be aired on, so you won't miss it

-NEARBY THEATERS
Find all theartes around you location.

-VIDEO PLAYER
Play your favorite MP4 links easily in our player

-SIRI SHORTCUT
Add a shortcut to siri and lunch cuev app using a siri voice commande of your choice

Note:
* This app is not suitable for streaming or watching movies. This app will help you find movies and TV shows easily.
