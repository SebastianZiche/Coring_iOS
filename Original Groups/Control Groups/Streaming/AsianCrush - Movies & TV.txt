App Description
AsianCrush brings the best of pan-Asian entertainment to the West, bridging language and cultural gaps to broaden the horizons of North American audiences beyond Hollywood. With a streaming library encompassing everything from award-winning films & blockbuster hits to iconic TV dramas & anime, AsianCrush is curated for the rapidly growing fandom of Asian media & pop culture.
