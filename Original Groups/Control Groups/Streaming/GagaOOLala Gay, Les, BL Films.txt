App Description
Gay, lesbian, transgender, bisexual, and BL… Welcome to GagaOOLala, the global LGBTQ + BL streaming platform from Asia.
Log into GagaOOLala at home anywhere you like and gain access to all of the world’s Queer & BL content.

-----
【Features】

• The comprehensive and diverse LGBTQ+ & BL library, with thousands of feature films, shorts, documentaries, series, and exclusive originals, including romance, comedy, erotic to horror.

• New titles every week. In addition to GagaOOLala exclusive originals, you'll find the best queer and BL content from Taiwan, Japan, Korea, Thailand, Europe, and the US, including fan-favorite titles Fragrance of the First Flower, My Beautiful Man, Light, Semantic Error, and a lot more.

• Available on computers, TV, smartphones, and tablets. Watch anywhere and anytime you want.

• Sitewide Chinese and English subtitles available with Thai, Indonesian, Spanish, Japanese, French and more coming.

• It doesn’t matter who you love. We are the same. Support original LGBTQ+ and BL content creation. All titles on GagaOOLala are officially authorized.

*Due to licensing restrictions some titles might not be available in certain territories.
-----
【Subscription Plan】
GagaOOLala is a monthly subscription-based service. Subscribed members have full access to all of the available content on the platform.

• Register and access our free section or pay a monthly fee and become a VIP member with full access to a vast catalog of BL series, gay, lesbian, and other diverse queer content.

• Simple payment. No contracts. Cancel anytime.

• No ads. HD video quality. Seamless streaming.

-----
【App Store payment】

• Payments will be deducted from your iOS App Store account on a monthly basis, members can cancel anytime at the App Store account.

• Payments will be deducted from your iOS App Store account 72 hours ahead of the expiration date.

• Should you wish to cancel the subscription, please do so at least 72 hours in advance of your next renewal date and confirm if the automatic subscription renewal on iOS App Store is turned off. Your subscription will continue to work until the last day of the final subscription period. Once the payment has been processed you will not be able to get a refund.
