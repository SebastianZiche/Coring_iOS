App Description
Never miss a TV show again with Sofa Time - The Ultimate App For Tracking TV Shows And Movies For Trakt!

"The Sofa Time iOS app is perfect for keeping track of everything you’ve watched" - iGeeksBlog.com

"Everything you need in one gorgeous app" - iDownloadBlog.com

"A Full-Featured Movie and TV Tracker" - appadvice.com

With Sofa Time (formerly known as TVSofa), you can easily keep track of what you're watching, discover new shows to watch, and stay up-to-date on the latest releases.

------

Key features:

· DISCOVER: Explore new TV shows and movies based on genre, popularity, and more.

· TRACK: Keep track of which episodes you've watched and when.

· STREAMING GUIDE: Find out where to watch movies or TV shows online across over than 500 streaming services worldwide including Apple TV+, Netflix, Hulu, Prime Video, Disney+, HBO, and more.

· WATCHLIST: Create a watchlist or personal lists of TV shows and movies you want to watch next.

· REMINDERS: Don't forget anymore when your favorite TV show is airing again.

· SOCIAL: Read what other people like you think about other movies or TV shows.

· RECOMMENDATIONS: Get personalized TV shows and movies recommendations based on your viewing history

· WIDGETS: Add our exclusive Widgets to your iPhone or iPad to stay up-to-date.

· SYNC: Keep everything synced across your devices thankfully to Trakt.tv

Whether you're a binge-watcher or a casual viewer, Sofa Time has everything you need to stay on top of your TV show and movie collection.

Download it today and start your watchlist!

Please note: You cannot watch series or movies with Sofa Time
