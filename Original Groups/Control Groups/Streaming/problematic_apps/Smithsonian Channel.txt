App App Store Notes
We’re rather partial to our planet, and not just because it’s the only one we’ve got. Smithsonian Earth takes us to corners of the world we didn’t know existed, with stunning documentaries and short films about exotic animals, fragile ecosystems, and much more. We’re continually delighted by the blend of astounding filmmaking and deep knowledge, inspired by Smithsonian’s renowned museums.
App Description
Enjoy award-winning programming exclusively from Smithsonian Channel, available anywhere, anytime and on your favorite devices. Stream original series and thought-provoking documentaries inspired by the Smithsonian Institution's museums, research facilities and publications, and explore history, mystery, science, pop culture and more. Just download and enjoy at home or on-the-go.

Now available on Apple TV®, iPhone® and iPad®.

Watch full episodes from your favorite series the day after they air. Sign in with your TV or cable provider to unlock even more episodes and exclusive content, including:

• Air Disasters
• Aerial America
• Inside the Factory
• Orangutan Jungle School
• Air Warriors
• Combat Ships
• Ice Airport Alaska
• How Did They Build That?
• America in Color
• Fever Pitch
• WWII Battles in Color

APP FEATURES:

• Free award-winning programming exclusively from Smithsonian Channel
• Full episodes of your favorite programs, plus curated playlists, bonus materials and original digital shorts
• Entertaining and educational programs
• Factual, informative and trusted content
