App Description
"Excellent TV from the UK that you can't see anywhere else." - Vogue
Created by BBC and ITV, BritBox allows you to stream the biggest collection of British TV — all in one place.

Choose from a world-class library featuring an impressive variety of genres including mysteries, dramas, comedies, lifestyle, documentaries and more.
Start your free 7-day trial and stream ad-free across all your favorite devices.
With BritBox, you’ll get:
- Weekly premieres including new & exclusive series, so there's always something to watch
- Exclusive new seasons of fan-favorites like Vera, Shetland, Grace, Silent Witness and more
- Exciting Original series like Payback, The Cleaner, The Tower, Stonehouse, The Confessions of Frannie Langton, Karen Pirie, and Sister Boniface Mysteries
- The biggest collection of Agatha Christie anywhere, including BritBox's original adaptation of Why Didn't They Ask Evans?, plus full series of classics like Marple, Poirot and much more

Download the BritBox app and start streaming today!

Please note payment will be charged to your iTunes Account at confirmation of subscription.

Contact us at support@britbox.com if you are experiencing any issues or have feedback.
