App Description
You want more TV shows ?

Showflix is the best app to discover all the new trending TV Shows !
You can watch thousands of episodes via iTunes !
Find the best series.
Download for free!
See the season premieres schedule. Download subtitles. Chat with the fans community. Track the episodes you watched.

The BEST app to discover NEW shows to watch.
You look for subtitles or the tv program schedule ? You missed an episode ?

Features :

- Watch the new trailers
- Get the next episodes dates
- Watch complete seasons
- Download subtitles
- Be notified when a new season starts
- Buy and watch complete seasons
- Track the episodes you have already watched
- Get the secrets, trivia and spoilers
- Read the community comments and the ratings
