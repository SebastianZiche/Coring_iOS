App Description
FIND SERIES TO BINGE TOGETHER BY SWIPING & MATCHING

Binger is a fun way of finding TV-shows & Movies to watch together.
Swipe, Match & Binge together.

See the video at: https://binger.app/demo

PAIR UP
Within the app invite someone that you want to binge watch with.


SWIPE
Each card contains TV-shows and movies from your favorite Streaming Services.


MATCH
When you and your partner swipe right on a show, it becomes a match.
Your matches are saved on a list, so that you can always find them.


DISCOVER
Watch trailers and get information about new shows and movies.


TITLES
Titles from the largest streaming services are available, sort and filter by the services that you have.

Netflix, Amazon Video, Hulu, Apple TV+, HBO Max, Disney+

This does not include any streaming content.
