App Description
From classic titles to new streaming hits, get Korean dramas, movies, K-Pop, variety shows, and so much more with premium multi-language subtitles on this app across the Americas.

Explore the Extensive Library of Korean Dramas, Movies, and TV Shows
• Korean Dramas: Enjoy all-time favorites, Coffee Prince, The Heirs, Descendants of the Sun, The Weightlifting Fairy Kim Bok Joo, Hwarang, and more titles with your favorite Korean actors. And do not miss on the most-talked-about Korean Dramas, The Penthouse, Weak Hero Class 1, My Dearest, Young Lady and Gentleman, and The Real Has Come!
• Korean Movies: Discover the most popular Korean movies like Along with the Gods, Train to Busan, The Wailing, The Beauty Inside, A Taxi Driver, and more.
• Korean Variety Shows: Watch the fastest subtitled episodes of the biggest variety shows like Running Man, Home Alone, and The Manager, plus variety shows starring K-Pop artists like NCT Universe: LASTART, EXO's Travel the World on a Ladder in Geoje&Tongyeong, aespa's Synk Road, Holiday Staff: iKON's The DreamPing, and so much more.
• K-Pop: Indulge in special performances, debut stage performances, and comebacks from K-Pop idols like BTS, BLACKPINK, Stray Kids, SEVENTEEN, Twice, EXO, and NCT, on K-Pop music programs and talk shows.

KOCOWA+ Exclusives:
• AI incorporated search engine optimization that will help you find the exact content you’re looking for
• Programming that’s available just a few hours after airing in Korea
• An All-Access Pass to over 25,0000+ hours of Korean dramas, movies, variety shows, documentaries, K-Pop shows, and more
• Weekly real-time LIVE streaming features for Running Man Classic episodes and K-Pop performances
• The fastest, premium multi-language subtitles
• An endless collection of Korean dramas that you can download to watch anywhere, anytime
