App Description
Whether you are an Asian TV show binge-watcher or a drama movie enthusiast, you’ve found your new favorite app.

HeTV offers a wide range of Korean, Japanese, and Chinese dramas, allowing you to quickly choose a bingeworthy TV show or a wonderful movie to watch.

⊚ EXPLORE
We have handpicked a range of Asian movies and TV series for you to choose what to watch next.

⊚ GET TO KNOW MORE
A wealth of information on the movie or TV show, including seasons and episodes, where to watch it, reviews, videos, and much more...

⊚ LIBRARY
Keep an eye on your movies & TV shows by assigning them to a list in your library, such as: Watched, Watching, Planning to Watch or Dropped.

⊚ REMINDER
Turn on the reminder for any forthcoming movie that you don't want to miss, and you'll be alerted when it's available to watch.

⊚ NOTIFICATION CENTER
All the upcoming movies you are excited to watch can be found in one place after the official release, find them in your notification center.

⊚ CLEAN INTERFACE
HeTV app comes with a very simple and elegant interface design, as well as integrated gesture features that make its usability even easier.

⊚ APP ICONS
Numerous app icon designs to choose from, customize HeTV app icon and make it your preferred style.

More features are being developed and will be released in the near future; stay tuned.

If you have any questions or recommendations, please leave them in the reviews area.
