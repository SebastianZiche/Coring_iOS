App App Store Notes
Movies, shows, news, sports: you name it, DIRECTV NOW has it. It’s all the channels we already subscribe to—like ESPN, HBO, Nickelodeon—but available on the go. And a clean interface makes browsing stations a pleasure.
App Description
The DIRECTV App, for DIRECTV and DIRECTV STREAM customers, is the fresh way to stream all the entertainment you love.
Live or On Demand, at home or on the go, big screen or small - no matter how you like to watch, we bring your TV together.

No matter your passion, we’ve got you covered with live sports, breaking news and thousands of On Demand titles.
Binge hit movies & most talked about shows, and quickly find your favorite shows using our intuitive user interface.

Version 3.0.0 (Aug 19, 2019)
    Say hello to AT&T TV NOW! The DIRECTV NOW you know is getting a new name.

    • Same great features: Cloud DVR Beta, no annual contract & no installation.

    • Same programming and content.

    • Plus, HBO included in PLUS and MAX packages!

    There's more! The DIRECTV NOW app name will be updated to AT&T TV. To make things even easier, the AT&T TV and AT&T TV NOW experiences are accessed from the same app for on-the-go viewing, called AT&T TV.


    This change will keep branding consistent and ignite customer excitement around the next generation of AT&T TV products!


    Questions? Answers Here!

    Do existing DIRECTV NOW customers have to do anything to get this new experience?

    • Existing DIRECTV NOW customers won't need to take any action and their service will continue as usual without interruption.

    • Their application should automatically update upon release of the new name.

    • AT&T does recommend having their user name and password on hand in case of sign-in issues.

    • If automatic updates are not enabled on their device, they will be directed to download the updated app when they login and they simply follow the onscreen instructions.

    • They will be asked to re-consent to the Terms and Conditions and Privacy Policy upon opening or post updating their app.


    The new app is called AT&T TV. Is that the right app for customers?

    • Yes, to make things even easier, the AT&T TV and AT&T TV NOW experiences are accessed from the same app for on-the-go viewing, called AT&T TV.

    • The customer's login credentials will determine what content appears, and for current DIRECTV NOW customers, their app will automatically update to the new AT&T TV app.

    • If automatic updates are not enabled on their device, they will be directed to download the updated app when they login.


    What does the new AT&T TV app icon look like?

    • The new AT&T TV app icon is blue and features the AT&T globe with TV in white. AT&T TV App

    • It will automatically update for anyone who has the current DIRECTV NOW app downloaded.

    • If automatic updates are not enabled on their device, they will be directed to download the updated app when they login.


    Do customers need to change anything for their third-party apps, such as HBO GO?

    • There will be no changes to any network apps customers are signed into.

    • However, if they sign out, they will need to select AT&T TV as their TV provider the next time they sign back in.
