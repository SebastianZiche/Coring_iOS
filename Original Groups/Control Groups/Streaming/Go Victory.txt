App Description
Watch live and on-demand Christian television from the most trusted Bible teachers on Go VICTORY, a 24-hour streaming and on-demand Christian television network made possible by the generous Partners of Kenneth Copeland Ministries. Watch the channel live or use the Watch Now feature for on-demand viewing. Focused on teaching the uncompromised Word of God and how to apply it to everyday living, featured ministers include Kenneth Copeland, Gloria Copeland, George Pearsons, Terri Copeland Pearsons, Jesse Duplantis, Jerry Savelle, Creflo Dollar, Andrew Wommack, Keith Moore, Bill Winston, David and Nicole Crank, Keith Butler, Gary Keesee, Butch Bruton, Mac Hammond, Mark Hankins and more.
