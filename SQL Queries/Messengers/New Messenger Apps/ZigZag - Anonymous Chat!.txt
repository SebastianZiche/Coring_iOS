App Description
You are feeling lonely, bored? Want to talk to someone?
You know someone, you like someone and you want to chat anonymously or ask them anonymous questions.
You want to find conversation partners for language practice...
Let join ZigZag!
Features:
- 100% real people, there are no bots here.
- 100% anonymous, no avatar, no profile, no one can know who you are.
- Simple UI, easy to use.
- Share messages, pictures, everything you want.
- Users can select topics of interest.
- Ask anonymous questions or answer questions from your friends.
- Chat anonymously with your friends or someone you already know by email, username, or phone number.
- Find random strangers near you or around the world.
- Share your link on Facebook to receive anonymous messages and questions from your friends.
- Add friend to keep in touch.
