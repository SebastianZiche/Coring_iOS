App Description
You don’t need a translator by your side. From now on you will have it in your pocket.

Translate.com Platinum is a top-notch translation service that will help you facilitate everyday communication, translate texts, documents, interpret casual conversations and so much more.

OUTSTANDING APP FEATURES:

94 languages available for translation
40,000+ qualified experts ready to provide professional human translation services
innovative interpreter mode
unlimited text, photo and voice translations
simple and easy-to-navigate interface

TEXT TO TEXT TRANSLATIONS

Translate any text from 64 different languages within a second.
Keyboard or handwriting input are available for your convenience.

VOICE TO TEXT TRANSLATIONS

Find a common language no matter where you are. Casual conversations will be interpreted in a blink of an eye. Your real-time chit chat with a foreigner will be interpreted from 30 different languages.

PHOTO TO TEXT TRANSLATIONS

Image to text translations have never been faster and easier. Simply take a picture on your cell phone camera and this powerful app will instantly translate every single word on it. 17 languages available.

HUMAN TRANSLATIONS

Professional assistance of 40,000+ accomplished translators are at your disposal. In case you need an impeccable translation our team of dedicated experts will work on any type of content to provide outstanding results.

Languages supported by our app include:

Afrikaans, Albanian, Arabic, Armenian, Azerbaijani, Basque, Belarusian, Bengali, Bosnian (Latin), Bulgarian, Catalan, Cebuano, Chinese (Simplified), Chinese (Traditional), Croatian, Czech, Danish, Dutch, English, Esperanto, Estonian, Filipino, Finnish, French, Galician, Georgian, German, Greek, Gujarati, Haitian Creole, Hausa, Hebrew, Hindi, Hmong, Hungarian, Icelandic, Igbo, Indonesian, Irish, Italian, Japanese, Javanese, Kannada, Khmer, Korean, Lao, Latin, Latvian, Lithuanian, Macedonian, Malay, Maltese, Maori, Marathi, Mongolian, Nepali, Norwegian, Persian, Polish, Portuguese, Punjabi, Romanian, Russian, Serbian (Cyrillic), Slovak, Slovenian, Somali, Spanish, Swahili, Swedish, Tamil, Telugu, Thai, Turkish, Ukrainian, Urdu, Vietnamese, Welsh, Yiddish, Yoruba, Zulu, Hmong Daw, Klingon, Serbian (Latin).
