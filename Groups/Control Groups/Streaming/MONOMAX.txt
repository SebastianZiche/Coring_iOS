App Description
MONOMAX The only application that provides all entertainment for families with premium series, movies, and sports from all over the world for more than 20,000 hours.

• Enjoy worldwide series: Chinese series, Korean series, Western series, Indian series and Thai series
• Enjoy blockbuster and unique movies: Chinese movies, Hollywood movies, Korean movies, Thai movies, and Indian movies in action-adventure, Sci-fi-Fantasy, horror and comedy
• Offer cartoons and animation for children
• Watch sports content as if you were in the games
• One account could be used simultaneously up to 3 devices
• Ads-free experiences
• High resolution: Full HD
• Provide both Thai dubbing and soundtrack
