App Description
AMC+ is a premium streaming bundle featuring world-class originals, award-winning series and exclusive movies made just for you. Enjoy full access to AMC’s critically-acclaimed originals, the spine-tingling horror of Shudder, captivating dramas & mysteries from Sundance Now, and culture-defining movies from IFC Films Unlimited.

Thousands of hours of curated content, all in one place, with new shows and movies added every week.

AMC+ Exclusive Features:
The Ultimate Fan Destination for The Walking Dead Universe and Anne Rice’s Immortal Universe, with bonus episodes, collections, and specials not available anywhere else.
Dive into full seasons of critically-acclaimed series such as Mad Men, Dark Winds, Interview with The Vampire, Gangs of London, Orphan Black and more.
Full access to SHUDDER - the leading home for horror, thriller and supernatural entertainment including new spine-tingling movies, iconic scares, and exclusive fan-favorite franchises like Creepshow, V/H/S and The Last Drive-In with Joe Bob Briggs.
Discover the best suspenseful dramas and mysteries from around the world with SUNDANCE NOW, including A Discovery of Witches, Riviera, The Bureau, and the True Crime Story franchise.
Indulge in award-winning independent films, including festival favorites and star-studded indie darlings, with IFC FILMS UNLIMITED.
Access to popular live TV networks like AMC plus streaming-only exclusives, including The Walking Dead Channel.

AMC+ delivers more of what you want, less of everything else.

Start streaming AMC+ today!
