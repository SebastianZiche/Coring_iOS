App Description
OnDemandKorea - Korean Dramas, Movies, Variety Shows
Discover OnDemandKorea, a streaming platform offering diverse Korean content, from classics to trending shows.


[Features]
Watch for free: Sign up and download app to watch selected high quality shows on your devices with ease.
Live TV: Enjoy 24/7 Live TV for curated channels and a real-time broadcast.
STANDARD Plan: Watch our FREE content with fewer ads；watch premium content with no video ads. Subscribe to watch exclusive content.
PREMIUM/FAMILY Plan: Watch exclusive with no video ads. Enjoy premium benefits for the whole family, create up to 4 profiles and watch on 4 devices at the same time.
Multiple Devices: Enjoy your favorite shows on your phone, tablet, and Smart TV with playback progress synced.
Bilingual support: App language available in both Korean and English.


[Now Streaming]
[Hot Dramas] The Escape of The Seven, The Elegant Empire, My Lovely Boxer, Good Doctor, Love in The Moonlight, Cruel Temptation, Taxi Driver 2, Game of Witches, Weak Hero.
[Popular Variety Shows] Running Man, Divorced Singles, I Live Alone, I am Solo, Immortal Songs 2.
[Premium Movie] The Policemans Lineage, Extreme Job, The Berlin File, The Devil's Deal, Miracle.


[Content Partners]
Major Korean channels: KBS, MBC, SBS, EBS.
Top cable channels: JTBC, MBN, Channel A, TV Chosun, Yonhap News, YTN.
Collaborations with content production companies: ENA, iHQ, Tcast, Kakao M, ShowBox, CJMovies and ODK Original.


For question or concerns, email: help@ondemandkorea.com
For more content updates, follow us on:
Instagram: @ondemandkorea_official
Youtube: @stream-odk
