App Description
FilmRise is the largest independent provider of FREE movies and TV, delivering unlimited streaming access to over 10000 hours of content. You’ll find something for everybody; laugh-out-loud comedies, gripping dramas, family viewing, and genre favorites including British TV, Westerns, sitcoms, and more.

FilmRise is now proud to offer the complete groundbreaking horror series HEMLOCK GROVE, and the international TV sensation HANNIBAL starring Mads Mikkelsen and Hugh Dancy.

Enjoy popular television favorites like the beloved family drama HEARTLAND and the iconic British mysteries of MIDSOMER MURDERS. Ride along with THE RIFLEMAN, or investigate UNSOLVED MYSTERIES with Robert Stack. Tune in to all-new true crime series like WORLD’S MOST EVIL KILLERS and BLOODLINE DETECTIVES, or return for familiar favorites like THE DICK VAN DYKE SHOW and HIGHWAY TO HEAVEN.

Explore our collection of acclaimed movies – featuring legendary stars like Nicolas Cage, Nicole Kidman, Jason Statham, Samuel L. Jackson, Whoopi Goldberg, Hallie Berry, Daniel Craig, Russell Crowe, and many, many more!
All FilmRise movies and TV shows are available instantly. No subscription is needed. For more information and high-quality film and TV entertainment, please visit FilmRise.com.
