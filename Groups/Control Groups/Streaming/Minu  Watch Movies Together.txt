App Promotional Text
JOIN MOVIE WATCH PARTIES WITH OTHER PEOPLE OR FRIENDS. BECAUSE WATCHING TOGETHER IS MORE FUN.
App Description
Welcome to MINU, your all-in-one entertainment destination where you can seamlessly connect your favorite streaming accounts and enjoy group movie watch parties with friends or make new ones! Elevate your movie streaming experience by accessing a vast array of local and international TV channels, movies, dramas, and videos on the go.

KEY FEATURES:

◆ GROUP WATCH - MOVIE PARTY TOGETHER & GROUP VIDEO CALLS

Choose your preferred movie genres and dive into live-streaming watch parties effortlessly. Simply sign in to your streaming platform account and join with a tap. MINU supports popular streaming platforms like:

- Disney+

- Amazon Prime Video

- HBO Max

- YouTube

- Hulu, and more (additional platforms coming soon)

◆ GROUP VIDEO CHAT

Enhance your group watch with dynamic video calls. Share videos, follow interesting individuals, and engage in lively discussions. Express your thoughts on scenes, reactions, and memorable moments.

◆ DISCOVER ENGAGING CONTENT

Bid farewell to mundane movie nights. MINU ensures a dynamic group watch experience. Explore diverse content in watch rooms, complemented by YouTube integration for shared music streaming.

◆ USER-FRIENDLY INTERFACE


Effortlessly navigate MINU with user-friendly features:

- Choose your favorite TV show and movie genres.

- Search for movies, TV shows, and people by name.

- Join watch rooms for a collective viewing experience.

- Initiate group video calls with friends and other MINU users.

- Seamlessly connect accounts and share screens.

- Watch movies together while participating in room group chats.

- Easily share group chat links for a broader social experience.

- Explore and follow users for a personalized touch.

- Stay informed with notifications about new watch parties.


► Try Minu for FREE now and unite with others for an exciting movie social experience!
