App Description
TBN+, the streaming service from TBN, the world's leading faith and family network, has thousands of hours of Christian teaching, worship, shows, and movies all in one place. Your favorite Bible teachers, like Joel Osteen, Priscilla Shirer, Joyce Meyer, and Steven Furtick, and your favorite worship leaders and Christian artists like CeCe Winans, Michael W. Smith, and Jenn Johnson are all here. You'll even find current events with a Christian worldview with shows like Huckabee, Centerpoint, and Stakelbeck Tonight. Choose your TBN+ streaming experience from one of two subscription plans:

TBN+ (With Ads):
· Watch 10+ live channels
· Watch TBN broadcast content, including teachings, worship, original shows, and movies.

TBN+ Premium:
· Ad-free experience*
· Unlock additional faith-filled content, movies, and TBN+ Originals.
· Download content to view offline so you can watch wherever you want, whenever you want.

*Due to streaming rights, our live channels will still contain ads.
