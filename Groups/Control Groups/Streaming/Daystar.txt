App Description
The Daystar app is a new way to interact with the fastest-growing, faith-based network in the world! You can enjoy the Daystar live stream or keep up with your favorite videos with Daystar on Demand.

Features:

- Watch Daystar Television Live - anytime, anywhere!*
- Save a list of your favorite shows available on demand.
- View a weekly schedule of the live stream.
- Submit Prayer Requests, Praise Reports and more.
- Submit Videos / Pictures and maybe even be included in an upcoming program!
- Closed Captioning of live stream and selected shows available.

*Live stream is broadcast in US Central time
