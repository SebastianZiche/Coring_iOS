App Promotional Text
Watch your favorite Cooking Channel shows and more from up to 14 networks, including TLC, HGTV, Food Network and more! All in one app, free with your pay TV subscription.
App Description
Watch Cooking Channel anytime with access to live TV and full episodes of your favorite shows such as Man Fire Food, Carnival Eats, Food: Fact or Fiction and more. It’s FREE with your TV subscription. Just sign in with your TV provider username and password.

New episodes are added all the time.

With this app, you can:

WATCH TV THE WAY YOU WANT:
• Stream live Cooking Channel.
• New episodes of your favorites are available the same time they air on TV.
• Binge full seasons and episodes of your favorite Cooking Channel shows, including Carnival Eats, Man Fire Food, Cheap Eats and many, many more!
• Get early access to premieres before they air on TV.
• Not enough time to finish the video? Just simply select the video again when you’re ready, and you’ll be able to continue watching from where you stopped.

Closed captioning.
