App Description
Dark Matter TV is an A-VOD app / Fast Channel that is available in the US, Brazil, Mexico, Spain, and Italy. Founded in 2019 by TriCoast TV, the app features the best of Sci-Fi, Horror, and Action themed content. Expertly curated, DMTV’s content is obsession-worthy and includes both fan favorites and cult classics. Check our website for new trailers and updates: join us on the dark side!
