App Description
Fetchfruit : Movies And Series is your ultimate, easiest way to find and track good movies or Tv Shows, stay on top of trending content, all in one place.

Feautures :

- Find popular movies and TV shows.
- Get Theaters around your location.
- Get a list of production companies for any movie or tv show.
- Search for any movies & TV shows easily.
- Get details movies & TV show.
- Get similar movies and tv shows recommendations.
- Watch official movies & TV show trailers.
- List of populqr actors .
- No registration required.
