App Description
Hello! Welcome to OyeTalk- A social audio platform where users can interact in discussions rooms with a pool of like-minded people and show their talent through audio podcasts to gain appreciation. This platform also enables users to play games with their peers.



You can discuss different topics in live discussion rooms with people from all around the globe- From movie reviews to sports, cooking tips to counseling, fashion hacks and so much more!



On OyeTalk, you can virtually interact with people from all around the globe, get a chance to exhibit your talent and get international recognition. It’s a place where you can create happy memories with others through showing your talent. So connect, communicate and enjoy!



We can’t wait for you to join us!



* Available in 100+ Countries



True friendship knows no distance. So, enjoy with your friends no matter where they are! Broadcast your favorite music in rooms, sing karaoke together and play an array of games directly in rooms. Show your love by sending exciting animated gifts to your loved ones. On OyeTalk, you can hear everything new and beautiful. Listen to pieces of masterpieces of classical poetry, motivational talks, singing and a lot more.



Special and Unique Features:



Live Audio Chat Conference Rooms:

- Free live audio podcast.

- Show your talent through live singing, poetry etc.

- Expand your friend circle.

- Increase your fan base.

- Gain international recognition.

- Make friends from around the globe.

- Live free audio call with your friends.



Impressive Gifts

- Show your appreciation by sending virtual gifts to broadcasters.

- By sending gifts, you gain access to bonus levels and prizes.

- Send popular gifts to your friends to make them feel special.



Be Exclusive & special:

- Become VIP and show others your value by availing extra features.

- Get Exclusive entrances.



Share and Win:

- Share your favorite room and events on Facebook, WhatsApp, Twitter and Instagram with your friends and invite them to win popular and expensive gifts every day.
