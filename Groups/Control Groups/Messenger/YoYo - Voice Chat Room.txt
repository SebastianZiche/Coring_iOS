App Description
In YoYo, You can enjoy voice chat and endless interesting events with international people.

You can make new friends easily in YoYo. You can create your own room, share your favorite music, sing together with strangers from different cities or countries. Personal chat, voice chat, live chat are supported here.

Let's start the voice chatting party and find friends at YoYo voice chat room!

Main features:

【Voice Chat Room】
Enjoy free live voice chat with real sweet people.
Select rooms by country or topic.
Create your own room without any level limit.
Chat with strangers to expand your social network.

【Funny Activities】
Interesting activities would be held every week and every festival.
You can even join the activities when you are chatting.

【Family】
You can create a ‘Family’ and invite your friends and families to join with. More fun activities are waiting for you

【Private Chat】
Have fun conversations and start sending messages and pictures with your friends from all over the globe.

【Amazing Gifts And Magical Entrance Effects】
Send gifts to your new and closest friends to express your emotion when you are chatting.
Many unique and amazing gifts are offered exclusively at YoYo.
Cool entrance effects, everyone pays attention to you, a super rock star.

【Share and Follow】
Share your favorite rooms and moments on Whatsapp and more. Invite new friends and followers to enjoy YoYo voice chat room.

【Moment】
Funny pictures and jokes, Whatsapp status, short videos are more than welcome to share.

YoYo is the best app for your needs! Let’s enjoy YoYo together!

Download YoYo and start to share your happiness with the world NOW!
