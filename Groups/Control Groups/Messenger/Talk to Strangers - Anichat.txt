App Description
Talk to strangers: Instantly get connected to chat with a new real person and make new friends. Endless possibilities.

Only REAL persons! No bots! No fake accounts!

Try it and meet new people now - it's quickly, easy and fun!

Instant private chat:
- Select a new user to talk with instantly. Free and unlimited.
- Choose the gender of who you want to initiate a chat with (available for premium subscribers only).
- Private 1 on 1 chat, no other user could see what you share.

Only real persons:
- Talk with real people only. No fake accounts and no bots. Only real users!

Chat around the world:
- Our service is available worldwide so you could make friends across seas and limits.

Be whoever you want:
- You choose how others would see you, so be who you want to be. change your profile pic or your nickname through app settings.
- We don't share any details about you besides your nickname, your age and your profile pic.

Keep new friends close:
- Stay connected with new friends through the app, no need to share any personal details.
- You can mark users as favorite to manage your friends list easier.

Media sharing:
- Besides text we currently support sharing of images and videos.
- One time media share, all images and videos you share could be seen only once.

Screenshot notification:
- If the other side takes a screenshot of the chat you would be instantly notified.

Report & Block:
- You can always report a user that you believe violates any of the app policies and we would check it out ASAP.
- You can easily block any user so they would not be able to contact you again.

It's' a FREE download, easy and fun so check it out NOW!
