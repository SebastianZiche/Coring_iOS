App Description
Welcome! Join Binmo Party, Connect with the World!

Binmo chat provides you with a wonderful way to communicate with friends all over the world. You can have wonderful voice group chats in rooms and play interesting games with people from nearby or all over the world.

[ Voice Group Chat Rooms ]
Enjoy free high-quality live voice chat with friends and other friendly people from different regions in Binmo. Join thousands of topics Live in public chat rooms around the world.

[ Make New Friends ]
Binmo is now available in 70+ countries in localized languages. Make friends directly in group chat rooms.

[ Various Themed Parties ]
Parties for the national day, birthdays, weddings, or real-time comments on football games are waiting for you. Spending your wonderful days in Binmo chat. Let’s get the party started!

[ Various Topic Rooms Are Online ]
Newcomers welcome, singing competitions, talent shows, poems and stories reading, language learning, making friends, daily greetings...various topic rooms are waiting for you to join in.

[ Exquisite Gifts ]
Show your love and uniqueness with a variety of exquisite gifts, luxury sports cars, beautiful avatar frames, and other decorations in Binmo. It’s a great chance to surprise your friends.

[ Share Your Moments ]
Share every fantastic moment in your life with the square on Binmo chat, Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, or Snapchat.

Download Binmo & start your wonderful group chat journey now!
