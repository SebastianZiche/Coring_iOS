App Description
Welcome to voice chat parties Lama!

Lama is one of the most popular voice chat entertainment app dedicated to the users who enjoy party together.

You do not need to show your face in Lama, you can meet new friends, find interesting people, play games, do social chat, sing songs, together PK battle through your particular voice anytime anywhere and join Lama rooms freely. Different kinds of topics in voice group chat rooms and keep welcome you all day long, such as birthday party, movie room, wedding celebration room, daily gossip chatting room, singing competition room, beauty contest etc... surely you can find your soulmate or same-minded people who can become good friends and get rid of your loneliness from now on.


---------MAIN FEATURES---------

【Enjoy Chat Parties】
Find real sweet people to chat with 24 hours all day long, find friends, find soulmate easily. Say hello yo to strangers, no longer feel lonely. Say hello yo to all friends in room, only love the voice of strangers.


【Your own voice room】
Enjoy the voice chat in your own room and share your room with others, also you can create a private room.


【Awesome Gifts and vehicle】：
Stunning animated gifts updated every week which can be sent out to express your favourite and support. Besides, luxury vehicle, beautiful avatar frame and other decorations, privileges for you to choose.


【Various Room Topics】
Browse THOUSANDS live chat rooms from NEARBY or AROUND THE WORLD that cover thousands of topics.
You can meet all kinds of interesting people and parties here, such as Karaoke rooms, birthday parties, reading the Quran, talking about football together, poetry recitation competitions, play games room, movie youtube room，PK battle room, etc.


【Multiple Funny Activities】
Here are many funny activities online evey month, such as Ramadan Kareem Activity, EID MUBARAK Activity , New Year Countdown Party etc...


【Various Games】
Ludo game with classic and quick mode for all ludo game lovers to choice, no longer feel loneliness.


【Funny Emoji】
Magical and special emoji, bring more fun to your voice chat.


【24h Official Customer Services】
Official team keep listening to your feelings and suggestions 24 hours a day, and give you all kinds of support and gift packages.
