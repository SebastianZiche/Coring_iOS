App Description
Using Wink, you can send high-quality group, text, voice, video, document(.doc, .ppt, .zip, etc.), and picture messages anywhere in the world without SMS or MMS fees.

You can create groups so that you can chat in real time with all your friends at once, and share media or attachments all with complete privacy.

Wink is a mobile and desktop messaging app with a focus on speed and security. You can use Wink on all your devices at the same time, your messages sync seamlessly across any number of your phones, tablets or computers.

Wink is based on time-tested algorithms to make security compatible with high speed delivery and reliability on weak connections. It’s superfast, simple, secure and free, and support end-to-end encrypted voice calls. The server never has access to any of your communication and never stores any of your data.

Wink groups have up to 10,000 members and you can create channels to broadcast messages to an unlimited number of subscribers.

SUPPORT: For online support, please go to Settings in Wink and select “Ask a Question” to send a message directly to our support volunteers.
