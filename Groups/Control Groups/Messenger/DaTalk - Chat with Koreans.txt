App Description
DaTalk is an engaging chat app that allows people from all over the world to meet and interact with Koreans. When you start a conversation with someone, it marks the beginning of a new connection!

With this app, you can have deep conversations with matched Korean users. DaTalk offers private messaging, enabling one-on-one chats, photo sharing, and the opportunity to learn more about Korean culture. It's ideal for global users interested in K-pop, K-dramas, and various other aspects of Korean content.

Boasting over 18 million members, DaTalk serves as a community for those eager to engage with Koreans. Popular on the App Store, this app facilitates one-on-one conversations with Korean individuals.

DaTalk requires only a nickname and age, with no need for other personal information, ensuring privacy and anonymity. This system supports sincere and in-depth conversations. You can also express closeness by sending 'points' to people you find interesting.

If you're looking to start new interactions and conversations with Koreans, download DaTalk now!
