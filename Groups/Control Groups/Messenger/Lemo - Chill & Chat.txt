App Description
Tired of all the same social apps that give you the same content over and over again? Meet Lemo. Lemo is the app that lets you meet new people from the comfort of your own room, and discover communities with common interests that you were always meant to meet .

Lemo offers the opportunity to make new friends virtually and a multifaceted platform where you can host video/audio chats, games, and, unlike ever before, unlock different themes and Lemojis as you accomplish unique in-app missions ! This app will allow you to post moments, join any room hosted by an array of people, and discover new music together . Lemo gives you the chance to be together while apart.
