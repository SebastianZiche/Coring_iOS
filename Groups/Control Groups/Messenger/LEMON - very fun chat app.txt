App Description
Find your best partner... Lemon
Lemon is a chat app for anyone who wants to find a best partner!
You can use this app for free and it’s easy to register!

【Recommend for those who...】
・can’t believe they can meet their match.
・are bored.
・are always with same friends
・want to meet new people but do not have the opportunity
・have nothing to do

【How to use】
You only need a nickname to register!
You can immediately start talking and find your favorite person.

If you are interested in this app, feel free to use it!^^

【Should not be used to】
・Slander or attack on any individual or group of individuals.
・Talk about job openings and solicitation.
・Transmit your personal information.

【Considerations】
This App is not available for under 18 years old.
Please don’t tell others your SNS IDs.

https://m.facebook.com/ads/ad_choices

【Cancellation policy】
Please note that we do not accept the cancellation for the current month subscription.
