App Description
Ngaichat is a chatting app that supports Video & Voice Calls along with the Stories feature, voice notes, etc. Chat and make calls with friends. You control your own privacy. Keep your conversation longer and interesting with Ngaichat online messaging app. You can start talking to your friends and family right away. The high-quality chat screen and video call function make each conversation more bright and lively. Make high-quality audio and video calls. Share moments that matter with the people who matters to you.

*Send texts and share files, videos, photos, voice notes, etc.. with your contacts in just one click.
*End-to-End Encryption (Only Sender and Recipient can read the messages)
*Create your own group to connect with your friends and colleagues
*Share your moments through Status Stories
