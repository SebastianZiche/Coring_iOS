App Description
Tchap is an instant messaging service designed for communications between French government officials, to exchange sensitive or less sensitive information, on the go or from an office workstation.

Tchap allows you to chat with one-on-one and group messages, and to share files like any instant messenger, with a built-in directory of user agents, simultaneous use on multiple devices, with a high level of security and confidentiality of private exchanges.

Tchap is reserved for state officials and people invited into a professional conversation with a state official.
