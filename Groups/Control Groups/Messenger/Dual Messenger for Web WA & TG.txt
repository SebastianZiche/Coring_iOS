App Description
You can easily use WhatsApp and Telegram simultaneously on your phone. You don't need to buy a new phone and number for another WhatsApp, you may use Dual Web Messenger for WA & TG for this purpose.

Your phone have to connected to internet and you must scan QR code in phone to use WhatsApp & Telegram Messenger.

Features:

--> Open your WhatsApp and Telegram Account via web connection.
You can click the page and choose your WhatsApp or Telegram page with bottom bars.
WhatsApp will have same view with your phone WhatsApp view.
Also, you will be notified when messages arrived. All you have to do getting messages when app is open. Then it will notify you.
WhatsApp notifications will be active. Videos and photos can be downloaded to iPad and iPhone.

--> Wise and popular Quotes
You could find popular people's word and can share them.

--> Direct Chat on your iPhone
You can send messages to people without adding them to your contact list.

--> Share Emoticons
Stylish text emojis could be shared for fun.

--> Text Repeater
Repeated text can prepared and sent to people or you can copy it to use for other purposes.


*Disclaimer: Dual Web Messenger for WA & TG is an UNOFFICIAL tool for WhatsApp and Telegram Messenger. Dual Web Messenger for WA & TG is developed by an independent developer. WhatsApp feature is loaded from the web version, published in http://web.whatsapp.com and Telegram feature is loaded from the web version, published in https://web.telegram.org
