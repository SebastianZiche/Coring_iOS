App Description
Congratulations to SoulChill for breaking 5 million fans!

SoulChill provides a CHILL & CHAT space for ‘Soulers’ to engage.
You can chat with online Soulers without any stress, create your voice chat rooms and join others’ rooms.
Share your daily life, hobbies, then become friends with others.

Voice Chat Room
-Chill & Chat. Join group voice chatroom or create your own LIVE Chat. Party & Chat in your favourite room.
We enable people to showcase themselves, and stay connected in a positive, healthy, and creative way.
Live voice Chat
-Start a text or voice free chat with friends to talk about your interests or latest trends.
Listen to Music together
-Listen to music or broadcast with your friends in the room with Real-Time voice communication or live stream.
Let's have party everywhere！
Moment
-Share every moment in your life, whether it's taking a selfie, showing your voice, or any little thing that makes you happy.
Soul Test
-Without collecting any personal data, easily answer a few questions to identify your personality.
Curious how accurate we are about you? Take the test and find out!
