App Description
Call, message, and chat securely with allgram, where your data is yours! allgram is a decentralized social, business, and personal communication app hosted on the blockchain. Take control of your information, and your data, with allgram’s secure personal chats and group messaging.



Confidently communicate on a social network optimized for your security. Meet new people, chat with friends, and be productive with your teams, knowing that your data will remain yours.



allgram is built on the blockchain and utilizes decentralized, triple encrypted peer-to-peer connections to ensure that your data remains secure. Share information knowing it will only be seen by the people you share it with. Your identity is safe on allgram, with a highly secure digital ID, stored on the encrypted blockchain.



Make plans with friends, sync with your team for projects, or organize your next big event on allgram. No matter what you do, allgram makes sure that your sensitive data is secure.



allgram has tons of features to help you stay safely connected, including:



SECURE CHATS & CALLS

● Call whomever you want, whenever you want with secure video, voice, and conference calling.

● Message, text, and chat anywhere to keep up to date with your contacts on the go

● Calendar scheduling features can be used to connect even the busiest of groups.

● Whether you’re organizing a business project or keeping your tabletop group up to date on the next session, allgram’s scheduling features can be used to plan your next group project.



YOUR DATA IS YOURS

● Blockchain technology is at the core of allgram, a secure platform that prioritizes your privacy.

● allgram’s digital ID system ensures your identity is protected. Your digital ID is represented by your QR code on the front end, stored on the encrypted blockchain, and accessed via multi-factor authentication.

● Decentralized, peer-to-peer connections ensure that your data is only shared with the people you want to share with.



DISCOVER NEW PEOPLE, JOIN A CLUB

● Enjoy the benefits of social media, while keeping your identity and personal data secure.

● Find friends with similar interests with allgram’s clubs.

● Can’t find a club for your niche hobby? Start a new one and safely meet new people who share your interests.

● Private or public clubs allow you to keep your club exclusive, or let anyone on allgram join.



Stay social, stay secure. allgram’s suite of features, along with its encryption technology, lets you enjoy the best of both private messaging and social networking. Join the next generation of social media, download allgram today!
