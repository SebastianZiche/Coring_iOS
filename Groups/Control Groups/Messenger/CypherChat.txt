Secure Messaging Platform where Privacy Meets Innovation
App Description
Looking for a secure messaging app that keeps your conversations anonymous and fully protected? Look no further! With CypherChat, your privacy is our priority. Say goodbye to invasive data collection and welcome a whole new level of secure communication.

Features that make CypherChat stand out:

Anonymous Sign-Up: No need to share any personal information. Your privacy is yours to keep!

Meet AskCyph™ AI Chatbot: An AI-powered chatbot at your fingertips! Generate content, ask questions, create stunning images, search the web, and visit websites right within your chat.

DocCyph™ - Your Productivity Booster: Extract key info or generate summaries from PDF documents with a breeze. Productivity has never been this efficient!

Seamless Audio/Video Communication: Enjoy crystal-clear, Peer-to-Peer (P2P) audio and video calls. Add virtual backgrounds to make your calls even more exciting!

Hassle-free Peer-to-Peer (P2P) File Sharing: Share files directly with your contacts, ensuring End-to-End Encryption (E2EE) for ultimate security.

Group Conversations: Stay connected with friends, family, or colleagues in private group chats.

Custom Privacy Settings: Tailor your privacy preferences with High, Medium, or Low settings. And guess what? You can send messages without leaving any trace!

Disappearing Messages: Chat with the highest level of privacy in our special chat rooms with disappearing messages.

Invite & Share Securely: Share your CypherChat link or QR Code with custom expiration, ensuring only the right people join your conversations.

Join CypherChat today and become part of our global community committed to safeguarding communication for journalists, human rights activists, and individuals who value their privacy.
