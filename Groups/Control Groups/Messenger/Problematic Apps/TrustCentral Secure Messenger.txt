App Description
In today’s hostile cybersecurity environment, TrustCentral mitigates spoofing attacks, data leaks, unauthorized data sharing, information disclosure, theft, and problematic backend accounting. TrustCentral offers secure, authenticated communication exchanges, ensuring your sensitive data is protected between users and devices. Our application uses patented, user-friendly cryptographic tools enabling you to reliably and securely share your sensitive data among family, friends, colleagues, enterprises, and clients using a first-rate security ecosystem. Our software engine provides flexible solutions built upon our patented Crypto-Enhanced Relationships (CERs). To establish trustworthy connections, remote endpoint relationships are authenticated and paired using CERs. This secure technology is built on longstanding, widely adopted cryptographic standards that are well trusted.
