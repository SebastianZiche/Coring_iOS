App Description
‣ Set your target language, and find a native speaker. Tabee supports 15+ languages.
‣ Chat, practice, learn and meet new people around the globe.

Learning new languages can be hard and dull. Especially, languages that you find difficult. But learning new languages through fun conversations with real native speakers is fun and effective. Tabee lets you find language partners, chat, learn, and make new friends around the globe.

► FIND LANGUAGE PARTNERS
Share info about yourself set your target language, and start searching for native speakers. See photos, bio, and location, and explore our vast global community. Connect with cool people, and who knows, maybe you’ll find your next best friend on Tabee.

► CHAT, LEARN AND HAVE FUN
Start a chat with anyone, and learn new languages through exciting conversations. Practice the language that you want to learn daily and speed up the learning.

Imagine being instantly corrected about grammar mistakes through laughter with a real native speaker. That will for sure make language learning effective and making the same mistakes over and over again. With Tabee you’ll have fun while exploring the culture, tradition, and people of the language that you want to learn.

► SUPPORTS 15+ LANGUAGES
Learn and improve English, Japanese, Korean, Spanish, French, Mandarin Chinese, Cantonese, Portuguese, German, Italian, Russian, Arabic, Turkish, Hindi, Indonesian, Thai, and Vietnamese for FREE!

► MAKE FRIENDS
Tabee is also a handy friend finder tool, as it lets you meet new people through language learning. You can also use it to learn new languages, cultures, and traditions of the nation that is your next travel destination, and find people to meet in real life when you are actually there.

►TABEE FEATURES:
- set target language (15+ languages supported)
- set native language
- meet native speakers and learners
- start a chat with anyone
- see chat history in the chat section
- browse our vast community of users
- set search filters (men/women, age)
- block users

Tabee breaks the complexity and limits of traditional language learning. It makes learning more enjoyable, natural, and vivid. So, join our growing community of language learners and meet new people the fun way!

► Download and use Tabee for free!
