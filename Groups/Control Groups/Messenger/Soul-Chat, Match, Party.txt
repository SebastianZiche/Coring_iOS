App Description
Soul is a social app where people could freely express themselves through audio chat and share what is on their mind while meeting awesome friends worldwide.

# Why Soul is the right place?

-Catering to Gen-Z, a platform to share everything without judgment.

-Fostering empathy, healing and acceptance different cultural backgrounds.

-A.I. empowered virtual playground to helps you make real friends near or far.
