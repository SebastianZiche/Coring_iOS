App Description
GoCut is the best Video Effect Maker. With GoCut, anyone can create stunning videos by customizing your animation with magic Neon glowing brush,adding neon stickers, retro filters, transition effects and so on. With many powerful toolset, you can easily take your video to another level. Now it's your turn to make awesome neon effect videos with GoCut and impress the world with your masterpiece.

Download Gocut and edit your photo and video like a pro with neon light, neon effect, neon spiral and neon signs... Turn everyday moments into works of art.

Making neon video becomes easier with GOCut :
>customize your animation with magic Neon glowing brush!
> Add neon stickers in your video.
> Magic neon spiral effect animation make your video stand out.
> Different Neon Sticker available: neon heart,angle wings,spirals,guitar,star,music style,rocket,beats,etc..
> Play the selected video if you want to check.
> Your video is ready and don’t hesitate to share it with your friends.
