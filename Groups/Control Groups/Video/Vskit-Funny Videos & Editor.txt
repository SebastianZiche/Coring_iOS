App Description
Vskit is an easy-to-use short video application with over 100 million users. No matter where you are, you can watch and create popular and funny videos for free. Choose Vskit to record & share your life by short videos, express yourself easily and make friends across Africa!

Discover - African Trending Videos
- Recommend millions of the hottest short videos daily from Africa and around the world , and make you learn about the local trends.
- Find amazing videos by talented African content creators here!

Special Effects -Super-Duper Cool & Beautiful
- Easy to record your beautiful moments with amazing new filters, beauty and makeup features!
- Customize your videos with various most fashionable effects & funny stickers & other shooting templates.
- One Click to complete your video by using various real-time updated photo templates easily.
- Duet with anyone you like； The next African short video talent could be you！

Creation - Music & Pro Editor
- Free to use global latest music from around the world across all genres such as Pop, Afrobeats, Gospel music etc. to create your viral music video.
- Various practical editing tools can help you make more professional and unique video in-app easily.

Social Community - Share the happy times
- Millions of Vskiters are online every day；It is easy to make friends!
- Support to send text, pictures and videos to your friends, making interaction easier！
- One click to download and share your content to Instagram, WhatsApp and other social media platforms, giving you a larger audience to showcase your creativity!

On Vskit, you will find the real people, real life, real feeling. A place where you can express yourself in your way. A bigger world awaits. Join vskit!
