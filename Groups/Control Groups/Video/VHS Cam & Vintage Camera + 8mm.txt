App Description
Now you don't have to choose between VHS and Vintage Camera Apps- You can get them all in one single app!

This app contains over 15 filters going back to the 1920's, 50's, 60's 70's, 80's, 90's and many more!

Filters are fully customizable with adjustments that allow you to get just the right look for the perfect movie. App Features:

-Over 15 Filters (fully customizable)
-Add glitch effect with a single tap
-Import photos or videos
-Customize your own date displayed
-Customize the film title
-Static or countdown effect
-Burn Film ending effect
-Customizable screen resolution
-Multiple different audio effects
+ Much more!

Try it today!
