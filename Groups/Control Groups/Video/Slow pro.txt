App Description
Create slow-motion videos! Slow pro is the best way to edit the playback speed of your videos.
Choose a video from your library. Select from a wide variety of speeds.
FEATURES:
• Apply slo-mo, time-lapse & stop motion
effects on your videos.
• Edit video speed up to 240fps or 12x faster or Slower.
• Adjust video speed from particular frames.
• Can hold the video’s specific portion.
• High-resolution video output.
• Quick processing time!
• Simple & very easy to use!
