App Description
VE app is popular video editor in which multiple functionality like apply effects on video, add glitch, add text add effects, add stickers, slow & fast motion effects, Drawing tool...etc

Features:
• Choose from Library for editing.
• Add Different Video Effects on Your Video.
• Best Video editor to add amazing and free stickers like Glitch, Text, emoji, Love, alphabet...etc on video.
• Draw different shape and signs on video with multiple colours.
• Edit your video with frames or borders for making video special.
• Add different stylish text on video.
• Brush tool for free to draw on video.
• Slow Motion feature for video to convert into fast or slow mode video.
• Fast edit video.
• Share video to TikTok and other social media quickly.
