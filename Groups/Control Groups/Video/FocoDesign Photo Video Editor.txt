App Description
FocoDesign is an awesome photo editor, video maker, and online design platform. Create custom eye-catching social media posts (including graphic design photos and animated videos), e-commerce posters, small business flyers, invitation cards, and more! Easily one tap to create videos, cards, photo collages for Insta stories, and video collages.

No edit experience is needed with FocoDesign. From a photo to an outstanding post, logo, poster, or flyer. All you need to do is select photos and tap! FocoDesign makes the design simpler!

The 1st background remover with tons of free graphic design templates, stickers, filters, videos, posters, and more! Super clearly, completely ruin the entire composition and edge of a photo and make it worthless and awesome, easily turn your photos into GIFs. Clips & Stickers!

Also, FocoDesign provides the largest online design platform for all social media lovers and small business runners!

You will find all your needs for your Instagram story, highlight, layout, post, and more. Besides, thousands of copyright graphic design templates fit nearly all features of your e-commerce business, which also helps thousands of users to grow almost 30% of sales volume.
