App Description
Edith photo & video editing app is designed to cover all of your everyday needs creating beautiful content.
All features are crafted by creators and driven by your obsession to make aesthetic content, like no one else.

We are honored to be a partner in your content creation journey and become your preferred editing app.

Edith app always includes the latest and greatest on-trend editing tools, including:

PRESETS + Exportable Lightroom presets
300+ easy-to-use presets on the tips of your fingers, made for different types and styles of photography. To list a few, film, moody, bright, minimalist, retro and vintage vibes.

TEXTURES
Wanna create standout content? No problem. Huge variety of textures and overlays here to help you do this in just a few taps. No need to buy disposable cameras anymore, all you need to have is the Edith app.

BORDERS
Borders tool lets you add some aesthetic feelings for your photography.

FEED PLANNER
Wanna have a perfect Instagram feed? Curate and preview your Instagram feed with our drag-and-drop planner. Multiple accounts are allowed as well.

BATCH EDITING
10 photos at the same time with the same editing. Sounds fantastic! Edith app can make it reality in few taps.

ADJUSTMENTS
Tweak your edits with professional tools. Our user's favorites are Dim and Grain for an effortless moody film look.

Follow us on Instagram and TikTok: @get.edith
Use the hashtag #editwithedith to be featured
