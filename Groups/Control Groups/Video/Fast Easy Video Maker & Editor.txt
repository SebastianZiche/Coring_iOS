App Description
LushVid helps you to create movies from videos with greeting text, animations, video filters and background music.

LushVid is very easy to use and extremely user friendly.

FEATURES:
- Overlay animations.
- Lot of beautiful themes.
- Text with video.
- Video filters.
- Long duration movie making.
- Add background music.
- Preview and Save to Camera Roll share to Instagram, Twitter, Facebook, etc.
