App Description
Video FX is a powerful one-click video effects studio for your phone!

Add music and dazzling special effects to your iMovie, Musical.ly, Instagram and Youtube videos. It just takes one click to add beautifully crafted effects to any video.

Download Video FX and get 10+ new animations and video enhancements, featuring effects like autumn leaves, falling snow and anamorphic lens flares!

Start spicing up your videos – get Video FX for FREE today!

HOW IT WORKS:
-Import a video from your camera roll, including Vine, Hyperlapse, Flipagram, Instagram or Dubsmash videos.
-Add effects from our library of LIVE animations with just a tap.
-Export and share!

KEY FEATURES:

VIDEO EFFECTS
Choose from 10+ handcrafted video effects, including:
- Cherry Blossoms: Instantly create a romantic atmosphere
- Party Time: Add 3D confetti to your party videos
- Dandelions: Give your video a cool and refreshing nature feel
- Film Damage: Create aged film effect instantly
- TV Glitch: Turn your videos into masterpieces of digital art with the TV glitch effect
- Color Frames: Simple and beautiful dynamic frames for your videos
- And so much more!

MUSICAL EFFECTS
- Add music to your videos, whether you want to lip sync, add background music or make an AMV
- Enhance your videos with sound effects

VIDEO EDITING
- Merge multiple video clips
- Add text to videos
- Overlay effects

Spice up your videos with music and effects, then share them on your favorite social networks! Download Video FX today and start making movie magic!
