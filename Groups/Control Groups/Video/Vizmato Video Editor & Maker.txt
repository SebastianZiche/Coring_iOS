App Description
Create awesome videos and slideshows with this easy to use video editing app. Add filters, themes, music, effects and text to make awesome movies. Create videos on this easy to use video editor and share it with your friends on Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, Whatsapp and other social media channels!

Download the free slideshow maker and video editing app on your iPhone or iPad now! Edit videos like a professional with Vizmato!

Some of the Vizmato’s features –

• A Simple yet Powerful Video Editor –
• Choose from 20+ video themes and from 40+ awesome visual effects to add a Hollywood edge to your video
• Clip, trim and edit videos with ease to create the perfect movie in our easy to use video editor
• Choose your background music from 140+ Free Viztunes, or add background music of your choice while editing
• Vizmato’s editor with a voice changer effects let’s you modulate your voice to sound like a Baby, chipmunk, ghost and more!
• Tell a video story and record a voiceover on your video
• Editor with speed control – Add a slow up or speed up effects on your video. You can also choose to reverse the video
• Add customised text and stickers to your movie with our text editor

• Amazing Slideshow Maker –
• Convert your photos into amazing videos! Just add your photos or videos, select a theme and add music to create an amazing video slideshow in seconds
• Choose from our collection of curated and royalty free music, or select your own music for your photo slideshow
• Customise your photos, videos and text in this easy to use slideshow maker app

• HD Video Recorder with Live FX -
• Vizmato is your own PRO HD Video Recorder! Use it to record a video in HD with filters, themes and instant video effects applied while you record!
• Play music while recording to get your lip sync just right

• Watch cool user created videos! –
• Watch movies made by a community of Vizmato video makers. Be an aspiring moviemaker and share your creation on the Vizmato platform

• Share it with the world –
• A simple one tap export in HD and sharing of your videos on Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, Twitter, Whatsapp, Vine, Snapchat or any social network of your choice.
