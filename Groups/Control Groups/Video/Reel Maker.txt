App Description
Reel Maker is a video reel editor app for your social media. It helps you easily create high-quality reels and stories to add mojo to your videos and gain more likes and followers!
This is the perfect tool for quick video editing, that shows off your ideas and makes a splash on social media. Post high-quality clips and start making the most of your editing skills.

Features:
▶ No Watermark
▶ Trim
▶ Transitions
▶ +250,000 Music Songs
▶ Add Texts & GIFs
▶ High-quality Effects & Overlays
▶ Edit Video or Photo
▶ Use also for Stories
▶ High Quality Export
▶ No coins, no sign-up

◆ Customer Support

◆ Subscriptions & Terms

• Any unused portion of a free trial period will be forfeited when the user purchases a subscription.
