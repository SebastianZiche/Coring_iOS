App Description
Muse is filled with dozens of on-trend templates, from travel to food and lifestyle, to help content creators, bloggers, and influencers create like-worthy Reels, Stories and TikToks without spending hours on editing.

How to create your masterpiece:

1. Choose a template
2. Add your photos and videos
3. Share your video on social media.

Key features:

- Various template styles: easily create travel recaps, vlogs, lifestyle videos, and more;
- Exclusive design from zen minimalism to high fashion;
- Weekly updates: you will not get bored with the current templates, because the library will be updated weekly;
- Auto beat sync: the app automatically matches your clips to the music and analyzes beat points to add video effects;
- Easy to use: сreating videos just got even easier, just pick a template, add your content, and you're ready to go!
- Personal Trend: if you haven't found a suitable variation among templates, simply choose the "Start from scratch" option and create your unique content!
- Simple sharing: once you’ve got everything just-so, you can then share the results to your favorite social media…and get lots of likes in no time!

So what are you waiting for? Start creating videos today and enjoy Muse!
