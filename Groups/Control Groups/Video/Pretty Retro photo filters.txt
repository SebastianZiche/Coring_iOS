App Description
PRETTY RETRO is a vintage filter app and powerful photo editor.

EASY TO USE

PRETTY RETRO is very easy to use. All you have to do is choose a filter you like, and that's it! Most of the work is already done. Then you can go to the editor and add parameters to your taste.

BECOME A PHOTO EDITING EXPERT

The editor provides limitless options from professional photography programs. You can change the color temperature, contrast and any other photograph parameters.
