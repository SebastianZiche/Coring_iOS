App Description
This app helps you to create beautiful video from photos with different types of transitions, video output mode, background and many more and edit your video like trim, add music and add overlays to videos.

Video Maker With Music also provide different music tracks and also you can pick music from iTune library, get music from video and record voice for your video and also advance trimming function and latest overlay collections.

App Features:-

• 30+ awesome photo transition effects for photo slideshow and photo with music
• Latest collection of background images and colour for photo transition and photo with music
• Support different type of video output modes like square, portrait, landscape, aspect fit and aspect fill
• Easy to set photo transition time duration with slider and sort photos again after selection
• Add music from different sources like iTune library, music from video and record voice and from over latest collect short music tracks
• Advance trimming function with frame selection
• 500+ latest and trending collection of video filters, fx effects, frames and stickers to make video more beautiful
• Easy to add text over video with different font style and colour
• Save video in custom album and share video directly from app


Usage of App:-

- Choose photos from camera or gallery
- Choose video from camera or gallery
- Apply photo transition effects, glow effects and overlays for music photo
- Apply photo transition effects and speed for photo slideshow
- Set output mode for video like portrait, landscape or square
- Add music to selected part of video and Trim/Cut unwanted part of video
- Apply filter, fx effect, frame and sticker overlays
- Add text overlay with different font style and colour
- Export video and save in custom album
- Share your video in social media directly from app


Now make professional look like videos from your photos and memories with different type of editing tools and overlay collection with Video Maker With Music!!
