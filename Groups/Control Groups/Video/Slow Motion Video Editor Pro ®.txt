App Description
The SlowMotion is a powerful application that allows you to create stunning slow motion videos effortlessly. Whether you want to capture a memorable moment in slow motion or enhance the visual appeal of your footage, this app has got you covered.

With its intuitive interface, you can easily import your videos and choose the exact moments you want to slow down. Simply move the points up or down to adjust the speed and create the desired slow motion effect. You also have the option to apply different speeds to different parts of the video, giving you complete creative control.

In addition to its speed adjustment feature, the Slow Motion Video Editor offers some editing tools to enhance your videos further. You can add filter, add background music and add text to personalize your creations.

Once you are satisfied with your slow motion masterpiece, you can save it in various formats and resolutions, making it compatible with different devices and platforms. You can also directly share your videos on social media platforms, allowing you to showcase your creativity and impress your friends and followers.

Whether you are a professional videographer or someone who enjoys capturing and editing videos for fun, the Slow Motion Video Editor is a must-have app for creating captivating slow motion videos. Its user-friendly interface, versatile editing features, and seamless sharing options make it the perfect tool to bring your videos to life in slow motion.

Features:

+ Choose a video from Photo Library, Files or record a new video with the camera.
+ Trim/cut the selected video.
+ Adjust slow and fast motion speed with 2 modes: basic and advance.
+ Add background music to the video
+ Apply video filter to make your video more lively and stylish
+ Add text to video
+ Adjust between video volume and music volume
+ Save your projects to edit them later.
