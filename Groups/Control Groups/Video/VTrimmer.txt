App Description
This is an app for trimming video easily like these 3 steps bellow.

-- How to use. --
1.Tap "Video Camera Icon" to open your camera roll, and select a video you want to trim.

2.You can set the starting/ending time with the slider has 2 thumbs.

3.When you press "save" button, the video will be trimmed and saved to camera roll.

-- Tips
- Press "Clock Button" to open time picker. Then you can select time detailedly like each 0.01 sec.
