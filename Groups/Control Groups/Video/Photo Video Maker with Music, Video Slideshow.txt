App Description
- Photo Video Maker with Music is the easiest way to create, edit and share amazing photo music videos and stories
- Photo Video Maker with Music is a video editor, photo slideshow maker & movie editing app
- With your photos, videos and musics, you can easily make fun, inspiring, or creative photo music videos

- Main Features :

+ Add multiple photos and music to the video slideshow.

+ Select Music from multiple predefined sound track , or import your own.

+ Save video Stories in your app and view, share them with Photo Video Maker App.

- Photo Editor :

+ Multiple background option, including blur pattern.

+ Stickers : Variety of stickers including snap, smiley, giddy, etc. to add on your photo to make it more attractive.

+ Text Editor : Add your favorite caption over your images.

+ Filters : Awesome filter collection to beautify your photos.

- This app is made to save your beautiful photos in movie style to view them later or share.
