App Description
Creative effects and filters for photos and videos: VHS, Stardust, Retro, Golden Hour. Retro, vintage, or Indie Kid – find your own aesthetic!

Take your photography to the next level with free presets. Easily import and edit your RAW photos. Use editing tools like Contrast and Saturation to make your photos pop or use Grain and Fade to add texture and mimic analog film effects. Adjust or play around with your photo perspectives with Crop and Skew. Save and recreate your favorite edits with Recipes.

Create and edit videos with our easy-to-use video editor Adjust white balance and experiment with color control with HSL. Take your IG Stories, TikToks & Reels to the next level. Crop video clips to the perfect dimensions and ratios. Try Glitch video effects and other trendy filters in the video editor. Trim videos. Add your best moments to a video collage

Show your best moments in the coolest possible way!
