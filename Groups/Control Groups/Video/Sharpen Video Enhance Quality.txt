App Description
Sharpen Video is a simple yet powerful video editor for sharpening and enhancing videos. Enhance the quality of your videos by modifying parameters like sharpness, saturation, exposure, gamma, brightness, contrast and more. You can then save or share the stunning result video.

ENHANCE QUALITY:

* Get beautiful enhanced video results
* Adjust video parameters like exposure, gamma, saturation, brightness, contrast, hue and more to get better results
* Sharpen the video using two smart sharpening filters
* Preserve the aspect ratio of the original photo/video.

SMART SHARPENING ENGINE:

* Keeps the original high quality of the photo or video and only sharpens it
* Choose between two smart sharpening algorithms. Each algorithm fits different video types.
* Zoom in to see the result in more detail
* Fast performance
* View history of all previously converted videos
* Save results to Photos or Files
* Share results with friends using any other social app you like
* Support for rotating the video 90º, 180º, 270º
* All common video formats are supported (mp4, mov, flv, and more)

BOOST VOLUME:

* Boost the volume of the video file up to x10
* The volume of the result file will be louder regardless to the phone's volume



HOW TO USE:

1. Load a video by clicking “Open file”
2. Apply sharpen effect and adjust color parameters as you wish to enhance the video
3. Watch the result, save it to Photos/Files and share with friends

* Note: Do not apply too much sharpening into the video as it may cause unwanted artifacts. Start with a small amount and then increase it as long as the result looks good.
