App Description
Hannah Godwin’s app for photo and video edits is here! Setty is your new editing BFF, with on-trend filters ranging from airy to moody, Instagram grid planning, and much more. Set your tone with—

FILTERS
23 styled filters with a focus on modern, styled presets

GRID
Visualize your best feed yet with user-friendly grid planning, with the option to upgrade and add multiple Instagram accounts

EFFECTS
Film-inspired effects like dust, grain, and vintage VHS dates take the guesswork out of creating a vibey edit 

EDITING TOOLS
Dial in the details with professional photo adjustments, including HSL

TEXT
Add subtitles and spell it out using clean and modern fonts

Get unlimited access with Setty Pro, which includes:
* All Setty filters, effects, and fonts
* HSL tool for specific color adjustments
* Unlimited grid access to add multiple social accounts

Keep up with us on social @settyapp or by using the hashtag #settyapp

MEMBERSHIP PRICING, TERMS, & CONDITIONS

Setty Pro Yearly:

The Setty Pro Yearly membership is $34.99 USD per year. Pricing in other countries may vary, and charges may be converted to your local currency depending on the country you live in.

The Setty Pro Yearly membership starts with a free 3-day trial period. Once the trial is over, you'll be charged $34.99 USD per year. Your Setty Pro Yearly membership will automatically renew unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. You can go to your Settings > iTunes Account & App Store > Apple ID > Subscriptions to manage your membership and turn auto-renew off. Your iTunes Account will be charged when the purchase is confirmed. Any unused portion of a free trial period will be forfeited when the user purchases a subscription during free trial.

Setty Pro Monthly:

The Setty Pro Monthly membership is $4.99 USD per month. Pricing in other countries may vary, and charges may be converted to your local currency depending on the country you live in.

The Setty Pro Monthly membership automatically starts upon subscription and you'll be charged $4.99 USD per month. Your Setty Pro Monthly membership will automatically renew unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. You can go to your Settings > iTunes Account & App Store > Apple ID > Subscriptions to manage your membership and turn auto-renew off. Your iTunes Account will be charged when the purchase is confirmed. Any unused portion of a free trial period will be forfeited when the user purchases a subscription during free trial.
