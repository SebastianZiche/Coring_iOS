App Description
【Your handy Video Collage Maker】

PhotoGrid Video does for videos what PhotoGrid does for photos. The best Collage App for you to create stunning videos for social media!

PhotoGrid Video is an easy-to-use video collage maker!
With the video merger, you can create a stunning photo and video collage in seconds!
You merely need to pick photos or videos to collage, then add adjustable layouts, with music, text, stickers, and background! Create your own video collages for free now.

● Fabulous feature ●
* Free collage app for collage video and photo together
* 300+ stylish layouts
* Supports up to 9 videos collage
* Supports up to 16 photos collage
* Resizable photos & videos
* Makes a video collage with beautiful patterns or colors background
* 300+ free background music
* Shares with your friends of TikTok, IG, FB easily
* Adds text on video in seconds
* Supports 1000+ fancy fonts
* Supports crop video feature
* Supports multiple ratios

● Professional Video Collage ●

◎ Up to 9 videos montages
Create 2-9 video collages for free in seconds with our grid feature.
Get inspired with a wide variety of grid layouts.
Collaging should be relaxing and fun!
Built for sharing on Instagram, and Facebook now!

◎Mix up videos with photos
Supports up to 9 photos and videos, letting you easily collage photos and videos without effort!
Allows you to combine all your holiday memories into one video.
Let creating photo and video montages become a real pleasure for your Instagram posts.

◎1000+ materials and fonts
A variety of backgrounds, stickers, and text fonts to choose from!
Basic text functions are for free, you can apply color, bold, italic to the text, change text backgrounds, and replace fonts.

Enjoy!

If you have any questions or suggestions, please feel free to contact us via video.collage.app@gmail.com
