App Promotional Text
The all-in-one photo editor you've been looking for! Enjoy our collection of over 300 exclusive filters and effects, and make your pics stand out.
App Description
Simple yet powerful, Photo Editor makes it easy to edit your favorite pics the way you like. Our huge collection of filters and effects is designed to make any picture stand out: try it yourself!

Ready to take your photos to the next level? Use our app to edit any pic of yours and make it unique. Did you know that we offer over 300 exclusive effects and filters to do that? Wicked!

Here's a complete list of all the powerful photo editing tools that you will find on the app.

TRENDY FILTERS
Get access to over 300 filters thoughtfully designed by professional photographers.

SPECIAL EFFECTS
Apply chromatic aberration, color shift, distortions, tv, glitch, VHS, oil painting, paper drawing, blur, broken glass and other cool effects to create extraordinary photos that will catch everyone's eye.

OVERLAY TEXTURES
Add light leak, lens flare, confetti, glitter, firework, fire, rainbow, dust, grain, scratches, rain, show, fog and mist textures to make your photos even more special.

COOL STICKERS
Is it even a photo editor if there are no stickers? Get access to a huge selection of realistic and cartoon stickers that you won't find anywhere else.

BACKGROUND EDITING
Add beautiful frames to your pictures and choose from 90+ stunning backgrounds (geometric, watercolor, floral, cosmic, marble, wood, etc.).

TEXT EDITOR
Add text to your photo and customize it with over 60 unique fonts. You can also adjust the text color, spacing and alignment, and choose among 30 beautiful text arts.

ADJUSTMENTS
Enhance the quality of your photos with our professional tools: exposure, contrast, hue, saturation, sharpness, vibrance, gamma, temperature, tint, shadows, highlights, grain and vignette.

EASY BEFORE AND AFTER
While editing, you can instantly compare the adjusted or edited photo with the original.

VIDEO FX EFFECTS
Bring motion magic to your still photos: create a short video out of a picture. Choose among 10+ video effects: rain, snow, movie, VHS, tv, glitch, water drop and more. You can also export your video in HD format: cool, right?

MORE COOL FEATURES
+ Instantly share your edited photos to social media such as Instagram, TikTok, Snapchat and Facebook.
