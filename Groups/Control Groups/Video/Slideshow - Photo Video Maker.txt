App Description
Slide Show Maker for tiro to use in one-click make video, easily to apply exquisite templates, select pictures and automatically generate video blockbusters to help you record intimate moments between you and your children, sweet memories between couples, moments with family members, and unforgettable student careers and so on, there is also a custom editing mode to create a cool MV for yourself

【Features】
Easily make cool Slide Show, effect previews, picture selection, and video generation in one moment

Many templates, choose as you like, suitable for parties, travel, birthday parties and other memory moments

Rich and interesting text styles and dynamic effects, let your short video and the audience carry out interesting information transmission

Cool transition effects to make your short video more characteristic

A variety of video templates, one-click import to easily get beautiful videos

Many large-scale texture filters, giving you a different style experience

Automatically select the best playback time, allowing you to arrange the picture display time most reasonably

Professional picture editing, easy to use picture animation, to add color to your video

Output ultra-clear image quality, which can be shared to different social platforms to show your lifestyle

Use Slide Show Maker to keep your good memories and show off your wonderful lifestyle.
