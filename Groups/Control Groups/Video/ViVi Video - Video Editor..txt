App Description
ViVi Video is a video editing software. Whether you are a video creation expert or a video creation novice, you can try vivi to make beautiful videos.

Vivi video clip features:

「Video clip」
The function is simple and easy to use, and Novice can also be used easily;

「Filter function」
Aestheticism filter makes your video different;

「Super multiple stickers}
Facial expression bag and stickers can be used at will to add some materials to your video;

「Text addition」
Text function, Add subtitles to your video
