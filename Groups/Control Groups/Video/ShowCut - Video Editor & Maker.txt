App Description
ShowCut - A free video editing app for video editing beginners.

1500+ theme template videos with music, transition effect, filter, text, emoji and sticker, etc.

Make video with One Click and let everyone can be a video creator!

[ An Easy-to-Use Video Editor ]
Are those video editing apps too complicated for you? It's not hard to edit video.
Try ShowCut, three-steps to make video:
1. Choose a template;
2. Import your videos and photos(or take some pictures);
3. Export video with One Click!

[ 1500+ Theme Video Template ]
- Celebrate 2021 festival & holiday, like Christmas, New Year's Eve, Diwali, Eid al-Fitr, etc;
- Make your selfie alive;
- Save birthday memory;
- Record honeymoon, witness wedding and anniversary;
- Turn family album to video collage & photo mv;
- Create vlog for travel, party, graduation, etc;
- Create story, intro video for your channel;
- Memory album: record every moment you cherish;
- Feel Lucky: take a picture, auto-apply a template for you;
- Even make funny video to prank your friend, LOL

[ Video Editing ]
- Merge video, crop video clips;
- Mute video clips;
- Video loop;
- Support video formats: mp4, mov, avi, H.264, H265.

[ Photo Editing ]
- Photo layout: crop photos to square fit;
- Selfie remove background: add effects to selfies or insert yourself into interesting background environments;
- Face & Selfie mirror;
- Support image formats: jpg, jpeg, gif, png.

[ Music, Sound Effect ]
- Edit video with music: template has preset music;
- Lots of sound effects.

[ Filter and Effect ]
- Video effects like beats, glitch, slow motion, noise, retro DV, etc;
- Cool filters like polaroid filter, old filter, movie filter for video;
- Preset filters are adjust brightness, contrast, curves and etc.

[ Video Transition Effect ]
- Combine clips with smooth transition effects;
- Make your video more eye-catching, editing video like a Pro editor.

[ Sticker, Gif, Text & Emoji ]
- Add texts, effect fonts, song lyrics and emojis on video;
- Add meme stickers & gifs on video.

[ Export HD Video ]
- Fast export 720p HD video;
- Export 1080p Full HD video(ShowCut PRO subscription required).

[ Share ]
- Want to become a meme maker, video star or vlog star? Share your videos to social apps, like WhatsApp, tiktok, Instagram, YouTube etc.

[ ShowCut PRO ]
By subscribing to ShowCut PRO, you will receive the following services:
- Unlimited use PRO templates
- Export 1080p Full HD video
- No watermark
- No ads

As the app developer, we will continue to improve ShowCut experience;
As the edit app, video maker, we will continue to improve our edit tool: edit video, edit photo and edit music, filters and effects features;
As the video template app, we will add more theme templates, cover more festivals, holidays, Anniversary, events and etc.
