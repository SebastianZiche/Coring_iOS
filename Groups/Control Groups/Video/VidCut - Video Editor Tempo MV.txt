App Description
Imagine the performance of a desktop editor, optimized for your mobile device. Video Master makes it easy to create beautiful videos on your iPhone. You can trim & merge video clips and add awesome transitions and glitch effects to your videos. Just tap to adjust your video speed, add the perfect background music to create professional-looking videos you’ll love to share. So simple and powerful!


POWERFUL VIDEO EDITING TOOLS
- Merge videos and photos
- Choose your transition style and control the speed between transitions.
- Adjust playback speed for fast or slow motion.
- Trim, cut and crop your photos and video clips.
- Bring your videos to life with funny and zoom effects.
- Create masterpieces from your summer getaway, your birthday party and your lovely family dinner

MUSIC & VOICE-OVERS
- Lots of Hot Music and Funny Sound Effects.
- Add narration or custom sound effects with the built-in voice recorder.
- Use a song from your own iTunes collection (subject to iTunes terms).
- Add your own music to video, like mp3 files and other format.
- Volume control for both original video sound and mp3 songs.

STICKERS & TEXT
- Add magic stickers to your video
- Add text on video and photo.
- Add emoji on photo.
- With timeline features, easy to sync text and stickers with video.

FILTERS
- Lots of Photo filters
- Unique Video filters

SAVE YOUR VIDEO & EASILY SHARE
- High video output resolution.
- Share to social apps, like Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, WhatsApp, TikTok, etc.
