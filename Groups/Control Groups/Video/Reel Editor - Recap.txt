Whether you’re a small business owner or daily vlogger, Recap will save you countless hours of editing by INSTANTLY syncing your uploaded content to trending audios!

Yes, it’s as good as it sounds. It’s time to take your social media game to the next level.

HIGHLIGHTS

● The easiest to use short-form video editor
● Sync videos & photos to music/audio

● New templates released all the time

SAVE HOURS of editing content by our exclusive templates. Simply upload your content and Recap will create a Reel/TikTok in seconds!
