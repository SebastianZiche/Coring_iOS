App Description
YouCut as an all-in-one [Video Editor] and [Video Maker] for YouTube and other social media, provide you ultimate editing experience. Edit videos with songs, filters, text, emoji, etc.

[Easy & No Watermark!]
Edit videos like a pro. Create your own videos from photos or video clips with YouCut in minutes. No video editing experience needed. Try it Now.

Features:

[Pro Video Editor and Movie Maker]
YouCut as one of top AI video editing apps, provide you with AI Avatar, AI Captions, AI Cutout and more. No banner ads.

[AI Art Photo Editor]
As AI art photo editor, turn your photos into any anime style in seconds with AI. And you can even explore a more realistic version of yourself with this pro video maker. No banner ads.

[Video Speed Control]
Fast/slow motion video (Adjust video speed from 0.2× to 100×), Editing video and adjust video speed with video filters and effects.

[Photo Slideshow Maker]
Powerful music video editor with photos, pro video combiner app, merge photos with PIP to create slideshow.
Combine videos with photos, add custom photo cover, auto captions for videos, edit videos with music like a pro.

[No Watermark]
As a music video editor and full screen video maker for YouTube, YouCut never add Watermark to your video. There is no banner ads on the screen, unlike other video editing apps.

[Add Music to Video]
It is a professional TikTok editor, YouTube intro maker and Instagram story cutter that meets all your needs.
1. 100+ music and built-in sound effects.
2. Edit videos with your music.
3. Extract audio from video.

[Video Filters & AI Effects]
Add beautiful movie style video filters and AI effects to video. Movie maker and film editor without watermark.

[Video Color Adjust]
Adjust video brightness, contrast, saturation, etc. Custom video filters and effects. Best movie cutter and AI captions generator for videos.

[Change Video Background]
Add different borders & no crop video.
1. Change background color of your video, blur background.
2. Remove background with AI cutout in a tap.

[Video Compressor]
1. Best HD video maker & Tik Tok editor.
2. Pro video editor and maker supports resolution up to 4K. Export videos without losing quality.

[Share Video]
Directly share video to YouTube, Instagram, TikTok…to get hundreds of 'likes'!

AI Art Generator, Cut, Blur, Photo Slideshow Maker, Add music, Add text to video, Apply transition effects, Add FX video filters, Add keyframe animation, Captions for video, Share to YouTube... Fast/Slow motion video editor without losing quality!
