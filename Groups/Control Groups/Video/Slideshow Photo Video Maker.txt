App Description
Turn your memories into movies with music. The app helps you to create beautiful SlideShow from your photos. It's completely FREE.

Simple steps:

• Pick your photos
• Organize them in the right order (Long tap a photo and drag to reorder it)
• Select a transition to animate between photos
• Choose your favorite music from our gallery or your own
songs.
• Select the slideshow's length to change the duration per photo.
• Share it on Facebook, Instagram, Snapchat, YouTube or save it to your camera roll.

So, let's make your memories even more amazing
