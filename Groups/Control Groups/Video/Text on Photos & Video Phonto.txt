App Description
Text on Photos Editor (Formerly Bizny) allows you to add texts on pictures and videos with ease!

Text
• 1,000+ fonts available for free
• You can install other fonts.
• Supports text curve, text bending and gradient text.
• Text size, text color are changeable.
• Text shadow and text background are adjustable.
• 20,000+ Background images for you to choose.
• Supports text on video and video trimming.

Language
• Supports English
• Supports Traditional/simplified Chinese
• Supports Japanese, Thai and Vietnamese
• Supports Arabic, Cyrillic and Greek

Watermark
• Create your own logo.
• Add social watermark such as Instagram and Facebook.
• You can upload your own logos.
• Create custom text watermarks
We will help you remember each of its position and save bunch of times!

Collage Maker
• Support photo collage up to 16 images
• Support up to 4 video collages
• Provides 720P and 1080P high-quality video output options.

Materials
• Huge collections of festive and useful stickers.
• 3,000+ awesome backgrounds.

Youtube Thumbnail
• Provides 16:9 ratios for youtube cover.
• Provides exclusive 1:1 aspect ratio for Instagram posts
• Supports plenty other ratios such as 3:4, 4:5, 5:7, and 9:16 for Instagram Stories, Facebook banner, Facebook cover, flyer and Pinterest collections! Get all you want in one second.
