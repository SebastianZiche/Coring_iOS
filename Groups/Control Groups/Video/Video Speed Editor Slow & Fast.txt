App Description
Easiest Video Speed Editor!!

- First divide your video into multiple short clips that need different speeds.
- Set the speed for each short clip, review. You can set up to 8x slow or fast motion speeds.
- Merge all the short clips back to one. That's it !

- You can apply various overlay shapes and filter effects for each short clip before merging.
- Add beautiful background music from the app library or from your iPod library. Or keep original audio as it is.

Have fun with the speed!!
