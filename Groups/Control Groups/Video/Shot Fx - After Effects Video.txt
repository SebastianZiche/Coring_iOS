App Description
Wanna record your own VFX movie to amaze your friends? Download ShotFX now to achieve your desire.


Shot FX is an effect video camera and editor for adding effects to your videos, and there’s no need for complicated equipment or studios now. No matter what you are, a casual user or celebrity on social media, you will find making masterpieces has never been easier than now.


- FX video templates -

·Dragons, magic circles, space and planets…add imaginary scenes in reality.
·Fire, laser, lightning…hold all these in your hands with ShotFX.
·Wings, swirling water, tornados…all can 3D-surround you in your videos.
·Shooting guide for every single template, making it easy and clear to shoot an FX video.

- Add and edit other FX -
·Add effects and filters right after shooting, making your FX movies even more shocking
·Select any video clips in your album and add effects on them
·Effect stickers can be rotated or zoomed to fit your videos best

As a group of VFX movie fans just like you, we wish to bring the joy of shooting magic videos to you. With ShotFX, you may travel in space, fly with wings, cast spells in magic circle… and all that you need is imagination, creativity, and an iPhone.  


Making a blockbuster has never been so easy - we've tuned every step visually, and operationally. We're actively improving the effects and new effects are updated regularly every week. More surprise is waiting for your discovery in the future.
