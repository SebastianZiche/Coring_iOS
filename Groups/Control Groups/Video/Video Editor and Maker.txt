App Description
Video Editor app for iPhone and iPad simplifies the entire process of adding music to your videos. Grab videos directly from your camera or gallery and cut out unwanted moments using the video trimmer feature. You can add tracks to any videos by recording the audio yourself or choosing songs from iTunes Music.

Key Features of Video Editor:
- Trim videos in just a few steps
- 3 ways to add music to videos: in-app tracks, iTunes Music, and by recording your own audios
- Trim audio tracks as necessary after adding
- Adjust volume, loop audio, use fade-in, and fade-out option
- Share on social media platforms like Instagram, Facebook, etc.
- No watermark
