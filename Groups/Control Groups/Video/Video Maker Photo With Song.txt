App Description
Just select several pictures, Video Maker will recreate a cool video in seconds for you. You can DIY the video in your own style, by picking animation mode you like best, editing your video with sticker, text and frames etc.

Video Maker is the most popular app for video with many amazing filters & effect that make your trip, birthday, festival memorable!
It's can easily create videos with your own photos, add your favorite music track, write text or name, apply various frames and add stickers.

You just need to pick up photos and music you love, Also you may Arrange The Photos in Different order with the Perfect Background, music and Animation, Text, Sticker to create a video of your own. With this app, you can create lyrical video status from photos. You can make creative music video with our awesome transition effects.
