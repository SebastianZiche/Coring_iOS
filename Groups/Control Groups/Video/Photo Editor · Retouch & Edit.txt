App Description
#1 photo editor & pic collage maker on mobile to share your best memories! Use more than a 1000 fonts, stickers and layouts to transform any picture into a masterpiece!

° Clipping - Cut out photos and erase backgrounds in one tap

° Magazine-level photo touch up and editing

° Effects - Predefined effects such as Glitch, chromatic, duotone, texture and many more

° Unlimited undo & redo, go back to every change you made

° Full fledged photo editor that puts the power of pro photo editing in your hand

° Filters - Dozens of filters for a professional look

° Lighting - Add lighting, enhance color and contrast

° Text - 100’s fonts and text effects

° Draw over your image

° Rotate & Crop the image to include only parts you need.

° Frames - More than 30 frames that add a pro look to any photo or portrait
