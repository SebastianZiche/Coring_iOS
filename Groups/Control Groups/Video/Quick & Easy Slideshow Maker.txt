App Description
Make beautiful slideshows with music in a snap! Use one of your own songs or pick one of our own. When it comes to making slideshows from your phone, it's best to KEEP IT SIMPLE! Create beautiful customized slideshows in just a snap!

Step 1: Add Photos
Step 2: Add Music
Step 3: Export Video (saves to your gallery)

Thats it- your video is now ready to be shared on Facebook, Instagram, Youtube, Instagram, Email, and more with just a single click.

The default settings already have beautiful random transitions built in. If you want to further customize your slideshow, just tap on any photo where you can add a filter, a frame, or custom text if you'd like.
