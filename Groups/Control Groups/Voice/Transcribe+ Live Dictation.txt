App Description
Transcribe+ is a fully-integrated audio recording and note-taking app. Perfect for lectures, meetings, interviews and conferences – giving you an instant overview of all the important details.

Streamline your note-taking with the effortless note and audio syncing app for iOS devices such as iPhone and iPad.

STUDY SMARTER
Take your lectures to the next level. You’ll never miss any important point from your teachers again.

PERFECT FOR PROFESSIONALS
Boost your productivity with better, more efficient note-taking. Noted lets you stay fully engaged during long business meetings and interviews, then review and share specific points afterwards with your workmates.

NOTE TAKING
- Capture or import images as references
- Make the most of your notes with the beautiful annotation tools
- Time stamp

DICTATION
- Transform your voice into text using Dictation.
- Take notes even more effortlessly by speaking into the microphone

SYNC & SHARING
- All the files will be automatically saved in iCloud Drive

[ Upgrade to subscription version ]
Subscription version offers cloud sync and back up feature so that you can save all the info in our highly secured cloud services and share the same data in multiple iOS devices such as iPhone and iPad.
Upgrading to subscription version requires auto-renewal subscription. Payment will be charged to your Apple ID account at the confirmation of purchase.
The subscription automatically renews unless it is canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. Your account will be charged for renewal within 24 hours prior to the end of the current period. You can manage and cancel your subscriptions by going to your App Store account settings after purchase.
