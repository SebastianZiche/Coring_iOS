App Description
A hight quality multi-format voice recorder with file export function.The export function facilitate backup the recording files to the computer.

Main feature:
- recording.
- Background recording.
- Support Apple Watch, remote control phone record and the watch app can record audio in local.
- Backup recording files to computer(access record files through Wi-Fi or iTunes).
- Share recording with email.
- Multi-format data.(m4a,acc,caf,diff.wav).
- Playback recording function.
