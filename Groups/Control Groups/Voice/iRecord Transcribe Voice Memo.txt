iRecord: High-quality Recorder with Accurate Voice Transcription


iRecord is the perfect app for anyone looking to record high-quality voice recordings and transcribe them with advanced AI technology. 



With iRecord, you can:
* Record high-quality audio from phone, meetings, and lectures with ease, even in noisy environments or from a distance.
* Transcribe your recordings into text with high accuracy, thanks to our advanced AI techniques that won the championship of the International Automatic Speech Recognition Challenge in 2021.
* Export your recordings and transcripts into multiple formats, including Word and PDF, for easy sharing and collaboration.
* Share your recordings and transcripts via messaging apps like WhatsApp and Messenger, making it easy to collaborate with colleagues and friends.


iRecord is packed with features that make it the perfect tool for recording and managing your voice recordings and transcripts. 

Some of our standout features include:
* Live transcription: Our app can transcribe audio in real-time, making it easy to take meeting notes or keep track of lectures.
* Automated transcription: You can set up iRecord to automatically transcribe your recordings as soon as they're finished, saving you time and effort.
* Import & Export: Import from any apps, transcribe and export transcripts into different formats.
* Editing tools: Our app includes editing tools that  automatically capitalize, punctuate, and break paragraphs, as well as allow you to adjust and refine your transcripts after they've been transcribed.
* Speaker identification: iRecord can identify and label different speakers in your recordings, making it easy to follow along and understand who's speaking.
* Language support: iRecord can transcribe audio in a wide range of languages, making it a great tool for multilingual users.


Security and privacy are a top priority for us. Your data is confidential, and we take great care to protect it. You have full control over your data and can delete it from your device at any time.
