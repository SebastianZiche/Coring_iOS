App Description
Sing karaoke for FREE at Vsing.



[New ways to make friends]

Our application is developed by genius technicians, who bring amazing recording sound effects to the software. Be absorbed in your music by singing in a private room, getting connected with others via chat message, or sending your friends gifts to show how much you like their voices.



[An innovative platform to show your talent]

No need to hide your talent since Live Karaoke allows you to show your capabilities without worrying about finding a place to shine. Don’t underestimate your talent for singing! You have the potential to be a STAR!!



[Hi-def sound effect]

Thanks to our amazing technology, studio class recording quality is no longer a dream for our beloved users; ALL you need is a set of earphones to enjoy.



[Vast Library of Free HD Songs]

Billions of free HD songs for genres like Rock, Pop, Rap, R&B, Electronic, and Country Music are available to our users!

Timely additions of trending songs give you plenty of songs to sing.



[A live party sweeping the globe]

You are free to host a karaoke live party that crisscrosses the world. From time to time, you may have new visitors in your room, and when you aren’t in your room feel free to visit other rooms to hangout!



[Mic Grab]

In Mic Grab, test your skills by seeing how many songs you can sing while competing with your friends. The one who grabs the fastest and sings the most accurately wins! Let's see who knows their stuff!



Isn’t it cool to express yourself, and meet new people all around the world through singing? Feel free to join and open up new paths to friendship!
