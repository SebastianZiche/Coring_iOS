App Description
Instant speech-to-text conversion
Transcription - converts video and audio to text almost instantly.
Automatically adds punctuation marks.

Ideal for interviews, lectures, meetings, voice memos, phone calls, and videos.
Transcriptions can be export as PDF, DOCX, TXT, and RTF.

Transcriptions are automatically syncing between iPhone, iPad, and Mac.
You can start the transcription from your iPhone and read it on your iPad or Mac.

Support 120 languages ​​and dialects.
It will help you recognise a foreign speech.

The first 15 minutes are FREE!
If you like, you can buy extra time or subscribe.

Subscription gives:
● +3 hours monthly (unused time does not burn out and transfers to the next month)
● Unlimited cloud storage of audio files
● Export to PDF, DOCX, RTF
