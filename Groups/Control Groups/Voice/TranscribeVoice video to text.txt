App Description
Kaka Transcribe App can transcribing videos,voice,speech,record,meeting into text. Transcribe provides quality, readable transcriptions with just a tap of a button.It also can translate 130+ languages, it make video/audio to translated text, subtitles and notes made easy.

Kaka Transcribe App, you can:
# Video to text, easily convert videos to text transcripts.
# Multi-language support,Transcribe and translate between English,Chinese, Japanese and 130+ languages.
# Record high-quality audio from phone, meetings, and lectures with easy.
#Transcribe your recordings into text with high accuracy.
# Live note - real-time transcribe.
#Export your recordings and transcripts into multiple Language.
#Export audio, export text to srt/txt/pdf format.
#Share your recordings and transcripts via messaging apps like WhatsApp and Messenger, making it easy to collaborate with colleagues and friends.
