App Description
Transcribe your voice memos and videos with ease using Speech to Text - AI Dictation. Our advanced speech recognition technology provides high-quality transcriptions that are easy to read and edit.



Main Features:

• Auto-transcribe text while recording

• Import audio and video files for transcription

• Highlight key points for easy review

• Punctuate, capitalize, and break paragraphs automatically

• Differentiate between speakers

• Search text to quickly locate important information

• Playback at adjustable speeds

• Edit text to correct errors

• Convert recordings to various formats: MP3, M4A, AIFC, WAV, and more

• Transcribe in real-time with high accuracy (when online)

• Export to png, txt, word, and other formats

• Supports 30+ languages and dialects

• Import files from other apps and Dropbox

• Export raw text to your favorite text editing app

• Synchronize unlimited files with our app

• No ads!
