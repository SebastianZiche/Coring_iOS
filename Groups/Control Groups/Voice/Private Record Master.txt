App Description
Recording has never been easier!


A modern, contemporary design that makes it easy to record and share personal notes, family moments, class lectures, work meetings, presentations and interviews. In addition, it can help you learn foreign languages ​​easily.


Easy to record:

Background recording, automatic recording, multiple format selection, custom recording plan, how to record how to record.


Listen to it:

Create your own playlist, customize the play mode, play time and playback speed, and listen to what you want to hear.


Good to share:

A variety of sharing methods, easy to share with family and friends.

Support social media share, mail, iCloud, Wi-Fi file sharing, iTunes file sharing, etc.
