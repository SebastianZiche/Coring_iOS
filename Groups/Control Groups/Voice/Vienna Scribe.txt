App Description
Vienna Scribe is a next-generation speech to text service. It delivers accurate transcripts to your phone in seconds.

Using the latest transformer-based machine learning models running on specialized hardware, we deliver much more accurate results than other services.

Our audio engine is based on Whisper, by OpenAI. It’s the most accurate speech-to-text model on industry-standard benchmarks. For details see: https://cdn.openai.com/papers/whisper.pdf

More Accurate & More Reliable
• Lowest WER (word error rate) across industry standard benchmarks
• Supports over 70 Languages (detected automatically)
• High background noise tolerance


Many Uses
• Record meeting notes
• Transcribe interviews, podcasts, lectures, and videos
• Use your voice to send an email or text message

Total Privacy
• We do not collect any personal data
• Audio and transcripts are stored on your device
• No ads

Import & Export
• All audio files are supported
• Import from Apple Voice Memos
• Share transcript to any app

Multilingual
• Choose “Multilingual” model
• Over 99 languages supported
• Automatically switch between languages in the same recording

Version 1.5 (Sep 1, 2023)
    New Features:
    - Paragraphs: Transcripts now split into natural paragraphs.
    - Auto Transcribe: Recordings transcribed automatically.
    - Improved Accuracy: Enhanced model yields higher accuracy.

    Bug Fixes:
    - Better automatic language detection.
    - Resolved an issue where incoming phone calls might hide recordings. Missing files are labeled as "Recovered Recording".
    - Fixed lock screen audio scrubbing issue.
    - Miscellaneous bug fixes and enhancements.
