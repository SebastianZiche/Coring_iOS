App Description
Sing the greatest hits of artists like Ariana Grande, Harry Styles, The Weeknd, and many others!

WHY CHOOSE KARAOKE ONE?
Do you feel like you could be the next Lady Gaga? Karaoke One gives you the chance to show off your talent, have fun with your friends, climb the weekly charts and win amazing prizes by challenging artists from all over the world! All you need is your smartphone and your voice!

HOW DOES KARAOKE ONE WORK?
Karaoke One is an absolute must-have for anyone who loves to sing! Free song updates every month make this application the best you can find on this app store!

Karaoke One is quick and easy:
1. Sign up on the app, it's very easy, all you need is a few clicks
2. Choose from our catalog the song you want to sing
3. Have fun recording your performance
4. Publish your video and show your talent!


TEST YOUR TALENT WITH THE GREATEST HITS: choose your favorite song between pop, hip hop, trap, rock, rap, country and more, in order to voice your talent! Search into our catalog or choose from our playlists at your disposal and challenge your friends!

CLIMB THE GLOBAL RANKINGS: with Karaoke One you’ll be able to become a true international star and climb the weekly and monthly charts by showing off your musical talent and challenging artists from all over the world!

RECORD AND PUBLISH YOUR PERFORMANCE: record and publish your video and in a few seconds you’ll prove your talent to everyone!


Download Karaoke One and become the star you've always dreamed to be!
