App Description
Mobile recording studio in your hands. Use your device as real microphone to record and edit any sound you want.

◆ THIS SOUND STUDIO INCLUDES:

• Various microphones with original sound and specifications
• Up to 10 tracks recording: each sound - its own place
• Notes for lyrics with auto scroll
• Pop Filter for removing unwanted overtones
• Audio signal’s visualization: too loud - red alert
• Volume mixer
• Simple audio editor: fast editing without any other apps
• Real-time pitch changer
• 2x volume booster & amplifier



◆ FEATURES WHICH MAKE THIS APP THE ONE YOU NEED

• Duplication in headphones: you can clearly hear what you’re recording
• Active noise cancellation during recording
• Built-in mic’s record choice: bottom, front or rear
• 3D effect: surround-sound thing
• Demo tracks: try all features
• Unlimited audio recording: only HQ
• iTunes / Files uploads: add any sound from your library
• Saving projects: Coming back to recording anytime
• Informative knowledge base for exploring the various functions of the app

◆ UNDOUBTEDLY COOL AUDIO EFFECTS

• 12 bands equalizer with presets
• Reverb + a bunch of «spaces» presets
• Delay
• Compressor
• Destroyer
• Pitch & Speed
• Choir effect
• Stereo Expander

Audiobus support: Now you can stream live audio directly to other Audiobus-compatible apps!

Fast and easy way to record, edit and boost audio.
