App Description
Transcriptor is a simple and useful voice recording and transcribing app.

* Transcribe from new recording or audio file
* Save and share in TXT or PDF format
* Copy to clipboard your transcriptions
* Edit your transcriptions
* Supported languages: English, German, Spanish, French, Simplified Chinese, Traditional Chinese, Italian, Japanese, Korean, Portuguese, Croatian, Czech, Arabic, Danish, Hindi, Indonesian, Thai, Ukrainian, Polish, Romanian, Dutch and Finnish
