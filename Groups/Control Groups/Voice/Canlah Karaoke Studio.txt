App Description
Ever thought of recording in a studio, but gave up because it is expensive? Now your dreams will become a reality with our Can Lah! Karaoke Studio app.

Karaoke singing at its best with superior audio and video effects, freestyle singing in Open stage and much more.

Join today and invite others to collab with you.

No expertise needed - singing made easier and exciting!

This is a platform for those who are creative. Yes! Open stage is all for those who want to show case their creative talents be it freestyle singing, singing with music loops, acting to name a few.

Features:
- Sing karaoke with moving highlighted lyrics in the language of your choice
- Invite other users to join your songs and join other invites
- Sing with just audio or add your own video
-Correct your mistakes by going back while recording.
- Save time by just by skipping other user part by forwarding.
- Enjoy studio like audio effects
- Apply special video effects
- Want to showcase your photos? Create your photo album and add to your audio recording for a slide show
- Don't have time to type a comment? You can add voice comments
- Use our Open Stage tracks to
- sing to your own tune
- sing and play with your instrument
- show your acting talents
- do anything you want to show off
- You can also use our music loops to create your own compositions.

And many more exciting features in the pipeline!

Bring out the musician in you!
As always, keep singing! keep exploring
