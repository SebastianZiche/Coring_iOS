App Description
Voice Recorder is a recording app designed for simple operation. The app improves the user-friendliness of standard voice memo apps through background recording & playback as well as integration with other apps.

Main functions
・Background recording & playback
・Changing playback speed (0.5 - 2.0x)
・Integration with other apps
・Changing names of created files
・Sending files by email

Recorded file specifications
・File format is Wave
・Sampling rate is 44,100 Hz
