App Description
Use your smartphone as a microphone on your computer.
Currently working with Skype, Teams ... and latest game like MW2.

You do not have a microphone to communicate with friends or other players?
1°) Download the installer at https://www.ez-mic.com.
2°) Plug your device into your desktop using your charging cable or using your wireless network.

Compatible with Windows 10 & 11.
