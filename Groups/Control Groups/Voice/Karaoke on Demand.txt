App Description
Karaoke On Demand: Sing Your Heart Out



Unleash your inner superstar with Karaoke On Demand! Sing along to thousands of hit songs, from chart-toppers to timeless classics. Whether you're in the mood for today's top tracks or a trip down memory lane, we've got you covered.



Key Features:



Vast Karaoke Library: Explore thousands of note-perfect karaoke songs, each accompanied by vibrant MOTION backgrounds.



New Songs Weekly: Stay in tune with the latest hits - we add new songs every week.



Search Made Easy: Find your favorite songs by artist, title, song key, era, or explore our themed collections.



Multi-Genre Magic: Belt out decades of hits spanning Pop, Country, Rock, R&B, Hip Hop, Latin, and more!



Sing Anywhere: Whether you're at home or on the go, Karaoke On Demand is your ticket to singing fun for all ages and moods.



Special Categories:



Worship: Lift your spirits with soulful worship songs.



Holiday: Celebrate the season with festive favorites.



Broadway: Feel the drama and excitement of Broadway in your living room.



Games & More:



Karaoke Games: Challenge your friends to karaoke games and test your music trivia knowledge.



Free Songs: Enjoy a selection of FREE songs to kickstart your karaoke journey.



Chromecast Support: Take the stage on your big-screen TV for a living room sing-along experience.



Kid-Friendly: Share sing-along fun with your kids, featuring traditional nursery rhymes and songs from children’s movies (bonus: it's great for reading practice!).



Get Started:



Free Trial: Sign up for a FREE TRIAL and dive into the karaoke world.



Subscription Options: Choose from various subscription plans to start after your 3-Day FREE trial. Pricing varies.



Auto-Renewal: Subscription auto-renews, but you can cancel anytime, at least 24 hours before the trial period ends.
