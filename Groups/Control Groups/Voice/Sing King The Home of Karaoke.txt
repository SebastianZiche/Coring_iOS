Sing King - Sing It Your Way with our BRAND NEW Game Mode (beta) & our original Classic Karaoke Mode!

Non-stop, free karaoke! Sing wherever, whenever and enjoy *unlimited* full-length Karaoke videos for FREE. Be one of the 20 million that enjoy Karaoke with Sing King every month! With the very latest chart hits added daily, discover Karaoke to your favourite new songs on Sing King before anywhere else. 

*NEW* Introducing Our Karaoke Game (beta)!

* Sing along to your favourite tracks and be scored on your performance *
* Earn up to 5 stars + a special medal on a song and climb the leaderboards *

The #1 YouTube karaoke channel is now available as an app! Sing King is the world’s biggest Karaoke community. Be Swift. Have Styles. Go Grande. Head West. And, most importantly, Sing It Your Way!

SING KING PREMIUM SUBSCRIPTION : 

Sing your heart out! Go PREMIUM to get ad-free playback for an uninterrupted Karaoke experience and the ability to download content to enjoy with no connection needed! 

Sing King Premium is available as a subscription (weekly, monthly or yearly) or for limited fixed periods (such as our 48 hour Party Pass).

All subscriptions are on an auto-renewal basis unless cancelled 24 hours prior to the end of any free trial or subscription period. Unused portion of a free premium trial period, if offered, will be forfeited when the user purchases a subscription during the free trial.

Payment will be charged to your iTunes account and therefore to cancel go to your Apple ID account settings.
