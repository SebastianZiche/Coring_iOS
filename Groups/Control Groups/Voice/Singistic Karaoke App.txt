App Description
Is singing your first love? Do you want to stand out in the league of professional singers? Singistic is the online karaoke app for iOS users. Having great UI and an exhaustive range of superhit Hindi, English, Bangla, and Urdu songs, it is an absolute delight for music lovers! Install the best music player for iOS devices to get lost in the world of music for free, away from all concerns. Now, you no longer need to waste your time looking for the most user-friendly karaoke app. Just a one-time login gives you access to thousands of songs across multiple genres, artists and languages. Install the app to tune into bliss!

Features of the Best Music Recording App for iPhone
Sing in sync with lyrics
Isn’t it annoying when you forget the lyrics to your favourite song while you sing it? With Singistic, this will never happen.

Get Musical Guidance
If you do not remember the lyrics or melody by heart, simply select the ‘Guide’ option and practice as many times as you want to.

Record Whenever You Want To
Once you install the karaoke app, you can use it anywhere and anytime at your convenience.

Pitch it Up
You can change the pitch or tempo of the song, transform or customize as per your like.

Record in a Format of Your Choice
If you’re camera conscious and would rather want to record only the audio of your performance, Our song recording app allows you to do so too ( But we highly recommend that you record a video. After all, your listeners must recognize the face behind the talent).

Apply Studio Effects
Want your song to have the effect of having been recorded in the studio? Add the echo or reverb effect in the best music recording app.

Get Access to an Exhaustive Library
Just one tap will make you enter a world of music that you have never explored before. Thousands of Hindi, English, Bangla and Urdu songs are now available at your fingertips.

Spread the word about your talent
After recording your favourite track, share the audio or video recording of your favourite karaoke songs with your friends and family across social media.

Always up-to-date musical library
Find all the latest hits and trending music albums across all four languages on the best music recording app.
