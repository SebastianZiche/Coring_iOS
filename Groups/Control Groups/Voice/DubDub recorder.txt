App Description
This is a simple multi-recording app.

Record a song you singing and sounds you playing, and mix down your sound.

You can export to your sound to Camera Roll as a movie, and also you can import sound from movie in Camera Roll.

Features:
1.Create a new record.
• Record sound with a microphone.
• Import sound from a movie in Camera Roll.
* Free version is up to two records.

2.Mix
• Record sound with a microphone for mix.
• Play recorded sound.
• Add reverb effect
• Play sound for mix.
• Send sound to another app.
• Save sound as a movie.

3.Copy
• Copy record.
• Mix and copy record.
