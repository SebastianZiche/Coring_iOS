App Description
Introducing the ultimate transcription tool for your iOS device! With our app, you can easily transcribe any recorded audio into text with just a few taps. Whether you're a journalist, student, or just someone who needs to transcribe audio on the go, our app has got you covered.

With support for 57 languages, you can transcribe audio in almost any language you need. Plus, our app is incredibly accurate, so you can trust that your transcriptions will be as precise as possible.

We also understand the importance of keeping your recordings secure, which is why our app allows you to protect your recordings with biometrics. This means that only you can access your recordings and transcriptions, giving you peace of mind that your sensitive information is safe.

Our app supports a wide range of audio formats, including m4a, mp3, webm, mp4, mpga, wav, and mpeg, so you can transcribe any audio file you have.

Don't waste any more time transcribing audio manually. Download our app today and experience the ease and accuracy of automated transcription!
