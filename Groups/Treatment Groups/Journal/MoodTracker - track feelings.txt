App Description
With Mood Tracker, you can easily track how you feel on a daily basis.

Here are some great features that Tip Tracker offers:
• Visualize your mood by year, month, or week with built-in graphs.
• Find out your most common moods.
• Quickly & easily log your moods.
• Create an account to keep your data safe, and access from multiple devices.

Optionally, create an account to access your data from other phones, and to keep your data safe.
