App Description
Mindmix is a simple but powerful tool for developing mindfulness and self-awareness. The Mindmix app helps you build a simple practice of checking in with your feelings and intentions. Just a couple of taps a day can help grow your self-awareness and reduce stress. Try it for yourself - it's totally free.
