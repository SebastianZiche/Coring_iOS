App Description
Seneca Go is your interactive digital companion for day-to-day tools and activities to sustain your leadership journey after a Seneca event. Custom-tailored content, stories, and interactive tools are built-in to help you practice Seneca behaviors on a daily basis.

Key Features:
Grow your mindset through unique content made specifically for your change journey.
Stay committed and practice discipline through micro-commitments.
Explore content the way you want - stories, videos, activities, and more!
Introspect daily with journals and prompts.
Gather feedback from your peers to help build self-awareness.
Earn badges and achievements along the way!
