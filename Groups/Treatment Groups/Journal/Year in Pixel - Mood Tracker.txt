App Description
Pixel in Years, lets you track your moods with colors and see years summary on one page with just a few taps. No typing required!

- There are 5 different types of mood and there is one color for each.
- You choose it for everyday depending on your mood.
- At the end of the year, you will be able to see your past years mood with colors.
- There are several different color palettes in the settings section. More palettes on the way!
