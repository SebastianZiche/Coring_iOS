App Description
Unlock the full potential of your past, present, and future with Audio Diary, your personalised, secure wellness tool. Our AI-powered diary actively listens, making it easy for you to intelligently chronicle your precious moments, explore your thoughts in a meaningful way, and set goals.

Features!

Audio Diary will ask you thoughtful questions about your previous entries.

Mood graph: See your mood changes over time.

Diary Lock: Lock your diary with your own custom PIN.

Home Screen Widget: Add a quick entry widget to your homescreen as a reminder to journal!

Choose your theme. Customize your diary by choosing between 8 custom themes.

Share your entries and AI analysis with friends using secure link sharing! Enable diary sharing with one click and easily turn it off any time.

Random entries: Every day, you get a new random entry to listen to from your past.

Search through entries: You can search through all of your entries from the Diary page.

Automatic goal setting: Our AI will listen to what you said and automatically give you goals to keep you on track!

Photos: Add up to five photos each day to enrich your memories.

Secure & Private: We take your privacy seriously. Your data is encrypted and stored with bank-grade security to ensure your personal reflections remain confidential, fostering a safe space for self-expression.

Multi-platform: Download our mobile app and sync your diary across multiple platforms.

Export feature: Export your entire diary at any point, on free or on premium!

Goal-Oriented Mindfulness: Set and track meaningful goals to achieve personal growth. Audio Diary will remind you about them to help keep you accountable.

Voice Journaling: The power of your voice is incredible. Express yourself effortlessly through voice recordings that capture your emotions, thoughts, and experiences with unparalleled authenticity.

Daily Gratitude: Transform your mindset with expressing daily gratitude that uplift and inspire, helping you combat negativity, self-doubt, and the effects of depression.

Stress Relief: Harness the power of self-reflection to soothe your mind, alleviate stress, and cultivate inner peace.

Track Your Mood: Use our mood tracker to monitor your mood over time with insightful analytics and graphs, allowing you to celebrate your successes and adjust your goals as needed. Track your mood with our mood tracking feature.

Join Audio Diary Today: Experience the future of well-being, embrace mindfulness, and conquer mental health challenges with confidence. Download Audio Diary now and take the first step toward a brighter future.


We packed the basic version with as much functionality as we could, and have the following features for Premium users.

Longer entries

More gratitude prompts.

Idea log

Dream journal.

Memory tracker

AI analysis: Just talk, and our AI will analyse and summarise your day.

Automatic goal setting: Our AI will listen to what you said and automatically give you goals to keep you on track!


Why Audio Diary?

Audio Diary is a fun and simple app that listens to you. Just talk, and our AI will listen, entitling your days and organising your diary so it makes looking back over it a pleasure.

Transform your life, one entry at a time. Your journey to self-discovery and mental well-being starts here.
