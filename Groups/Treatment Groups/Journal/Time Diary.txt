Time is a great diary app, designed to record activities, experiences, thoughts and ideas throughout a day and sync data across all your devices. It lets you organize your created diary / lentries or notes from the past in the easiest way.Powerful search helps to find diary entries by any keyword in the title or text and filter results by date, folder and tags
