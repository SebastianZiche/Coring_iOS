App Promotional Text

App Description
Handy Translate is the preferred application for translating text, voice and images. Support translation in multiple languages. Help users learn language and communicate without barriers, and high-speed and accurate translation brings users a new experience. At the same time, it has exquisite wallpapers for users to choose and use, so as to make their mobile phone screen take on a new look.
We support translation in English, German, Russian, French, Portuguese, Japanese, Korean, Turkish, Ukrainian, Spanish, Italian and Chinese.
*Voice translation: use microphone to input voice and translate (support most languages)
*Text translation: The translation can be read aloud (support most languages)
*Instant camera translation: aim your camera at the text in the image and translate it immediately (support most languages)
*Quick detection: quickly identify the language to be translated
*High quality: accurate identification of background programs to reduce translation errors
*Collection translation: click the collection icon to save words and phrases for future reference
*Wallpaper selection: high-definition exquisite wallpaper, new clothes for mobile phone screen
Translation is so simple！
