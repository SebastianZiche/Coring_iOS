App Description
Speak Translator Supports more than 100 languages such as English, Japanese, French, Russian, Spanish, Portuguese, etc.; supports text translation, document translation, voice translation, to meet your all-round translation needs, and there are desktop widgets to help you record completed translations, Make your work more efficient.

【The main function】

-Text translation: supports 104 languages, input text can be translated, it is a powerful assistant for your work.

-Document translation: Support word, pdf and other document translation without entering text, quickly and efficiently translate documents, and obtain high-quality translations in real time.

-Speech translation: No need to enter text, just say what you want to translate, you can get accurate results and make your communication more fluid.

-Desktop component: The text widget can help you record the translated content at any time, allowing you to view the translated content anytime, anywhere.
