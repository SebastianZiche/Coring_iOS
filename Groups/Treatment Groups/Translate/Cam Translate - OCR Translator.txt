App Description
Cam Translate uses the latest in-depth neural network of large language model (LLM), combined GPT with cloud image recognition technology, to recognize text in images with high accuracy and to translate texts in multiple languages.
When you travel, street signs, books, recipes, readily take a photo, you can translate the language you need.
Cam Translate App also has speech recognition capabilities, using voice-blur technology, easily identify the voice content, completely liberate your hands!
