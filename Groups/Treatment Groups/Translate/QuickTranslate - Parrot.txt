App Description
If you're traveling to a new place, or trying to learn a new language, "Quick Translate" will help you overcome language barriers.

-With the voice translation feature, you can dictate what you want to translate and have it read aloud by the app or read on your screen.

-With camera translation, you can scan any picture or text with your camera to translate it into your language.

-You can enter the phrase you want to translate to get the result in text format, or to hear the correct pronunciation.
