App Description
Easily and quickly translate over 110 languages.

Come and use it now!

Communicate smoothly and conveniently with people all over the world!

"Parrot Translator" is the perfect app to translate languages and learn correct pronunciation.

Whether you are traveling to a new place or trying to start learning a new language, with "Parrot Translator", language is no longer a barrier to communication.



• Text translation

Translate text into more than 110 languages. Enter the phrase you want to translate to receive the results in text format or to hear the correct pronunciation.

• Speech translation

It can recognize multiple international languages and quickly translate to the language you want.

• Use offline

During the translation process, no internet is required, offline translation can be used even if the mobile phone has no signal.

• Unlimited use and no subscription required.
