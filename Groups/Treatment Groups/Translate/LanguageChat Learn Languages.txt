Learn Spanish, French, German, Italian, Russian, Portuguese, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Turkish, Dutch, Danish, Swedish, Polish, Norwegian, Arabic, Hindi or even English by chatting, listening and speaking.

Give LanguageChat a try and see things yourself.



LanguageChat App: A Combined Cloud AI Tech To Help You Learn To Speak 16 languages 10x Faster. LanguageChat App wants to change the way you should learn a language forever! You could be addicted to it.

With LanguageChat App, Language learning is a lot easier, faster and fun.

LanguageChat is fun and easy to use for anyone that wants to learn a new language. Use your native language as a primary guide and start learning today! In three simple steps, you are in with fun. Absolutely the easiest way to learn a language with a higher language retention.
