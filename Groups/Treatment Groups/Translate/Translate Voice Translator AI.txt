App Description
Translate Voice Translator AI App- Fast voice and text translation worldwide.

Want to travel abroad?
Trying to start learning a new language or upskilling?
Want a language teacher ready to teach?

Don't worry, enjoy the most accurate Translate Voice Translator AI - GREAT CHOICE FOR TRANSLATION! Supports all popular languages.

Users can effectively communicate with people who speak different languages without the need for interpreters or language specialists around. Express yourself as if you were a native speaker! Moreover, you can order food, ask for directions or speak to a professional customer. Chat with anyone, anywhere. Turn your smartphone into your personal multilingual translator!

===========
KEY FEATURE
===========
> Voice-to-Voice: Communicate with each other in two-way native language in real time.
> Text-to-Text: Translate entire text accurately and easily with advanced support for grammar and context.
> Voice-to-Text: Speak and translate into text-based translation. Hands-free approach when busy working.
> Text-to-Voice: Instantly convert voice from typed text.

The translator supports the following 100+ languages:
English , French , Spanish , Arabic , Chinese, Portuguese, Urdu, German, Hindi, Indonesian, Vietnamese, Malay, Italian, Japanese, Korean, Thai, Turkish, etc.
....with more languages supported!

USER MANUAL:
> Select language you want to translate
> Enter text or tap the voice button
> Get high quality translations
Everything is done on one screen!

ADVANCED FEATURES:
- Record and save your translations to use whenever you need.
- Easily share translations.

PREMIUM REGISTRATION:
Download to use with 3-day free trial grants access to all pro features. Surely there will be attractive special features waiting for you. No commitment. Cancel anytime.

It is the first choice for users who want a reliable and efficient translation application on their IOS devices. All you need to do is download the Translate Voice Translator AI App - Translator Expert in seconds with this translator.
