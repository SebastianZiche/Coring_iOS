App Description
Are you ready to start exploring the fantastic world, Nova Translator is an excellent assistant.
Nova Translator can help you with the following:Supports translation into over 50+ languages.Text translation: you can get accurate results with texts.Voice translation: real-time voice interaction will make your experience better.OCR translation: as long as you select a picture, you can get the translation result of the text on the picture.
No matter what work or study, this intelligent translation application will bring you many surprises. Let's try it!
