App Description
Translatesy provides the most accurate translation and helps you travel seamlessly or learn new languages every day. Translate text, voice, camera photos, screenshots or dialogues with ease.



Voice translator is an easy way to communicate between people who speak different languages. Just click on the microphone icon and say words or sentences in your native language and they will be translated into the language of your choice. You can hold a conversation in any language , use the voice translator, translate different texts, and use the "Speak" button.



Now you can easily translate from/into 58 different languages at any time using your mobile device. Simply enter the phrase you want to translate, select the original language and the target language and press the "Done" button. The translation will be displayed in the window below the text, which you can copy immediately to any other application, email, SMS.
