App Description
Seamlessly transcribe and translate text in real-time, unlocking communication in a wide range of languages, even in offline mode for your convenience. Experience personalised text-to-speech functionality, with a diverse library of voices to suit your preferences. You may use your own voice for text-to-speech! The Babelfish Translator is your ultimate linguistic companion, enabling efficient and accurate multilingual conversations for both personal and professional use, no matter where you are in the world.
