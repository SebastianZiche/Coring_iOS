App Description
Translate Now is the indispensable translation and dictionary app for voice & text translations in 100+ languages. Our Offline Mode allows you to use iTranslate abroad without having to pay expensive roaming charges.

FEATURES
• Get translations in over 100 languages.
* Apple Speech Recognition Technology support - real-time voice recognition significantly enhances the speed of your translations
* 117 languages for text translations
* 54 languages for voice translations
• Listen to translations in male or female voices.
• Switch between different dialects.
* Language detection for text-to-text mode: if you don't know what the language of the source text is, select Detect language from the language list
• Phrasebook - With over 250 predefined phrases.
• Transliteration, Sharing, Favourites, History, and much more.

PRO FEATURES:
• Unlimited translations
• Instant camera translation
• Real time conversations
