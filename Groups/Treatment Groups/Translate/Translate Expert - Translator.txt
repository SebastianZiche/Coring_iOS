App Description
Translation - Based on Google's artificial intelligence translation technology, the translation is natural and smooth, and the quality of translation is high. Support the world's more than 100 languages, with voice, large-screen display, travel work essential translation artifact.

The main features include:
- Beautiful and intuitive user interface
- Translatable text in more than 100 languages
- Read aloud the result of the translation

Supported languages:
