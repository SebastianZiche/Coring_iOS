App Promotional Text
Translate text effortlessly, snap & translate images with OCR tech. Your all-in-one language companion!
App Description
Welcome to Easy To Read - Your Comprehensive Translation Assistant!



No matter where you are, Easy To Read is here to meet all your translation needs in daily life and communication.



Key Features:

1. Text Translation


- Supports translation of text in multiple languages, breaking down language barriers effortlessly.

- Precise and efficient translation based on industry-leading text translation services, ensuring accuracy and fluency.

2. Photo Recognition Translation


- Utilizes advanced OCR (Optical Character Recognition) technology for text recognition in images.

- Translate text from photos in real-time, providing a convenient and quick solution.

3. Daily Spoken Language Summary


- Records and summarizes everyday spoken language, helping you better understand and use languages in practical situations.

4. VPN
- Connect to a VPN to safeguard your data privacy and enhance your internet speed.
Security Assurance:

We are committed to protecting your privacy, ensuring a secure usage experience.

How to Use:

Download and install Easy To Read.

Select the desired function and start enjoying convenient translation services!

Easy To Read makes language no longer a barrier to communication, connecting you more closely with the world. Thank you for choosing Easy To Read!
