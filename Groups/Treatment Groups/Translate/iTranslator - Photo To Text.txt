App Description
Take a photo or Voice of anything you want to translate or edit & app will change it to text & translate to any language of your choice. Scan & edit a PDF, Invoice or CV & Export.

How nice would it be to Speak, Write and Understand any Language you want! Take a photo of an image you want to Translate and app will change it to text and translate it to any language of your choice.

Books, Menu, PDF, Invoice, emails or any text or voice you want to translate.
First FREE App with Camera, album, OCR and Voice Translations.
If you are going to job interview or having a business meeting and you want to apply with your CV, Use iTranslator to help you edit it before the interview. Edit your Invoices, Travel & Talk with foreign people using instant speech recognition. It is your guide and your dictionary.

Best Quality, Translation by camera, OCR & Speech Translation.

iTranslator is not like any Translated app, it detect & translate all the languages in the world with best quality and service.

If you are traveling to a new country, at a business meeting, in a public place where there are many signs such as airport, train stations, restaurants, street signs, books... Or other important information that is written in a language other than your own!
If you go to another country or talk to another foreigner, this app can help you translate instantly, translate images containing words or any file such as PDF or JPG into text, and add or remove information.
How nice it is to understand, speak, read or hear any language you want!!

How app Works?
1- Use the camera or Photo Library to get the photo or document then the app will simply detect the letters and change them to text using OCR Service.
2. Then It translates the detected text to any language you want, quick and secure.
3. Press on mic and speak your language and app will talk it into any other language you choose or let the others speak and you hear it in your own language.

Features:
- Unlimited Free Translations
- Instant OCR & translation
- Instant voice Translation with speech
- PDF scanner and editor
- Use Edit for unlimited Free translation.
- Copy Text to Clip board
- Text to speech
- Select photo from Gallery or take live one.
- Support all the Languages
- Easy to use interface.
- Light & Clear colors.

Please leave us a review to keep supporting you. ask for new features, we are here to keep you handy.
