App Description
Get your perfect companion for traveling and learning a new foreign language on the go.
Communicate easily with everyone without language barriers in any country!
Choose a preferred language and the app will automatically translate your voice or text.
Using your phone camera, instantly recognize and translate any text.

Features:

- Voice and text translator
Get ready-made voice and text translation in any language!

- Translation from the camera
Recognize and translate any text from menus, books, magazines, any pictures and photos using the camera

- Dialogue Mode
Speak a foreign language in real time using the dialogue mode

- Keyboard translator
An extensive keyboard with 60+ languages at your disposal will help you to stay in touch with all of your foreign friends across the globe.

- Offline mode
Use the translator without internet connection!


The app supports translation in the following languages:

Afrikaans, Albanian, Arabic, Azerbaijani, Basque, Belarusian, Bengali, Bulgarian, Catalan, Chinese (simplified), Chinese (traditional), Croatian, Czech, Danish, Dutch, English, Esperanto, Estonian, Filipino, Finnish, French, Galician, Georgian, German, Greek, Gujarati, Haitian Creole, Hebrew, Hindi, Hungarian, Icelandic, Italian, Indonesian, Irish, Japanese, Kannada, Korean, Latin, Latvian, Lithuanian, Macedonian, Malay, Maltese, Norwegian, Persian, Polish, Portoghese, Romanian, Russian, Serbian, Slovak, Slovenian, Spanish, Swahili, Swedish, Tamil, Telugu, Thai, Turkish, Ukranian, Urdu, Vietnamese, Welsh.


Additional info:
* Complete functionality may not be available for all languages.

** Free version of the app may have some limitations such as the number of translations in a day and other.
