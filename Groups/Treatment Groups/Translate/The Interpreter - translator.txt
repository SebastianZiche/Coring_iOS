App Description
The Interpreter Translator FREE aquires text from your voice or using the keyboard and in real time, translates it and reads it for you in one of the 70 available languages.
It's a text translator, translator by voice and is easy to use, small and fast.

Main features:

1- Listen
Recognizes what you say through the advanced vocal recognition software.
It uses voice recognition software of your device, available by the microphone icon on virtual keyboard (***).

2- Translation into more than 70 languages (*).
Unlimited and immediate online translation.

3- Read text
Automatic spoken interpretation into the desired language (**).

4- Keyboard use
In text field you can insert or modify the text to be translated by keyboard. You can also use this interpreter without the vocal recognition feature.

5- Translation sharing
Translation sharing of texts via social network, mail or other app without closing the application. You can move the text to any other application by using the Copy to Clipboard function.

6- Create documents from the translation
Translations can be exported and downloaded in Adobe PDF format for later reading. You can customize header, footer and font size of exported document.

7- Translation history
Every translation is recorded on device and is available off-line for later reading.
