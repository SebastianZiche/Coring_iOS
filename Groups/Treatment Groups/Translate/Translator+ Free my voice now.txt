App Description
Have you ever wanted or needed to communicate with someone who didn't speak your language?

Vocre breaks down the language barrier and brings us all closer - no matter where we are or what language we speak.

[App Features]

• Fun and easy way to have a conversation with someone who speaks a different language.

• Perfect travel companion. It works quickly, allowing you to understand -- and be understood almost anywhere on Earth.

• Gives people the ability to talk to anyone in any language. Our world is full of people worth talking to. Now, nothing is holding you back.
