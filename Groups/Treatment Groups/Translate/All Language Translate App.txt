App Description
Language Translator AI is a leading/professional Translator App to translate between 100 languages using features like text translator, voice translator, text to speech, language translator with English conversation. Free translation app allows you to translate between all languages. Free translation app is especially helpful when you are out of the country or in a place where you do not know the local language or somewhere foreign. Language Translator Free, Voice Text Translate All allows to speak and translate between multiple languages. Text and speech translator gives a fun and effective way to memorize vocabulary.

Multi language and voice translator, which allows you to take notes easily by speaking. Learn local/indigenous languages including all International languages. If you’re in a foreign place, no need to worry & just use language translator’s speak to translate functionality to translate words pronunciation of the local/foreign language. With this Language Translator Free- Voice Text Translate All, speak any words and voice language detector translator will convert spoken language to English or any foreign language to English and in this way you can translate all languages you want.

Our Camera Translation section offers unique interface and functionality to allow you to translate multiple words in a picture. Our machine learning algorithms identify different words and phrases in images and offer and organized and intuitive list of translations.
