App Promotional Text
Trulinco is the no.1 business communication app that provides real-time translation across 200+ languages during messages, calls, and documents & image text translation!
App Description
Trulinco is a full-fledged business communication app that provides real-time translation across
200+ languages during messages & calls with accuracy. It also allows document & image text
translation with accuracy, across 200+ global languages.

Our Mission:
Provide a high-tech platform to break the language barrier, and serves accuracy in translating
any documents, image text, message, voice calls or video calls. The focus is to establish
seamless & effective communication globally!

Our Vision:
To unite the people across the world irrespective of their languages.

Advanced Features:

Text Chat Translation
- Instant Chat with Real-time translation in your language.

Voice Call Translation
- Seamless Voice Call translation in your language.

Video Call Translation
- Smooth translation during Video Calls.

Group Call Translation
- Business meetings with people speaking different languages.

Image Text Translation
- Scan images for instant translation in your language.

Documents Translation
- Upload documents and get instant translation in your language.
