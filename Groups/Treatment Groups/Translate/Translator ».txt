App Description
Translator allows you to translate any sentence or phrase into 26 languages by the tap of a button. Translate by typing, using your voice or scanning text with your camera

- Translate into 26 languages
- Real-time Voice recognition
- Scan text with your camera
- Built-in Phrasebook
- Text-to-speech
- History feature
- Favorites
- Share your translations
- Attractive UI
