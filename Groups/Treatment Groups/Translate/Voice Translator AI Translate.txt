App Description
Voice translation was never so easy! With the power of the ChatGPT* API, Voice Translator allows for seamless communication worldwide. Instantly hear translated phrases on the go, making any conversation a breeze with our AI translator. Can’t read the menu items in a fancy foreign restaurant? Instant camera translation has got you covered!

Communicate freely at the airport, hotel, gas station, supermarket or any other place with our voice translator. Read street signs in a foreign country, translate manuals of your overseas purchases or the text of any picture you already have in your phone. Just put your personal AI translator into a pocket and use it for all your translating needs!

Voice Translator is an essential learning tool for kids and adults – learn new phrases and listen to the correct pronunciation.

Start using our voice translator now and forget about looking for the correct phrase in the dictionary – say any phrase and your interpreter will pronounce the translation out loud!

Features:
– Immediate translation of real-time conversations with the voice translator
– Use your camera for instant text translation
– Use AI Magic to choose а friendly, formal, or romantic tone for translated texts
– Translate texts from your photos and pics
– Translate as you type in any of your favorite messaging apps
– Extended offline phrasebook
– Language detection for text-to-text mode
– 100+ languages working in speech-to-speech and text-to-text modes
– You can either speak or type to translate your text
– Smart text-to-text mode
– Speed up your text input with typing hints
– Convert text from languages with non-Latin alphabet
– Copy, paste and share translations in a single tap
– Intuitive dialogue conversation interface of the voice translator
– Speak the translated phrase out loud
– Control the speed of voice
– Male and female voices available
– Human-to-Pet and Pet-to-Human modes (for entertainment purposes only). Translate your pet’s meow or woof into your language.
