App Description
Translate AI is the all-in-one translation app that makes it easy to communicate with anyone, anywhere.

It translates text, voice, photos, and websites in over 100 languages, even without an internet connection.



With Translate AI, you can:

Speak and translate in real time: Have conversations with people from all over the world, without having to learn their language.

Translate photos and images: Point your camera at a sign or menu, and Translate AI will instantly translate the text into your language.

Translate websites: Browse websites in any language, and Translate AI will automatically translate the content into your language.

Translate text and clipboard contents: Translate text from any app or website, or simply paste text from your clipboard into Translate AI to get the translation.

Translate AI is the perfect translation app for travelers, students, business professionals, and anyone else who needs to communicate across language barriers.
