App Description
The Translate Anything app is your best choice for translating any type of text, speech, photos, or objects in more than 50 worldwide languages.

We offer multiple input methods for your words or objects that need translations such as:

- TYPING or SPEAKING: Translate bilingual conversations on the fly

- PHOTOS: Take or import photos containing words or objects for the most accurate translations

- LIVE TRANSLATIONS: Translate text or objects instantly by just pointing the camera where needed

- OBJECT DETECTION: Use your photos and camera to take advantage of our cutting-edge objects detection system and receive words within objects translated for you immediately

You don't need to worry anymore about any kind of conversation in any language anywhere across the globe. We translate languages immediately and accurately!


We created the app in order for it to be very easy to use and to solve all your needs. You will only need to focus on the main subjects of your conversations from now on and not worry about any stressful translations.

Permissions Requested:

- Microphone for speach translations

- Camera and Photo Gallery for translating text and objects from your pictures


Download the Translate Anything app now and enjoy the powerful features that make conversing with friends, family and coworkers across the globe easy and fun! Take the stress out of translating anything and enjoy your conversations!
