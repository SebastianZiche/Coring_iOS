App Description
DeftPDF Document translation Instantly translates documents into 130+ languages. You can use our app to translate document in Pdf, Docx, Pptx, Xlsx, Txt, and Html formats.

here are the key benefits:
• Instantly translate and preserve the layout of any document format
• Support 130+ languages
• No registration required, no more hassle
• No watermarks.
• Large files supported! you can translate more than 500 pages
• Support Scanned PDF format: we use optical character recognition (OCR) technology to extract and translate text in the document with AI while retaining the original layout.

Pricing:
• 3 pages are free
• no subscription needed! pay as you go (price is calculated based on document page count)
• Note: there is NO free translation option for scanned document.
