App Description
Thanks for using our Voice Translator, you can use it to easily translate text, menus, signs, speech and conversation translation, fast and easy.

Our voice translator supports multiple languages, allowing you to communicate with people all over the world anytime, anywhere.

Our app features include:



· Text Translation: Accurately translate words, phrases and sentences.

· Voice translation: Voice input, automatic text conversion, no need for manual input.

· Picture translation: Translate with camera or pictures.

· AI translation:Use the current advanced large language model to provide you with a better and more accurate translation experience

The various translation modes we provide can help you quickly and accurately translate it into multiple languages around the world.

Thanks again for using our Voice Translator APP!
