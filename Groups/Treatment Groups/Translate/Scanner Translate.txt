App Description
Scanner Translate is a camera translation software for iPhone that translates business documents, menus, assignments or other images into the language you need with super fast speed.
- Support over 100+ languages
- Advanced character recognition technology
- automatic detection
- Offline translation

A good translation app is an efficient assistant in daily work or study. It can extract text from any printed source and translate it into more than 100 languages. The supported languages are:

How to use Scanner Translate:
1. Open the required app permissions: camera and photo album
2. Take a photo of the document you need to translate or select a photo in the album
3. Click the Scan button
The above three simple steps can easily get the desired results . Come and download the best scanning and translation app right now.
