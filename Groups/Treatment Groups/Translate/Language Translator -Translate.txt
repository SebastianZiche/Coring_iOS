App Description
This is a perfect Language Translator app with all related functionalities to provide users with a real-time translation aid. This is a text and voice translation app that helps translate text from photos (you can capture and translate text) and by voice as well. It brilliantly allows for translation from any language to up to 100+ languages. This app is a Real-time voice Language Translator for your everyday needs.

The live translator of it is best at instant translations using the live camera translator from inside the app. This is a Voice to Text translator & Text to Voice Language Translator app to bring ease into everyday communications with people from different regions of the world. You can change the language preferences and other settings of the app anytime you want.

Camera translator
Simply move to the camera translator tab and choose an image from the gallery to translate text from it or use a live camera translator for translating any current image. Also, use a scanner to scan any physical document and translate it instantly.

Voice Translator
This voice Language Translator app is so convenient to make voice translation from one to other multiple languages. You can play audio translated version of your query in seconds and copy and share it with anyone at the moment. Also, you can keep it maintained in the history or delete it as per your preferences.

Text to Voice translator
You can type to get the audio translation on the go. This is real-time multifunctional translation aid that anyone can require.

History Maintenance
Move to the history tab and get the recent translated versions to be viewed from there. Also, you can delete any translations from there if you don’t want them anymore.
