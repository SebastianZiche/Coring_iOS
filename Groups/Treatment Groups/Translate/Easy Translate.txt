App Promotional Text
Easy Translate is a quick & simple online translator for multiple languages. You can use it to translate text, conversation and scan words to translate from photos.
App Description
Easy Translate is a quick & simple online translator for multiple languages.

• Text translation translate between 26+ languages by typing or voice recording.
• Instant Camera Translation translate text in images by pointing your camera on.
• Conversation Translation a split-screen mode for two participants having a bilingual conversation.

Translations between 26+ languages are supported
English, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, French, Spanish, Thai, Arabic, Russian, Portuguese, German, Italian, Greek, Dutch, Polish, Bulgarian, Estonian, Danish, Finnish, Czech, Romanian, Slovenian, Swedish, Hungarian, Vietnamese
Some features are not available in all languages.
Internet connection is required.
