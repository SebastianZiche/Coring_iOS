App Description

** Lao Keyboard And Translator **

Lao Keyboard is the best app for Lao writing.

Lao keyboard app will allow you to write message, story, facebook posts, blogs, Emails in Lao language.

you will find eye catchy and user-friendly Lao keyboard to write Lao language.

Lao keyboard has easiest keypad to write Lao alphabets, letters and words.

you just type Lao words you can find suggestions of that Lao words automatically in this application.

The most exciting feature of this application is you can translate Lao word to any other language like English, Marathi, Lao, Lao and all other Indian languages as well as all international languages like Lao, Lao, Lao, Lao, Swedish etc....

Features :

* you can easily change English to Lao and Lao to English keypad using Lao Keyboard application.

* you can translate Lao words into any other Indian and International languages.

* You can edit the text and also change the colour of the font.

* It's also provide all the emoji icons

* Lao keyboard has special characters, icons and numeric so that user can easily write any Lao words very easy and effective manner.

* It’s free and easy to use.

* Lao Keyboard And Translator has also Lao Keyboard Extension.

* With the help of Lao Keyboard Extension you can write in Lao language in any application. You have add the Lao Keyboard Extension from your phone settings.


** Lao Translator **

The Lao Translator is the best online translator. It translate the Lao to other Indian languages as well as all international languages like Lao, Lao, Lao, Lao, Swedish etc….

Lao Translator app will allow you to translate message, story and you can share translated text to your friends and family bye Facebook, twitter and emails.

Features:

1. Online Lao language to Any other language translation.

3. Lao keyboard to type to text in Lao Language.
