App Description
Come and download the best translation app Hello Translator for your iPhone, no matter any text or picture, you can immediately get translations in more than 80 different languages.

► Features:
Recognize over fifty languages
Translated into over 80 languages
Advanced OCR technology
Support text, voice and camera translation
Edit, copy, share anytime

► How to use:
Select original language
Enter text, voice or take photos directly
Select the language to be translated
Click the translate button

Yes, it's that simple.
Hello Translator is a good tool in your life, no matter if it is menus, work documents or study materials, no matter where you are, when you need to translate, take out your iPhone, open Hello Translator, choose text, voice or OCR mode, input what you want to translate, and you can get the translation results immediately, and you will have unlimited use.
