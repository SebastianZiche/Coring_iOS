App Description
Rec translator is a free unlimited recording, smart translation application. It has excellent recording recognition services, supports multi-language real-time recording to text. It also has efficient translation functions such as photo translation, voice translation, etc. it meets various needs such as recording, voice conversion characters, character extraction, online translation, etc.

TOP FEATURES：
1、Record & Transcribe Live (the latest feature)
- Support real-time recording & voice to text, which can accurately and quickly convert the content of real-time recording&voice into text.
2、Photo translation
- Auto recognize and translate text in the photo from any language to any language. Menu, books, road signs… you can take photos at will and get translation results in time.
3、Real-time dialogue translation
- Just speak to the phone, say what you want to convey and get the real time translation.
4、Multilingual text translation
- Support more than 50 languages, you can translate the converted text of the recording&voice into various languages you need.
5、Free export and sharing
- It supports free editing and copying of recording text conversion results, so as to export and share them to e-mail, social software and other platforms.
