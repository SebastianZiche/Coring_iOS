App Description
Are you searching for an accurate translations app that you can rely on to provide support in over 100 languages and will show you the proper pronunciation too? How about being able to order professional human powered translations for all of your documents and be able to translate very large bodies of text within the same app? Well, then this is the app for you!

Day Translations Translator app connects you with the world.

HIGHLY ACCURATE INSTANT TEXT TRANSLATION
Day Translations is a world-class translations company, so we understand languages. Our premium translator will help you translate phrases fast and accurately,

*NEW* INTERPRETING FEATURE: SPEECH-TO-SPEECH TRANSLATIONS
Speak into the microphone and get an instant interpretation of what you’re saying in almost any language! This feature is especially helpful when you’re meeting with someone that speaks a different language than yourself. Understand exactly what they’re saying in an instant, and practice proper pronunciation of that language yourself.

*NEW* PRO FEATURE: HUMAN-POWERED TRANSLATIONS
If you need professional Human-Powered Translations for documents or other files, you can order the translations directly from the app using the "Pro" feature. Our app lets you sign up or login within seconds through our “Pro” button so that you may order you're a professional human powered translation in a wide variety of file formats.
You can even get an instant, FREE quote on human translation orders for your documents.


*NEW* PAID SUBSCRIPTION FEATURE: MACHINE-POWERED TRANSLATIONS
If you need large bodies of text translated accurately within a moments notice, you can subscribe to one of our paid models that best suits your needs. Our app lets you translate up to 450 thousand characters per day for only 22 dollars per month.


SUPPORT AND TRANSLATION IN OVER 100 LANGUAGES
The translation app provides translations in languages such as:
Afrikaans, Albanian, Amharic, Arabic, Armenian, Azerbaijani, Basque, Belorussian, Bengali, Bosnian, Bulgarian, Catalan, Cebuano, Chichewa, Chinese (Simplified), Chinese (Traditional), Corsican, Croatian, Czech, Danish, Dutch, English, Esperanto, Estonian, Filipino, Finnish, French, Frisian, Galician, Georgian, German, Greek, Gujarati, Haitian Creole, Hausa, Hawaiian, Hebrew, Hindi and many more.

USER-FRIENDLY UI
Our Free Translator App is incredibly easy to use, so you can share it with anyone without having to explain much.


Note: This app requires an internet connection. For document translations, visit our website www.daytranslations.com or sign up on the app.
