App Description
Universal Language Translator App is one of the best leading free translate/translator and free translation app, easily translate voice-to-voice conversations or text start in over 60+ (Plus) languages.

Voice Universal Translator, for speaking and understanding foreign languages

You speak in your language and the Voice Universal Translator translates vocally in another language and in real time what you said

With the "Just in Time" translation it works as a real interpreter so you won't have problems with foreign languages any more.

Main features:

1) It translates in about 60 languages
2) It translates your voice, your spoken text
3) Translations are done instantly with the "Just in Time" function.
4) You can listen the translated text
5) Speech Recognition to get the spoken text
6) Text to Speech to pronounce the translated words
7) You can share the translated text with other apps
8) You can copy&paste the translated text
9) It is totally free.
