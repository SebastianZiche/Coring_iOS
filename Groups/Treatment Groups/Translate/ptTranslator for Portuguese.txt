Text translation: Portuguese translation, Italian native language library, give you the most authentic Portuguese translation.
Photo translation: support Portuguese text recognition, mobile phone photo text recognition translation, efficient and accurate text extraction translation.

Translation collection: Portuguese learning, new words, difficult words, professional vocabulary can be collected and reviewed at any time.

Letter pronunciation: Portuguese 26 letter pronunciation table, online learning, real person pronunciation follow.
Common spoken language: select 100 sentences of daily spoken language, and 1800 spoken language and travel common words.
