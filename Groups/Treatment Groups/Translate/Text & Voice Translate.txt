App Description
With "Text & Voice Translate", you can communicate with people who do not speak you languages. Help you to communicate effectively in any corner of the world. Instantly speak in more than 30 languages!

Features:
* Apple Speech Recognition Technology, realtime voice recognition.
* Photo translation: Take photos for higher quality translations.
* 30 supported languages for text-to-text and voice to voice translations.
* Search histories.
* Speaks out translations. the app will instantly speak our translations.
* Share translations via email,message or social Network.

translations are supported between the following languages:
Arabic, Catalan, Chinese, Croatian, Czech, Danish, Dutch, English, Finnish, French, German, Greek, Hungarian, Indonesian, Italian, Japanese, Korean, Malay, Polish, Portuguese, Romanian, Russian, Slovak, Spanish, Swedish, Thai, Turkish, Ukrainian, Vietnamese
