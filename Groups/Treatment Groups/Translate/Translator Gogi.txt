App Description
Translator Gogi - The application recognizes speech and displays text on the screen and quickly translates it into the language you need. Very useful in medicine, for people with hearing loss.


Benefits of the Speech to Text & Translation app

• Application for people with hearing impairments

• You can record a visit to the doctor and send a text to your family

• Over 30 languages

• Font size adjustment

• If you do not understand what is being said, the application will record the speech into text and translate it


How to use our Translator:

1. Click the microphone icon.

2. Speak your language. The application will recognize your speech and display it in the text, and immediately translate it into the language you need


Advice. Hold your phone so that the microphone is facing the speaker. This will help the application to better recognize the text.


Important: The app will ask for permission to access the microphone and speech recognition. Please allow the application access.


The application can be used at a doctor's appointment. The recognized text can be sent to friends on social networks.


Our technology is not affiliated with Google.

Online speech recognition and translation takes place via an Internet connection and is available for all languages ​​that are in the application.
