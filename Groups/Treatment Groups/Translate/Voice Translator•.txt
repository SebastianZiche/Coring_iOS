App Description
Translator+ allows you to speak into your device and have your words translated in an instant! You can also easily translate text and voice in over 100 languages.

FEATURES
• Translate between over 100 languages.
• Voice command allows you to directly speak to your device for instant translation
• Translations in both text and voice
• Instantly share translations via text message or social media.
• History feature allows you to revisit previous translations.

Note: This App Requires and Internet Connection.
