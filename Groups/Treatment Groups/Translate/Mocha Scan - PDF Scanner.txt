App Description
Mocha Scan will turn your iPhone and iPad into an easy to use mobile document scanner.
Scan receipts, notes, book pages, newspaper articles, business cards ,etc.

- Scan a set of documents with the device camera.
- Save as PDF and JPG.
- Convert to TXT format using an OCR (optical character recognition) module on the device.
- Sign documents.
- Share, email and Print documents.
- Organize documents in folders on your local device.
- No subscriptions or fees to use the App. It is 100% free.
- Privacy: Image to text translation is processed locally on the device. Nothing will be send to an external server.
