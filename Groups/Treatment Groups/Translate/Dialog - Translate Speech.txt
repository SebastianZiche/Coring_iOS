App Description
Enjoy the easiest speech and text translator app on the App Store! Translating has never been so easy. Select languages > tap the button > get the translation - that’s it. Everything is done within one screen!

TRANSLATOR THAT ALLOWS YOU TO:
• Communicate in over 100 languages with voice-to-voice translations
• Read & write with easy-to-use text translations
• Learn words & phrases in over 100 different languages
• Communicate with ease, everywhere you go
• Make accurate speech & text translations in real time

FEATURES:

CAMERA TRANSLATOR
Use your camera to instantly translate signs, menus or any other printed text.

KEYBOARD FOR MESSAGING
Chat with friends via messenger or email using a keyboard with built-in translator.

TRAVEL HUB
Find helpful tools to plan your next trip: Listen to travel & language podcasts. Use the currency converter to access over 150 exchange rates.

APPLE WATCH APP
Instantly translate over 100 languages, straight from your wrist. Subscribe to Dialog Plus to unlock unlimited voice & text translations for Apple Watch.

OFFLINE MODE
In offline mode you can translate texts without Internet connection.

HOME SCREEN WIDGET
Quickly access Dialog in a single tap, right from your home screen.

SIMPLE & EASY
Tap the button and speak. Just stop speaking to hear back the translation.

ADVANCED SPEECH RECOGNITION
Works well in noisy environments such as streets, stations and other crowded places.

LANGUAGE DETECTION
The language is automatically translated as you speak between the selected languages.

SURPRISING VELOCITY
Translations delivered in real time.

EXCELLENT ACCURACY
Uses the best translation engine available today.

100+ LANGUAGES
Translates speech and text instantly between more than 100 languages.
Get the app and start one-tap translations right away!
Supported languages and dialects: Arabic (Saudi Arabia, United Arab Emirates), Afrikaans (South Africa), Amharic (Ethiopia), Armenian, Azerbaijani, Bengali (Bangladesh, India), Basque (Spain), Bulgarian, Catalan, Chinese (Cantonese, Mandarin, Taiwan), Croatian, Czech, Danish, Dutch (The Netherlands), English (Australian, Canada, India, UK, US), Finnish, Filipino (Philippines), French, French (Canada), Galician, German, Greek, Georgian, Gujarati (India), Hebrew, Hungarian, Icelandic, Indonesian, Italian, Japanese, Javanese (Indonesia), Korean, Khmer (Cambodia), Malay, Moldavian, Norwegian, Nepali (Nepal), Polish, Portuguese, Portuguese (Brazil), Lao (Laos), Latvian, Lithuanian, Romanian, Russian, Slovak, Slovenian, Spanish, Spanish (Mexican), Swahili (Kenya), Serbian, Swedish, Sinhala (Sri Lanka), Thai, Turkish, Ukranian, Urdu (Pakistan), Vietnamese, Zulu (South Africa).

Google Translate is utilized to power Dialog’s translations.
