App Description
Photo Translator is a simple, easy-to-use and powerful photo translation software with advanced image recognition technology, supporting text translation and photo translation in hundreds of languages. It is an essential artifact for office, business and travel.

The main function:

1. Take pictures to recognize text: With advanced picture recognition technology, take pictures of the text or select photos from the album to get high-quality translations. Quickly recognize text in pictures, greatly improving your typing efficiency.
2. Take photos to identify items: Take photos or select pictures from albums to get multiple languages ​​and translations of the items in the pictures. You can also share, read and copy.
3. Text Translation: No members are required. Enter the text you want to translate to get a high-quality translation.

We will offer in-app purchases for the premium version of Photo Translator, and depending on the plan you choose, you can also enjoy a free trial period. The cost will be deducted from your iTunes account, and the charge rates will vary by country.
* Your free trial subscription will automatically renew as a paid subscription, unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24 hours before the end of the free trial period.
* You can turn off auto-renewal through your iTunes account settings and cancel your free trial or subscription at any time. This must be done 24 hours before the end of the free trial or subscription period to avoid being charged. Cancellations will take effect after the last day of the current subscription period and you will be downgraded to a free service.
* The account will be charged for renewal within 24 hours before the end of the current period, and the renewal fee will be determined.
