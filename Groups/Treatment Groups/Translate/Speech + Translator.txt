App Description
"Translator - Translate Speech" allows to dictate and translate using your voice. It uses speech recognition technology and translates into 40 languages. Just use Translator and your voice to make conversation in any language.

► Time saving of "Translator - Translate Speech"
The benefit of Translator in saving time is tremendous. Compared to typing the savings in time of speech translation can be up to 80%. You also can translate while being in the car, you just need to speak.

► Free test period of "Translator - Translate Speech"
You can use "Translator - Translate Speech" right out of the box without any restrictions. If you want to use it for longer, please upgrade to the Pro Version. This allows unlimited usage of the app !

► Supported speech recognition and translation languages of "Translator - Translate Speech"
English, Español, Français, Português, Deutsch, Italiano, Nederlands, Dansk, Norsk bokmål, Svenska, Suomi, Hindi, Indonesia, Tiếng Việt, Türkçe, Català, Bahasa Melayu, Hrvatski, Magyar, Polski, Română, Slovenčina, čeština, 粤语, ไทย, 한국어, עברית (ישראל) , हिन्दी , 中文, русский, العربية (المملكة العربية السعودية) , 日本語, Ελληνικά, 中文, 上海话, українська,

► Important notes concerning subscriptions
All above "Translator - Translate Speech" subscriptions renew within 24 hours before the current period ends, and you will be charged through your iTunes account. Any unused portion of a free trial period, if offered, will be forfeited when you purchase a subscription. Manage or cancel your subscription from iTunes Account settings. See our Terms & Conditions (https://www.ibn-software.com/app-terms-conditions) and Privacy Policy (https://www.ibn-software.com/app-privacy-policy).
