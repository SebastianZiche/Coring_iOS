App Description
In life, we often encounter many moments that we do not understand, especially travel studies, menus of exotic restaurants, imported goods in supermarkets, foreign mails and foreign publications, etc. At this time, a simple and reliable translation app is essential, so Super Translator must be the good helper you are looking for.

Features:
-Supports many languages
-Real-time voice translation
-Advanced OCR translation
-Language detection in text-to-text mode
-Copy and paste translations with one click
-Friendly user interface

Request permission:
1.Microphone for voice input and output
2.Camera and photo Photo Gallery for OCR translation

Currently supported languages:
Afrikaans, Albanian, Arabic, Armenian, Azerbaijani, Basque, Belarusian, Bengali, Bosnian, Bulgarian, Catalan, Cebuano, Chichewa, Chinese, Croatian, Czech, Danish, Dutch, English, Esperanto, Estonian, Filipino, Finnish, French, Galician, Georgian, German, Greek, Gujarati, Haitian Creole, Hausa, Hebrew, Hindi, Hmong, Hungarian, Icelandic, Igbo, Indonesian, Irish, Italian, Japanese, Javanese, Kannada, Kazakh, Khmer, Korean, Lao, Latin, Latvian, Lithuanian, Macedonian, Malagasy, Malay, Malayalam, Maltese, Maori, Marathi, Mongolian, Myanmar (Burmese), Nepali, Norwegian, Persian, Polish, Portuguese, Punjabi, Romanian, Russian, Serbian, Sesotho, Sinhala, Slovak, Slovenian, Somali, Spanish, Sundanese, Swahili, Swedish, Tajik, Tamil, Telugu, Thai, Turkish, Ukrainian, Urdu, Uzbek, Vietnamese, Welsh, Yiddish, Yoruba, Zulu...

Super Translator is your best choice for translating any type of text, voice and photo. Download now and enjoy the powerful features that take you away from worrying about translations.
