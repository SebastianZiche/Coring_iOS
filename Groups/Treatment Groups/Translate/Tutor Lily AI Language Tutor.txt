App Promotional Text
Become fluent in a second language by practicing real-life conversations with a friendly AI companion, who corrects and explains all your mistakes!
App Description
Struggling to learn a new language? Become fluent now by having engaging real-life conversations with a friendly AI companion, powered by ChatGPT! With Tutor Lily, you can practice speaking a new language at your own pace, wherever and whenever you want.




Tutor Lily currently supports all the following languages: English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Japanese, Korean, Chinese, Arabic, Dutch, Russian, Turkish, Ukrainian, Greek, Polish, Swedish, Hindi, and more coming soon!


Key Features include:

• Personalized AI Conversations: Tutor Lily adapts to your learning needs, engaging you in meaningful interactive conversations to help you practice your way to fluency.

• Instant Corrections & Explanations: Tutor Lily automatically highlights and corrects your mistakes. Click on "Explain Mistakes" to receive detailed explanations for every single mistake.

• In-app Translations: Stuck on a word or phrase? Use the "Translate" feature to translate Lily's responses in your native language, making it easier to grasp new concepts and vocabulary. You can also say some words in your native language and let Lily translate them for you.

• Voice Recognition & Pronunciation: Use our Speech-to-text feature to speak to Tutor Lily with your own voice, and ensure that your pronunciation is on point.

• Listening Comprehension: Click on "Read Aloud" to listen to Tutor Lily's responses and perfect your listening skills.


So why choose Tutor Lily?...

• Focus on Fluency: Our app is designed to help you become fluent in your target language through engaging, interactive, and immersive conversations with Tutor Lily. Experience language learning like never before!

• Fast & Efficient Learning: Tutor Lily's real-time feedback, explanations, and personalized approach allow you to learn more effectively, saving time and accelerating your progress.

• Expert AI Tutor: Leveraging state-of-the-art ChatGPT and GPT-4 algorithms, Tutor Lily provides a language learning experience that rivals traditional tutoring, making it easier and more accessible for everyone.

• Privacy & Security: We value your privacy and ensure that all user data is protected and secure.

• Continuous Improvement: We are dedicated to enhancing Tutor Lily with new features, languages, and improvements based on user feedback and Generative AI innovations.


Download Tutor Lily now and embark on your language learning journey with your friendly AI companion. Experience a new way to learn languages, gain confidence in your speaking and writing abilities, and unlock your potential!

Your feedback is important to us! If you have any suggestions, questions, or issues, please contact us at support@tutorlily.com. We are always here to help and continuously improve Tutor Lily for a better language learning experience.

Don't wait any longer. Join the Tutor Lily community and start mastering a new language today!
