App Description
Start translating in a matter of seconds!

Listen to your documents with our PDF to Voice feature - over 500 voices available!

With an ease-of-use navigation, you can translate your ebooks, contracts, manuals or just about any documents.

You can choose from over 100+ languages. PDF Translator Pro will auto detect the language of your documents.

PDF Translator Pro allows you to translate unlimited number of pages in a single document.

Your translated files are accessible through the Translation History feature which allows you to access your documents whenever you need them.

Once you get your results, you can share them on a device or platform of your choice.

Not sure about the file you are translating? PDF Translator Pro offers Quick Preview on all steps of the translation, so you never loose track of your content.

Features:

- Text to Speech
- QR Code Support
- Over 100+ Languages
- Scanner feature
- Merge multiple files
- Protect your documents
- Turn any web page into a PDF document
- No page limits
- Translation History
- Easy File Sharing
- Quick Preview
