App Description
The perfect translation and dictionary application for camera and voice translation in more than 100 languages.

Turn your phone into a real-time translation device. Choose two languages and communicate easily anywhere in the world. Language is no longer an obstacle!

Main feature:
• Translation in more than 100 languages.
• Conversation translator, voice to voice dialogue.
• Camera translator for menu or logo.
• Website translator, translate website in Safari.
• AR Translator, translate in Augmented Reality.
• Keyboard Translator for Messaging.
• Voice Translator for Apple Watch.
• Share Translator for copying and share.
• App Shortcuts, use Siri and Spotlight to run translator.
• Family Sharing, share subscriptions with your family.




Information about auto-renewal:

• Subscription will automatically renew unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.
• Subscriptions can be managed by the user, and automatic renewal can be turned off by going to the user's "Account Settings" after purchase.
• When a user purchases a subscription, any unused portion during the free trial period will be confiscated.
• Payment will be deducted from the iTunes account when confirming the purchase.
• Account will be charged for renewal within 24 hours before the end of the current period. The cost depends on the plan selected.
