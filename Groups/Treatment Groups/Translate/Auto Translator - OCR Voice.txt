App Description
Auto Translator iOS is a translation app for your iPhone, you can translate text, voice and pictures anytime, anywhere.
About Auto Translator:
1. Faster translation speed
2. Simple and easy-to-use operation interface
3. Support online and offline translation.
4. Support more than 100 languages.
Text Translation: You can get the translated text immediately by inputting it through the mobile phone keyboard, or you can choose the microphone output.
Voice Translation: Don't want to type? Real-time voice translation can also be achieved against the phone's microphone.
OCR Translation: Traveling abroad and don't know what the signs and restaurant menus are saying? The text in the translated image can be automatically recognized by taking a photo.

Auto Translator supports the following languages:
English, French , Spanish, Arabic, Chinese (Simplified & Traditional), Portuguese, Swahili , Urdu, Catalan, Czech, Croatian, Danish, Dutch, Esperanto, Finnish, German, Greek, Gujarati, Hebrew, Hindi, Indonesian, Vietnamese, Khmer, Macedonian, Malay, Malayalam, Marathi, Mongolian, Filipino, Bengali, Farsi, Hungarian, Bulgarian, Icelandic, Italian, Japanese, Javanese, Kannada, Korean, Lao, Latin, Latvian, Burmese, Nepali, Norwegian, Polish, Romanian, Russian, Serbian, Sinhala, Somali, Slovak, Sundanese, Swedish, Tamil, Telugu, Thai, Turkish, Ukrainian, Uzbek, Welsh, Zulu, Afrikaans, Amharic, Armenian, Albanian, Arabic (UAE), Azerbaijani, Cebuano, Estonian, Galician, Georgian, Haitian Creole, Hausa, Hmong, Igbo, Irish, Kazakh, Kinyarwanda, Kurdish, Lithuanian, Luxembourgish, Malagasy, Maltese, Odia, Pashto, Punjabi, Sindhi, Slovenian, Sesotho, Uyghur, Yiddish and Yoruba.
