App Description
Speak and translate is free online all languages voice to voice translator and interpreter designed for the people who want to translate one language to another language. The beauty of this voice translator app is only you have to open the app, select your desired languages and target language, just speak and in return the app will translate the given sentence into your targeted language.

The app is not only working with voice, but it will also work when you need to write any text and translate it from one language to another if needed.

This speak to translator and interpreter app is not only work as voice to voice translator but you can use this as a all languages voice dictionary. Whenever you need to learn or need to find out meaning of any word, just open our translator app and input the word you want to learn the language.

This all languages translator app do have a lot of features in the app listed as below.
• All languages translator and interpreter – Main feature of the app is voice to voice all language translator free.
• All languages Voice dictionary – The app will also work as all languages voice dictionary app , used for education or any purpose.
• User can speak to translator from both side ( source and target), and the app will translate to another selected language.
• This online speech to translator app have accurate voice recognition for all available languages.
• The app will save all conversation history, user can use it whenever he /she need it
• User can share the translated sentence / conversation to any chat application
• The app contain over 100 languages
• All languages do have text to voice, voice to voice and voice to text translation
• The app will work on voice to text and will translate from source to target language
• Every conversation do have the functionality to copy, speak, share or delete it
