App Description
The English to any other language translator app is a best translation app for travelers around the world.

Look up default English to other language sentences and phrases or manually type your own English to other language words and sentences with a few clicks.


It allows instantaneous language translation, so it's easy to see how the changes in other language as you type in English.
More than 2000 phrases are available in each of the languare for common conversation situations and are divided into categories like Tickets, Hotel, Dining, Hospital, Transportation, Sightseeing, Conversation, Airport, and etc. for easy use.

More features:
• Includes sentences and phrases for common Conversation situations using English to other language.
• Bookmark your Favorite phrases to plan easy English other language conversations
• Audio plays as you type words to and read out the text of the translation result
• Offline other language translation
