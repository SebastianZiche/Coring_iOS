App Description
Hello Translator is a simple and efficient translation software. When you are at work, traveling abroad, meeting with foreign friends, solve your language barriers overseas business travel, and support easily translate over 60+ kinds of languages.
 
Core Function:
1. Text translation: input text, you can get high quality translation;
2. Camera translation: translate text in images instantly, support camera taken and album selection. Map. Menus, books, street signs and etc, take photos and get translation results in time;
3. Real-time speech translation: real time two-way speech translation, making communication easier and more convenient;
4. Simultaneous interpretation: simple and fast, while speaking and translating;
