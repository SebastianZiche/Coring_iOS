App Description
Language Translation is a free translation app for your iPhone that helps you overcome language barriers and easily translate over 100 languages.
Features:
1. Multiple translation modes: text, voice and OCR translation.
2. Instant translation: supports offline and online translation, fast and reliable.
3. Completely free: fast and reliable.
Text translation: As long as you enter the text you want to translate, Language Translation can quickly detect and output the language you want.
Voice translation: Use the microphone to input your voice and select the language you want to translate.
OCR translation: Our advanced scanning technology can help you translate the text in the picture at any time, take a photo or select a picture, it can present you with the perfect translation result.

LANGUAGES SUPPORTED:
