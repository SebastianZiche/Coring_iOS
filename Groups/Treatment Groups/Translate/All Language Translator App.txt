App Description
All Language Translator App is a Leading/Professional/Unique Translator App to translate between 100+ languages using features like text translator and voice translator.

All Language Translator App is recommended for you that is helpful when you are out of the country or in a place where you do not know the local language or in foreign.

All Language Translator App is a language translation expert that allows you to translate from English to any other language.

All Language Translator App allows you to speak and translate bilingual conversations in multiple languages with best UI and simple steps.

ALL Language Translator App supports translation between the following languages:
Albanian , Afrikaans, Amharic, Arabic, Armenian,Azerbaijani, Belarusian, Basque (Euskara), Bengali , Bosnian, Bulgarian , Georgian , German , Greek , Gujarati, Haitian creole , Hausa, Hawaiian, Hebrew , Hindi, Hmong, Hungarian, Icelandic , Indonesian, Igbo, Irish , Italian, Japanese , Javanese, Kannada, Kazakh, Khmer,Kurdish, Korean , Kyrgyz , Lao, Latin, Latvian , Lithuanian , Luxembourgish, Macedonian, Malagasy, Malay, Malayalam , Maltese , Maori , Marathi, Mongolian, Myanmar, Nepali , Norwegian , Pashto, Persian , Polish , Portuguese, Punjabi, Romanian Russian, Samoan,Scots gaelic , Serbian , Sesotho, Shona , Sindhi , Urdu , Uzbek , Vietnamese , Welsh, Xhosa, Yiddish , Yoruba, Zulu.

All Language Translator App best features:-
1) Beautiful and easy to use interface
2) Easily copy and paste sentences
3) Easily clear the text.
4) Instantly translate words and sentences
5) Universal Translator to translation between 100+ languages
6) Translate spoken text
7) Free Online Language Translation
8) Share your translation with friends and family
