App Description
Going on a journey or a business trip, but have not enough time to learn the local language? Use Translate+ - the best speech translator with an advanced speech recognition and on-the-fly translation technology! Your indispensable helpers in any journey will help to communicate freely in more than 75 languages ​​all over the world!

A unique function is available in the application - automatic determination of the end of a sentence, with which you can quickly and easily enter the full text without having to interrupt and write each sentence separately!

Destroy language barriers and freely communicate with locals in their language without restriction!

Use the free 3-day trial to evaluate all the benefits by yourself!
Benefits of Translate+ PRO:
- unlimited translates;
- no ads;
- sound translating;
- detecting end of the speech

Terms of subscription:

* After purchase of subscription, you can control its settings and/or turn off it in settings of your iTunes account
* You can't cancel your current(active) subscription during its working period, however you can unsubscribe from future periods
