App Description
The default long press to copy text on a mobile screen or image does not always work. This app helps you extract text/words from a mobile screen by sharing your screenshot with the app or by selecting text from an image.

With its advanced OCR technology, this app allows you to extract text and words from your mobile screen and images. The OCR recognition accuracy is between 99% and 100%, ensuring that you get the exact text you need.


Main Features:

• Copy any text on the mobile screen to your clipboard by sharing screenshot with this app.
• Extract text from any image.
• Extract text easily from any application including social media apps like Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, Youtube and more by just share screenshot.
• Extract text on image and read it aloud with text to speech.
• Recent scans history.
• Supports 92 languages for text recognition and translations in over 100 languages.

How to use this app to extract Text from screen:

Step 1: Take a screenshot.
Step 2: Open that screenshot and share with this app.
Step 3: Select the text by touch and drag on screeenshot and choose the language for OCR recognition.
Step 4: Please wait while the app performs OCR (Optical Character Recognition) operation to extract the text.
Step 5: Now you may copy to clipboard or share the extracted text.

Never struggle to copy text from your mobile screen and Image again. Get Copy Text app now and enjoy the ease of extracting text and words from your mobile screen and Images!

You can take screenshots by pressing and holding the 'POWER BUTTON' and 'VOLUME UP BUTTON' at the same time for a second.
