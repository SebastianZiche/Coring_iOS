App Description
Use your camera to scan & translate texts or documents. Detect objects and recognize locations with one touch. Take a photo and translate anything!



Scanning texts and documents have never been easier. Just take a photo and our powerful AI service detects text automatically. The camera can scan large documents in various languages and translate them into other languages.



Air Translate is a godsend for business, travel, and learning. Translate road signs, images, titles, documents, books, instructions, notes, ads, and much more besides. Recognize physical objects, locations, places, animals, food, and more. Hear, read, edit, translate, and share your text in seconds!



Highlights:

• Scan text or documents and translate from and into more than 50 languages, including English, Spanish, Chinese, Arabic, Portuguese, German, Japanese, French, and Russian

• Listen to translated text

• Detect objects and translate their names

• Recognize and translate locations

• Real-time translations

• Edit & Share text on Instagram, Facebook, Twitter, etc. or send by email

• All translations are saved in history and can be found at any time



Supports Languages:

Afrikaans, Albanian, Arabic, Bangla, Belarusian, Bulgarian, Catalan, Chinese, Croatian, Czech, Danish, Dutch, English, Esperanto, Estonian, Filipino, Finnish, French, Galician, Georgian, German, Greek, Gujarati, Haitian Creole, Hebrew, Hindi, Hungarian, Icelandic, Indonesian, Irish, Italian, Japanese, Kannada, Korean, Latvian, Lithuanian, Macedonian, Malay, Maltese, Marathi, Norwegian, Persian, Polish, Portuguese, Romanian, Russian, Serbian, Slovak, Slovenian, Spanish, Swahili, Swedish, Tagalog, Tamil, Telugu, Thai, Turkish, Ukrainian, Urdu, Vietnamese, Welsh, Zulu
