App Description
Voice Translator is a great translator for voice translation, text translation and photo translation. No matter where you are in the world, you can communicate and travel efficiently and smoothly.

It has great translation translation capabilities:
* Voice-to-speech translation - Real-time speech recognition automatically translates into text and voice corresponding to the voice, and can translate the voice of the other party into your voice
* Text to text translation: it can translate the text you input into the corresponding text, and automatically play the translated voice
* Photo translation: translate the street signs, signs, slogans or documents you see into the language you have.
* Supports voice-to-speech translation in up to 12 mainstream languages.
Voice settings: Select male or female voice.

* Get the premium version to access all advanced features *
(1) Unlimited voice translation
(2) Unlimited photo translation
(3) Text translation to voice
(4) No ads
