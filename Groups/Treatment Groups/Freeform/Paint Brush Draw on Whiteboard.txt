App Description
About:
» You can draw everything what you want on blank paper use the painter brush tools and make it to one picture, save it and share with it.
» You can edit your photos, apply effects, add stickers, remove blemishes, whiten smiles and so much more!
» It Includes: Pen,Line,Arrow,Rect(Stroke),Rect(Fill),Ellipse(Stroke),Ellipse(Fill),Eraser,Text,Text (Multiline),Effects,Focus,Frames,Stickers,Enhance,Orientation,Crop,Lighting,Color,Splash,Text,Redeye,Whiten,Blemish… Many many colors, tools, filters and effects and they are all FREE!.

Features:
» Enhance with Hi-Def, Scenery, Food, Portrait, and Night
» Stylize with over 100 free and purchasable filters
» Get creative with stunning stickers, frames and overlays
» Touch-up selfies with redeye and blemish removal
» Boost smile wattage with teeth whitening
» Selectively remove and replace color with Splash
» Add drama with Sharpness
» Add text and create your own memes
» Crop, rotate, and straighten your photos
» Draw attention to the subject of your photo with Focus or Vignette
» Modify brightness, contrast, highlights and shadows
» Adjust saturation, warmth, tint and fade
» Share photos to TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, X
» Print your favorite photos to the nearest Walgreens
