App Description
My Sketchbook is a simple and intelligible application that teaches you how to draw step by step quickly and effortlessly. Intuitive tools help you draw even the most challenging drawings easily!
After downloading the application, you will find a wide variety of templates and drawing ideas, divided by the level of difficulty. Choose your favorite sketch and follow the tips step by step until the drawing is ready, then paint it with the brightest colors from our palette!
Main Features:
Undo/redo function
Pencil and eraser functions
Opacity scale and brush size selection scale
Color palette
Drawing tips
Brush and marker functions
Your personal drawing teacher is now always with you! Draw anywhere and share your works with your friends! Surprise everyone with your new skills!
Don't forget to leave us your feedback and ratings. We hope you enjoy our app and leave a positive review! Please don't hesitate to contact us if you find any bugs or want to suggest any improvements!
