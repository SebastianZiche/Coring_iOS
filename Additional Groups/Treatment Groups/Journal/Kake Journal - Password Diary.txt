Version 3.9 (Jul 6, 2023)
    Compatibility improvement
    --------
    Recent updates:
    - [Image]: Added the 'Image' option. Pro version allows up to 9 images, Free version allows 1 image. After adding an image, you can preview, save, or share it. Long-press on the preview screen to add a frame.
    - [Location]: Added the 'Location' option. Click to include various location information and coordinates.
    - [Inspiration]: Introduced the 'Inspiration' option. Now you can select inspirational sentences and diary themes in Chinese, English, or Japanese (randomly generated) to ignite your creativity.
    - [Export]: Included additional export formats. Pro version supports PDF, Encrypted PDF, PNG Image, TXT, and CSV, while the Free version supports TXT, CSV, and DOCX.
    - [Font Size]: When adjusting the font size, the specific size will be displayed in real-time on the right side of the title.
    - [Custom Toolbar]: Removed the previous simplified mode. In the appearance settings, you can now customize the toolbar by sorting or deleting unnecessary icons. Enjoy full customization.
    - [Main App Icon]: Replaced the main app icon; however, you can still use the original one if desired.
    - Resolved occasional issue of duplicate diary generation.
    - Enhanced performance, stability, and localization.
