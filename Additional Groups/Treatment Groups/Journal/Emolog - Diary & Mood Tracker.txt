Capture your feelings with delightful animated Emojis!

Emolog is like a treasure chest, keeping your daily emotions safe. Whether it's sadness, joy, love, excitement, negativity, or positivity, let's gather each precious feeling.
I'll help you understand and love yourself more! Don't lose sight of your invaluable self over a week, a month, or even a year.

Using Emolog is super easy!
Just pick today's date and record how you're feeling. Simple, right?
In just a few seconds a day, you can keep track of your important emotions!
Emolog will be there for you, through your ups and downs!
