App Description
Sweetalk is an AI translation communication application that helps you connect business partners and friends around the world for easy and barrier-free communication. Sweetalk can translate both online and offline, so that the world understands your voice.

Why use Sweetalk:

• Conference translation: Support real-time translation between 6 languages.
• Conversation translation: Automatically recognize the spoken language, translate in real time, and make offline chat easier.
• Voice and video translation: Provides high-quality calls around the world and supports real-time translation.
• Face-to-face translation: Compatible with all wireless Bluetooth headsets, real-time translation, and offline chats are more private.
• Text translation: real-time translation of text messages and dynamic text.
• Voice translation: Voice is translated into text in real time, making online chat easier.
• Translation languages: 80 languages ​​are supported, with more than 6400 translation combinations.
• Translation effect: accurate and fast translation.
• Multimedia Messaging: Supports sending video, picture, text and voice messages.
• Chat color version: change the mood color with your friends at any time.
• Moments: Share the wonderful moments of your life with friends around the world.
• Privacy protection: Strictly protect users' privacy and security, one-click burning function, and completely clear data.
