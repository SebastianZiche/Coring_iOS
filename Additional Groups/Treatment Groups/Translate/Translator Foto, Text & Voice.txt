App Description
Translator Go is very useful during work, business trip and travel.

It supports as many as 100 global languages, such as English, Japanese, Chinese, French, Korean, Spanish....

Using Translator Go, you can easily scan & translate:
• Official Paper Documents
• User Manuals
• Articles from Books & Magazines
• Menus in Restaurants
• Instructions
• Texts on Product Labels
• Road Signs
• and more...

Main Features of Translator Foto:
1) Support 100+ languages
2) Voice Translation
* Take photos or import images from camera roll
* Choose, adjust and crop texts from photos or images
* Convert image format into text format
* Translate text instantly
3) Text Translation: copy text or input manually
4) Photo Translation
5) Edit, copy, share text or translation
6) Check history & favorite
