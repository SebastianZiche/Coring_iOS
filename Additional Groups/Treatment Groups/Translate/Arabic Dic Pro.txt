App Description
Ultimate Arabic-English Talking Dictionary & Translator

Super Fast and Powerful

Arabic - English
English - Arabic


- Including over HALF MILLION (330,000 ) words.

- Listen and Learn to speak in English / Arabic with words pronunciations

- Arabic - English , English- Arabic instant Text TRANSLATION

- Integrated with two Speech Engines ( Offline & Online )

- Synonyms and Antonyms

- Phonetics

- Easy to use

- Over 20 specific categories (Law,Financial,Engineering,...)

- Intelligent relative words navigation

- Designed for iPhone Xs, X, 8, 7, 6, SE

- Designed for iPad, iPod

- Optimized for Retina Display

- Landscape & Portrait view mode support

- Intelligent translation without need to set input language

- Presents "Word of A Day" feature to help you memorize new words
