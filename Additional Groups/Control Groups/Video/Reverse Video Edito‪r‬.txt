App Description
Create funny backwards videos. Download now for free!

You jumped in a pool and want to make it look like you are magically coming out of it? Use our Reverse Video Editor app to play videos backwards and create extremely funny videos.

You can either use an existing video from your library or record a new one. The app will create a reverse video from the original and save it as a new one.

Both video and sound will be played backwards.

Create stunning videos, post them on social media like Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, Snapchat, YouTube or send them to friends and family on Whatsapp and iMessage.

This app is also ideal and a great tool for creating funny reels and TikToks.

Ideas for funny backwards videos:
- Jump in a pool and make it look like you are magically coming out of it
- Drink a glass of water and make it look like you are filling it with your mouth
- Throw a ball away and make it look like it's magically flying from the ground into your hand

There are plenty of activities that look extremely funny when you play them backwards. Just try it, be creative and impress others with your funny rewind videos!
